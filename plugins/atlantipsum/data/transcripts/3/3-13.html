Yeah.
KOLYA
If we were to bury you alive, it would protect you from the weight of the soil, but you would still suffocate. Fascinating way to die, don't you think?
LUCIUS
Gotta tell ya, it's not that fascinating to me.
KOLYA
(chuckling)
It is to me.

[He stands and nods to the guards.]

LUCIUS
Wait, wait, you can't kill me. Look, I'm the perfect bait. If Sheppard finds out that I'm in trouble, he's gonna come rescue me. We're really close.

[Kolya stops and turns back, his expression clearly skeptical.]

KOLYA
You and Sheppard?
LUCIUS
Yeah.
KOLYA
I don't think so.

[He continues to walk away.]

LUCIUS
Then Weir…or-or Rodney or Carson. We're like—we're like the best of friends.

[Kolya comes closer.]

KOLYA
And how is it you know them so well?

[Lucius stands.]

LUCIUS
I spent some time in Atlantis. It's the nicest place I've ever been. Best time I ever had. It-it's like I remember—like I was there this morning!
KOLYA
(intrigued)
Tell me more.

INT—VILLAGE TAVERN

[Lucius and the Genii are sitting around, drinking alcoholic beverages.]

LUCIUS
So I finish off the last Wraith guard, and I turn to the queen and I say-I say: "If you were twenty pounds heavier, you'd look like my ex-wife."

[Lucius and the guards laugh drunkenly.]

LUCIUS
Of course, my ex-wife was a knockout, but you know, where's the funny in that?

[This time he's the only one laughing.]

AMARIS
You know that personal shield's amazing. Mind if I try it?
LUCIUS
Yeah, go ahead, go ahead.

[He pats the shield on his breast.]

AMARIS
Give it right back.

[Amaris reaches over but is repelled by the shield. The guards and Lucius laugh.]

LUCIUS
Gotcha, uh? Oh, you think I'd let you have this? You'd kill me right on the spot.
AMARIS
(nodding, grinning)
I'd slit your throat from ear to ear.

[They laugh again, Lucius's laugh is more nervous.]

LUCIUS
Good times…good times.
KOLYA
As much as I love hearing about your former wives, Lucius, I'd rather hear more about what you know of Atlantis.
LUCIUS
I told you everything already.
KOLYA
In that case, it's getting late and I could use some rest before the funeral.
LUCIUS
Funeral?

[Kolya gets up and leaves. The guards follow.]

LUCIUS
Oh no. Oh no-no. Don't bury me alive. Please, I don't deserve that. You know what I-I-Nobody deserves that. I deserve banishment. That's what I des…

[The last Genii walks out the door, shutting it behind them. As soon as they're gone, Sheppard appears from a back entrance.]

SHEPPARD
Lucius?
LUCIUS
What are you doing here?
SHEPPARD
Kolya's got all the exits out of town covered, and rumor has it they were going to bury you alive.
LUCIUS
I can't believe it. You came to save me?
SHEPPARD
Yeah, well neither can I. Let's go.
LUCIUS
Yeah, I can't-I can't. I'm tied to the chair.

[Sheppard sighs and crouches under the table to undo the bindings. He spots a dynamite-like bomb attached to the table.]

SHEPPARD
Whoa no. Good luck with that shield.

[He pats Lucius on the shoulder and runs out.]

LUCIUS
What? Come on. Untie me!

EXT—VILLAGE OUTSIDE TAVERN

[Sheppard barely escapes the room before the explosion and is knocked down by the blast and debris.]

EXT—VILLAGE ALLEYWAY, DAY

[Ronon moves swiftly down the street, his weapon primed as he looks for intruders. Genii start to surround him, and he keeps his weapon pointed at them.]

MCKAY
Ow!

[Behind him, Ronon notices McKay has already been taken prisoner by the Genii. They threaten to hurt him if Ronon does not surrender. He relinquishes his weapon.]

EXT—ANOTHER VILLAGE ALLEYWAY, DAY

[Teyla also moves down the street, standing off against some Genii. She spins seeing some ways blocked by guards and Beckett being led by others. She surrenders.]

EXT—VILLAGE SQUARE, DAY

[From the alley, Sheppard sees the rest of his team led into the square as prisoners. He runs away. A disheveled Lucius stumbles out of the tavern. Kolya is waiting in the square.]

LUCIUS
What are you crazy? You coulda killed me!
KOLYA
That shield of yours impresses me more and more. You did your part Lucius. You get to live another day.
KOLYA
(to Amaris)
Sheppard?
AMARIS
He got away.
KOLYA
That's unfortunate.

[He raises his pistol and fires at Amaris, but nothing happens. Amaris breathes a sigh of relief.]

AMARIS
Thanks for sparing me, sir.
KOLYA
Don't disappoint me again.
(to other Genii)
Secure the prisoners. Perhaps they'll be more effective bait.

[As they all move off, Kolya hands his sidearm to an aide that remains.]

KOLYA
Get this fixed.

EXT—VILLAGE, DAY

[Sheppard evades guards as they patrol the area.]

EXT—VILLAGE JAIL, DAY

[The rest of the Atlantis team is held prisoner in a large cell, its open bars along one side facing onto the street.]

TEYLA
Do you think he got away?
BECKETT
If they captured the colonel, wouldn't he be here with us?
MCKAY
He'll be back in a jumper in no time.

[The villager who first greeted the team arrives with bowls of soup.]

MCKAY
Oh!

[He quickly grabs the food and starts eating. The others take bowls.]

VILLAGER
Your friend escaped the village. They are still looking for him.
MCKAY
(between mouthfuls)
Eh, if they haven't found him yet, chances are he managed to slip the perimeter.
VILLAGER
(pleased)
Yes. He and Lucius are no doubt plotting our rescue as we speak.
MCKAY
Lucius?
VILLAGER
He would not abandon us.
MCKAY
Oh sure he wouldn't. Just like he wouldn't sell us out while we were trying to rescue him.
VILLAGER
He would never do that!
MCKAY
Okay, you know what? Just stop it! All right? Lucius is not a superhero! Batman, that's a superhero.
VILLAGER
I don't understand.
TEYLA
Your people cannot place their future in Lucius's hands. The time has come for you to save yourselves.
VILLAGER
We have no hope of defeating these soldiers.
RONON
You will with our help.
VILLAGER
Then why did you not do so before?
TEYLA
We were concerned for the safety of the village.
RONON
Now we don't care.
MCKAY
Okay, just pretend he didn't say that, and then just let us out of here.
VILLAGER
(after thinking it over)
No. It's too dangerous. Best to wait for Lucius to return, then he'll save us all.

[He walks away, smiling.]

BECKETT
No, wait! Lucius isn't a hero!

EXT—OUTSKIRTS OF VILLAGE, DAY

[Sheppard evades another patrol. As he begins to move out, he hears and sees motion behind him. He raises his weapon. Lucius breaks out of the foliage.]

LUCIUS
Don't, don't! Don't! I swear I didn't know about the bomb. I don't know what those guys were doing under there.
SHEPPARD
How the hell did you get out here?

[He turns and starts walking again, weapon ready for Genii. Lucius follows.]

LUCIUS
Everybody's looking for you. I just walked out unnoticed. Isn't that something? So what are we going to do?
SHEPPARD
What are we going to do? Well, I'll tell you what I'm gonna do. I'm gonna get by Kolya's men so I can get to the 'Gate and call for reinforcements.
LUCIUS
Reinforcements? Whaddya say we just get off this planet?
SHEPPARD
My team is back there.
LUCIUS
Yeah, so is a guy who wants to bury me alive!
SHEPPARD
Oh, you're done playing hero now, are you? Fine. You can leave as soon as we get to the 'Gate. Just keep up.
LUCIUS
You're angry now, aren't you?
SHEPPARD
Shut up!
LUCIUS
See, now, that's anger!

EXT—VILLAGE JAIL, DAY

[Ronon tugs and kicks against the bars to no effect. Beckett sighs, then turns to see McKay digging at the back wall of the cell with his soup spoon.]

BECKETT
Rodney, you're not trying to dig your way outta here, are you?
MCKAY
See if you can find another spoon. With someone helping, this will go twice as fast.
TEYLA
Someone's coming.

[McKay hides his spoon. Kolya walks over to their cell.]

KOLYA
I have to give Colonel Sheppard credit. No opponent's ever caused me this much trouble. Unfortunately, I'm going to have to kill him.
MCKAY
That's funny, he says the same thing about you.
KOLYA
Really?
(he laughs)
I'm flattered.
RONON
But you can't kill him if you can't catch him.
KOLYA
(sobering)
I will. Eventually.
MCKAY
And what about us, huh? How do we fit into your, uh…evil plans for galactic domination?
KOLYA
You'll be allowed to stay alive for the time being…unless you were stupid enough to eat the meal you were served.

[McKay drops his hidden spoon and grabs at his throat, beginning to choke in panic. Beckett reaches out to help him. Kolya laughs.]

KOLYA
I'm just kidding.

[McKay stops clutching his throat, relieved.]

MCKAY
Oh thank god.
KOLYA
In fact, I intend to send you back to Atlantis in exchange for a few things.
BECKETT
There's no way Doctor Weir would cut a deal with you, even if our lives are in danger. You know that.
KOLYA
In the past, I may have been a little too ambitious with my demands. This time, however, I have no intention of backing Doctor Weir into a corner. After all, what's a small number of your P-90s, a few grenades, and some ammunition in exchange for your lives? And who knows? If we're successful in toppling Ladon, and I assume rightful leadership of the Genii, we could end up allies.
RONON
(snarling)
That's not going to happen, Kolya.
BECKETT
While we're standing here having this little chat, there's no doubt Colonel Sheppard has reached the Stargate and contacted Atlantis. In just a matter of minutes, this village will be swarming with our people.
KOLYA
And I can guarantee you, that's not going to happen either.

EXT—GARDENS NEAR THE STARGATE

[Sheppard and Lucius move through the foliage. They get to the Stargate to see it heavily guarded by Genii]

LUCIUS
I don't believe this. Isn't it the worst day you could imagine?
KOLYA
(into Atlantis radio)
Colonel Sheppard? I'm going to assume you can hear me.

[Sheppard jerks at hearing Kolya's voice in his earpiece. He raises a finger to his lips to silence Lucius and listen to the broadcast.]

KOLYA
(over radio)
I'm also going to assume that…

EXT—VILLAGE JAIL CELL

KOLYA
(continuing into radio)
…if you haven't reached the 'Gate by now, you soon will. So I want to save you some trouble. My men have secured the 'gate. There's no way you're getting through. Come back and face me, Colonel, or I'll start killing your friends.

EXT—GARDENS NEAR THE STARGATE

[Sheppard looks worried.]

EXT—VILLAGE JAIL, DAY

[Kolya unholsters his sidearm and waves it at the imprisoned team.]

KOLYA
(into radio)
Which one of them shall we kill first? I'm leaning toward Doctor McKay, myself. Colonel?
SHEPPARD
(via radio)
Kolya, this is Sheppard. I'm coming back.
KOLYA
(into radio, smiling)
Don't keep me waiting.

[He puts down the radio.]

MCKAY
That's what this is all about? Killing Sheppard?
KOLYA
Absolutely, Doctor McKay! But don't feel left out. If I don't get those weapons from Doctor Weir, I fully intend to kill you as well.

[Kolya walks away.]

EXT—AREA OUTSIDE VILLAGE, DAY

[Lucius and Sheppard walk back to the village.]

LUCIUS
I'm coming with you.
SHEPPARD
You're sure?
LUCIUS
No…um…yeah…I am. I am. I'm gonna come…I'm coming with you. Just…
SHEPPARD
Yes or no?
LUCIUS
Don't pressure me like this! I'm gonna come with you—
SHEPPARD
Make up your mind!
LUCIUS
I'm gonna help you out! Okay? Oh boy, they better give me a parade for this one.

INT—VILLAGE, DAY

INT—VILLAGE JAIL, DAY

[Several guards come, including Amaris who opens the cell door.]

MCKAY
(nervously)
What's going on?
AMARIS
Out! Let's go!

[The team is herded into the square.]

EXT—NEAR VILLAGE, DAY

[Lucius and Sheppard walk the path to the village.]

LUCIUS
I'm just spitballing here. What if we hugged each other…and the device would-would, uh, you know, help us, uh…
SHEPPARD
I don't like that idea.
LUCIUS
No, I know…
SHEPPARD
Bad idea.
LUCIUS
I don't think that…
SHEPPARD
And it's not possible.
LUCIUS
Yeah, It doesn't work that way. Okay, okay. Here's one. What if I swung from a clock tower on a long rope, right into town.
SHEPPARD
And…?
LUCIUS
I don't know. That's all I have. I could set myself on fire?
SHEPPARD
I like that.
LUCIUS
(rueful grin)
Okay…um…I could run naked…oh, I know, I know! I have one! What if I got on your shoulders, and we have a cloak-like thing…some religious thing they could be afraid of giants?

LATER

LUCIUS
Okay…what if we build a tunnel? Okay, we could tunnel…? Or…a…boulder! Huge boulder. I'm camouflaged. I'm protected. Now the boulder protects you. Or, um…a magnifying glass…and some tape!
SHEPPARD
I have an idea, Lucius.
LUCIUS
Some poisoned tape.

INT—VILLAGE ROOM

[The villagers are all gathered in this room, watching the Genii through the window. Lucius sneaks in the back door.]

VILLAGER
Lucius!
BARMAID
I knew you'd return to save us.
LUCIUS
I've returned. But…you people, you're gonna have to save yourselves.

[The villagers mutter "what" and murmur in consternation.]

VILLAGER
You do not wish to help us?
LUCIUS
No-no-no. Of course I wanna help. It's just…look, what if I wasn't here? What would you do then?
VILLAGER
Wait for you to come back?
LUCIUS
All right, there-there's the problem. What if I didn't know you were in trouble? Or…I couldn't get back in time? What would you do then? I mean, look, you're a great group of people. It's a lovely town. But, there's a lot of horrible people out there, so you gotta fend for yourselves. Starting today.
BARMAID
I-I understand what Lucius is saying.
LUCIUS
You do?
BARMAID
Well, by allowing him to assume the responsibility of protecting us, we're surrendering our independence.
LUCIUS
That's right!
BARMAID
Right. So, in exchange for the convenience of having someone safeguard our town, we're giving up the very part of ourselves we seek to protect!
LUCIUS
Listen to her. She's smart!
BARMAID
We need to regain our strength and our independence by taking responsibility for our lives, and the lives of our neighbors.

[The villagers are all murmuring in agreement.]

LUCIUS
Yep.
BARMAID
(gaining confidence)
After today, we will no longer be dependent on one man.
LUCIUS
(nervously)
Well okay, okay…
BARMAID
(intensely)
There will be no champion protector. For it will fall to us to protect—
LUCIUS
Okay, okay. Hold up-hold up. Wait-wait-wait. You're getting a little carried away here. Okay? Independence is a great thing, but so is the comfort of having somebody there to pick up the slack now and again, okay. You don't want to be doing it all the time, because, you know, you got jobs. Or, you-you have hobbies, right? Beardo here, he likes to fish. Right?
(the bearded man nods in agreement)
You don't want to be bothered by having to "rise up" and save the village every day. You know what I mean? But that's a thought for another day. Okay? For today, you're gonna have to fight for yourselves.
(pats barmaid on shoulder)
Okay, so remember what you said about strength, and integrity, and responsibility. So you get out there, and you fight for your town! Go to the alleys. Spread the word! Now go-get out! Get out! Okay, yes, rally, rally. Yes, good kid.

[The rallied villagers scramble to prepare.]

EXT—VILLAGE SQUARE

KOLYA
(into radio)
Colonel Sheppard?
AMARIS
Maybe he changed his mind.

[Kolya glares at Amaris.]

KOLYA
(into radio)
You're out of time, Colonel.

[He tosses the radio away.]

KOLYA
Doctor McKay!

[Ronon steps in front of McKay.]

RONON
Me first.

[Teyla steps in front of Ronon.]

TEYLA
No me.
BECKETT
I'll do it.
MCKAY
I'm…
KOLYA
Well, how positively moving. I almost wish I could shoot you all at once.

[He raises his pistol.]

SHEPPARD
Kolya!

[Sheppard appears from the other side of the square. Kolya holsters his sidearm. All the other soldiers keep their weapons trained on Sheppard, except for those holding the rest of the team.]

KOLYA
I should've killed you when we first met, Colonel. It would've saved me a lot of trouble.
SHEPPARD
The feeling's mutual.
KOLYA
Goodbye, Sheppard.

[The soldiers start shooting. They expend several rounds from various guns, but all are repelled by the green Ancient force field. After they're done, Sheppard unzips his tactical vest, revealing the device.]

SHEPPARD
Well, now that you've got that out of your system…

[The shield shorts out.]

SHEPPARD
Oh crap.
KOLYA
Reload!

[The soldiers begin reloading. Lucius walks into the square.]

LUCIUS
Don't bother!
KOLYA
You should've left town when you had the chance.

[The villagers swarm out of all the buildings, carrying rifles, pitchforks, picks, and other makeshift weapons. They heavily outnumber the Genii.]

LUCIUS
I'm sure you're thinking the same thing right about now.
SHEPPARD
If I gave you the opportunity to surrender, I don't suppose you'd take it?

[Kolya chuckles.]

SHEPPARD
I didn't think so.

[They stare each other down, then both draw their sidearms. Sheppard outdraws Kolya, shooting him dead.]

SHEPPARD
(to other Genii)
Put down your weapons.

[The Genii surrender.]

LATER

[A large table has been set out, brightly decorated. The villagers are happy, setting up a celebration. Lucius walks with the Atlantis team.]

LUCIUS
You sure you guys can't stay for some schnitzel? I mean, this is a party for you guys as much anybody, right?
SHEPPARD
You guys have a good time. We're overdue.
LUCIUS
Well, thanks for everything.
SHEPPARD
Just remember, stay out of trouble, or we'll be back.
LUCIUS
Oh…uh, Colonel Sheppard? Aren't you forgetting something?
SHEPPARD
(smiling)
Oh, yeah. I did say I'd give that back to you, didn't I?

[Sheppard reaches into one of his vest pockets.]

LUCIUS
Yes you did.

[Sheppard slaps the Ancient shield device onto Lucius's chest.]

LUCIUS
(smiling)
You're a good man. You're an honest man. And you made me a better man myself, you know that, don't you?
SHEPPARD
Good. Bye, Lucius.

[Sheppard seems to be fighting a smile. The rest of his team look bemused as they follow Sheppard out of the village.]

MCKAY
I thought you said that personal shield was depleted?
SHEPPARD
Oh yeah.
TEYLA
Then, it is worthless.
SHEPPARD
(shrugging)
Pretty much.
BECKETT
You're not going to tell Lucius?
SHEPPARD
Nope.
LUCIUS
Hit me as hard as you can.

[The child kicks Lucius in the shins. Lucius doubles over in pain.]

LUCIUS
Ow! Ow! Ow!
SHEPPARD
He'll figure it out.

FADE OUT

END CREDITS
