SGA 3.19 "Vengeance" Episode Guide

Précis

The team stumbles upon the results of Michael's genetic experimentations and ends up fighting for their lives against a giant bug army.

Guide | Transcript

MICHAEL
(voiceover)
I allowed the bug to gorge itself, to absorb as much human DNA as possible. This, of course, led to the death of the subject. The timing of the feeding was critical, ensuring that the bug laid its next egg quickly, before the genetic material could filter out of its body.

[Michael discards the body in the room carelessly on a pile with many other Taranans the room of corpses Ronon discovered.]

[Later in his lab, Michael injects serum into the eggs. And later still, he watches the pods as they develop.]

MICHAEL
(continued voiceover)
The embryo then contained enough human DNA for me to manipulate. I accelerated the development, augmenting the complexity of its physiology. There were several adjustments…several mistakes…until I finally found the perfect balance between strength, agility, and resilience.

END FLASHBACK

MICHAEL
A perfect animal to do my bidding.

[Michael gazes rapturously to the doorway. A growling can be heard. Teyla, frightened, looks to the door. One of the creatures stands there.]

INT—TARANAN UNDERGROUND COMPLEX CORRIDOR

[The rest of the team runs to the end of a corridor, where a metal door is sealed shut. They can't open it.]

MCKAY
Must be some kind of security lockdown. Whoever took Teyla no doubt triggered it.
SHEPPARD
We've got to open this up.
RONON
Stand back.

[Ronon immediately fires at it. McKay and Sheppard can barely duck in time. The blast has no affect on the door.]

SHEPPARD
Jeez! You mind giving us a little more "heads up" before you start blasting everything?
RONON
You got any more C-4?

INT—MICHAEL'S LAB

MICHAEL
I saw how you and your team destroyed the incubation room. But don't think that's the only one in this complex. And don't think that this is the only planet from which I am conducting this experiment.

[Teyla looks horrified as the creature moves away.]

MICHAEL
That's right. Soon, hundreds of these creatures will be at my disposal, and their numbers will continue to grow—so long as I have human subjects in supply. And now that you're here…I do.

[Michael moves away. Teyla tries to get out of her bindings.]

INT—TARANAN UNDERGROUND COMPLEX CORRIDOR

[McKay, Ronon, and Sheppard take cover at a side corridor.]

SHEPPARD
All right, that's the last of the C-4. Let's hope it's enough. Ready?

[He triggers the detonator. Nothing happens.]

MCKAY
What happened?

[Sheppard peeks around the corner. The explosion finally happens, and Sheppard barely ducks in time. The door is blown.]

SHEPPARD
All right. Let's go.

[They run to the broken door and cautiously enter. McKay's scanner beeps.]

MCKAY
Whoa. Serious energy spike.
RONON
What does that mean?
MCKAY
We've probably just entered the shielded section of the settlement.

INT—MICHAEL'S LAB

[A red light appears.]

MICHAEL
Your friends are coming for you. They'll meet the same fate as you…one by one.

[He nods to the creature at the doorway, who leaves.]

TEYLA
Others will come for us. Dr. Weir will send—
MICHAEL
Don't concern yourself with Dr. Weir. She thinks your team has met up with the Taranan people, and all is well. One of the marines she sent to help you was…kind enough to assist me in transmitting a message back to Atlantis.
TEYLA
(sighing, pleading)
Oh…Michael…please don't do this.
MICHAEL
After what you did to me…twice…how could you expect anything less?
TEYLA
What happened back on the planet—
MICHAEL
(interrupting)
No more excuses. Your experiment failed. You decided to kill us.

[Michael picks up a small box and brings it over to the foot of Teyla's table.]

TEYLA
We believed we were left with no choice.
MICHAEL
And now I'm in the same position. You drove me to this.

[He opens the box. An Iratus bug comes out and starts climbing up Teyla's body.]

INT—TARANAN UNDERGROUND COMPLEX CORRIDOR

MCKAY
Oh, my god.

[Sheppard and McKay crouch by the bodies of two of Negley's team.]

RONON
Where are the others?

[He moves ahead.]

RONON
Sheppard. Over here.

[Sheppard and McKay run ahead to where Ronon crouches over the other two dead marines. Sheppard pulls Negley's dogtags. There is a chirping of insects.]

RONON
You hear that?
MCKAY
Oh, it sounds like bugs.
SHEPPARD
Come on.

[He and McKay head out. Ronon takes the other man's dogtags and closes his eyes.]

[Sheppard leads the group while McKay works with his scanner.]

SHEPPARD
Put that thing away. I thought you said it didn't work in this area anyway.
MCKAY
Please, I-I just thought I could recalibrate it to take into effect the—

[The creature tackles McKay. Sheppard turns and starts shooting at it. Ronon fires at it as well.]

INT—MICHAEL'S LAB

[Michael hears the shrieking of the creature and gunfire in the distance. Teyla also hears. Michael stalks away.]

INT—TARANAN UNDERGROUND COMPLEX CORRIDOR

[The trio stand over the creature's limp body.]

SHEPPARD
It's dead.

[McKay collapses in relief.]

INT—MICHAEL'S LAB

[The bug chirps as it climbs up Teyla's leg and torso towards her throat.]

TEYLA
John! John!

[The bug backs up a few steps, frightened by Teyla's screams, but hisses and decides to strike. Ronon's gun blasts it away. He and Sheppard stand in the doorway.]

SHEPPARD
(to Ronon)
Nice shot!
(To Teyla)
You okay?

[The three men rush in and start to unstrap Teyla's bindings.]

TEYLA
Yes. It's Michael. He is the one doing this.
SHEPPARD
(surprised)
Michael? Our Michael?
MCKAY
He's alive?
TEYLA
Yes. He created the creatures, and he's attempting to build an army of them.
RONON
Where is he?
TEYLA
I don't know. He ran off as soon as he heard the gunfire.
SHEPPARD
All right, we need to come up with the best plan here.
RONON
The best plan is to kill him.

[Ronon starts after him.]

SHEPPARD
Hey, just hang on a sec.
MCKAY
Maybe I should check out these workstations. There might be some intel…
RONON
We don't have any time.
MCKAY
(continuing, insistent)
…such as how many of these creatures he created, and if they have any weaknesses that we might be able to exploit.
SHEPPARD
All right. Teyla, you stay with Rodney. Ronon and I are going after Michael. We'll stay in touch on the radio.
MCKAY
Can't. Shielded, remember? Look, the energy spike, it fouls with radio communications as well.
SHEPPARD
Well, then un-shield it. Let's go.

[Sheppard and Ronon leave. McKay sighs and gets to work.]

INT—TARANAN UNDERGROUND COMPLEX CORRIDOR

SHEPPARD
You go that way, I'll go the other. Oh, hey, wait. Set your gun to stun.
RONON
(exasperated)
What?
SHEPPARD
Yeah, we're going to need to question him.
RONON
No.
RONON
No more talking, no more questioning. I'm going to do what we should've done the first time we captured him.
SHEPPARD
Ronon, listen to me—
RONON
(interrupting)
No, you listen to me, John. This whole retrovirus thing was a mistake. I said it then, no one listened to me. It was a bad idea.
SHEPPARD
We had to try. If it worked, we wouldn't have to—
RONON
But it didn't work! Admit it. It just made things worse. How long do you want to keep paying for it?

INT—MICHAEL'S LAB

MCKAY
It is amazing how much work he's been able to get done in such a short amount of time. He's quite the industrious little fella…or Wraith… or whatever. What does he look like now? I mean, is he full-on Wraith again?
TEYLA
Not quite. There are still human characteristics present in him. No doubt it's why I was confused when I first felt his presence.
MCKAY
You would think that the human side of him would temper his aggression a bit.
TEYLA
(sarcastically)
Yes…because we humans aren't aggressive at all.
MCKAY
Well, certainly less aggressive than— Oh, sarcasm. Yeah. Nice.
TEYLA
What Michael is doing right now must be stopped, and he cannot be allowed to escape. He's far too dangerous.
MCKAY
Eh, you're preaching to the choir, my dear.
TEYLA
But I understand his anger. His life has been destroyed. He no longer has a home or a family…
MCKAY
He had a family?
TEYLA
I don't know. But I've often wondered what he left behind when he was captured by us. We know nothing of his past, other than he was a Wraith.

[McKay studies Michael's data.]

MCKAY
A very smart Wraith.
TEYLA
Perhaps he was a scientist.
MCKAY
Sure. Probably. Why not? One of their very best, I would wager.
TEYLA
Like you.
MCKAY
Right. Wait a minute.
TEYLA
What is it?
MCKAY
I think I just figured out how to disable the EM interference that is causing the shielding.
(into radio)
Sheppard, you there?

[The exchange switches between the lab and the corridor where Sheppard patrols.]

SHEPPARD
Yeah, I'm here.
MCKAY
I got the shielding disabled.
SHEPPARD
So I see.
MCKAY
Any luck finding Michael?
SHEPPARD
No sign of him yet. Is that lifesigns detector working yet?
MCKAY
Oh. Of course.

[He pulls out the lifesigns detector and checks it. He sees several dots massed in front of Sheppard's life sign.]

MCKAY
Oh, my god.
SHEPPARD
What?

[Teyla joins McKay to look over his shoulder.]

MCKAY
I am reading a ton of lifesigns near you.
SHEPPARD
Well, define "ton" and define "near."
MCKAY
A few dozen? I mean, they're all bunched together.
SHEPPARD
Prisoners in a holding cell, maybe?
TEYLA
Or worse.

[Teyla goes back to keep watch on the corridor.]

INT—TARANAN UNDERGROUND COMPLEX CORRIDOR

[Michael goes to a closed door and opens it. Several of the creatures walk out.]

MCKAY
Whatever they are, someone just released them, and they're headed your way.
SHEPPARD
Oh, crap.

INT—TARANAN UNDERGROUND COMPLEX

[Ronon moves down a corridor. He's attacked by a creature as he turns a corner. He distracts it with a knife and then blasts it in the vulnerable underbelly, killing it.]

[Sheppard patrols another corridor.]

MCKAY
(into radio)
Keep going, straight up the tunnel. Ronon should be coming around the corner towards you…now.

[Michael comes around the corner, his weapon at ready. Sheppard raises his own sidearm.]

SHEPPARD
It's not Ronon.

INT—MICHAEL'S LAB

MCKAY
(afraid)
It's one of the bugs?
SHEPPARD
Michael.

[Teyla and McKay exchange looks.]

INT—TARANAN UNDERGROUND COMPLEX CORRIDOR

MICHAEL
Colonel Sheppard.
SHEPPARD
No offense, but I was kind of hoping never to see you again.
MICHAEL
I had hoped just the opposite.
SHEPPARD
Well, then. You've seen me. Now, say your goodbyes…but first, give me the control crystals to the DHD.
MICHAEL
I don't think so.
SHEPPARD
Or I can just kill you.
MICHAEL
Unless I stun you first.

[They can hear a creature approaching.]

SHEPPARD
As much as I'd like to sit around chatting…
MICHAEL
Extraordinary, aren't they?
SHEPPARD
Your army of bug people?
MICHAEL
I thought you'd be impressed.
SHEPPARD
Not so much.
MICHAEL
After all, I'm merely continuing the experiment your people began.
SHEPPARD
What then? Take over the entire galaxy? That's a little too "Dr. Evil," if you ask me.
MCKAY
(over radio)
Sheppard, the creatures are closing in on you fast. You better get moving.
SHEPPARD
You and Teyla go to the gate. We'll meet you there.
MCKAY
(over radio)
All right.
SHEPPARD
Give me the control crystal.
MICHAEL
I can't do that, Colonel.
SHEPPARD
Just so we're clear, I have no problem killing you.

[Michael lowers his stunner and steps forward.]

MICHAEL
And I have no problem with dying. Because even if you kill me right now…I'll die knowing that you and your team did not get off this planet alive.

[The creature attacks Sheppard, throwing him down the corridor. Michael runs away. From the other direction, Ronon arrives, blasting the creature off of Sheppard.]

INT—TARANAN UNDERGROUND COMPLEX

[Teyla and McKay hurry to where their rappelling equipment is hooked up.]

TEYLA
Go!
MCKAY
Rope climbing's the reason I got bronze instead of silver in the ParticipAction program.
TEYLA
What?

[Creatures get closer. Teyla looks down the corridor.]

TEYLA
Hurry! They're coming!

[She fires at them while McKay hurries to climb up.]

INT—TARANAN UNDERGROUND COMPLEX CORRIDOR

[Ronon and Sheppard run down the corridor, firing at creatures as they meet them.]

SHEPPARD
This is messed up. (seeing something to the side)
This must be where they got up.
RONON
What?
SHEPPARD
Air shafts. It's about time we used them.

[He and Ronon climb into an air shaft ladder.]

EXT—TARANAN FOREST, NIGHT

[Sheppard and Ronon reach the surface. Ronon blasts away some creatures trying to follow them. They look around, spotting a dart parked on the surface.]

RONON
Michael's.
SHEPPARD
No doubt.
RONON
You thinking what I'm thinking?
SHEPPARD
I fly it, pick you, Rodney, and Teyla up. We use the dart's DHD to dial the gate.
RONON
(considering)
I was thinking "blow it up," but your idea's better. Get moving.

[Sheppard runs to the dart. Ronon blasts some creatures climbing up the air shaft. He hears more converging on them in the forest. They appear out of the fog, surrounding him.]

RONON
Sheppard!

[The dart powers up. Ronon starts firing on them. The dart culls Ronon.]

EXT—NEW TARANAS FOREST, NIGHT

[Teyla and McKay run through the forest. McKay checks his scanner.]

MCKAY
Wait!
TEYLA
What is it?
MCKAY
Lifesigns up ahead.
TEYLA
Colonel Sheppard? Ronon?

[They hear the creatures growling nearby.]

MCKAY
Doesn't sound like it. Lifesigns behind us, too. It's closing in fast. Oh, god.

[The scanner beeps frantically and McKay spots dots on all sides drawing closer to Teyla and McKay.]

MCKAY
We're surrounded.

[Teyla and McKay stand back to back, searching for enemies. Teyla is hit by a Wraith stun blast. She falls. McKay turns, but he too is felled by the stunner. Michael strides forward. A dart flies overhead. It culls Teyla and McKay before Michael can get to them. He breathes heavily, looking frustrated.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[The Stargate activates. Weir approaches Chuck's station.]

CHUCK
Incoming wormhole. Colonel Sheppard's IDC.
WEIR
Lower the shield.

INT—ATLANTIS GATE ROOM

[The shield lowers as Weir approaches the Stargate. Sheppard's team walks through.]

WEIR
Are you all right? Where are the marines?
SHEPPARD
Didn't make it.
WEIR
What?
MCKAY
We barely made it ourselves. We had to commandeer a Wraith dart just to dial out.
SHEPPARD
We ditched it on another planet. We didn't want to risk damaging the control room. Daedalus can pick it up later.
WEIR
What happened to the Taranans?
RONON
They're dead, all of them.
WEIR
Wait a minute. Lieutenant Negley sent a message saying they just relocated to a new settlement.
TEYLA
No. It was Michael. He sent the transmission. He's the one that's killed the Taranans.
WEIR
(shocked)
Michael?
SHEPPARD
We'll tell you about it in a minute, but first, is the Daedalus here yet?
WEIR
It's still a few days out. Why?
SHEPPARD
We're going to need it to make another stop on the way.
WEIR
Okay.

EXT—ATLANTIS, NIGHT

INT—ATLANTIS CORRIDOR

[Weir walks down a corridor, deep in thought and looking disturbed. Teyla meets her in the passageway and notices her demeanor.]

TEYLA
Elizabeth?
WEIR
I just came from McKay's lab. He's been examining the research he was able to upload from Michael's experiment.
TEYLA
Has he been able to determine just how many more of those creatures Michael was able to create?
WEIR
Well, not an exact figure, no, but Rodney believes it's well into the hundreds.

[Teyla gasps in shock.]

WEIR
Yeah, and it appears Michael has similar labs on at least three different planets.
TEYLA
It's amazing how quickly he's been able to incorporate our science into his.

[Weir is still deep in thought.]

TEYLA
What is it?
WEIR
Michael found out about the Taranan settlement through information the Wraith hacked from our database during our brief alliance with them last year. We led Michael straight to them.

[She walks away.]

INT—WEIR'S OFFICE

[Weir studies a tablet computer. Sheppard enters.]

SHEPPARD
Colonel Caldwell just radioed. They did a full sweep of the planet. No lifesigns. They searched the settlement. No Michael, no creatures.
WEIR
He already dialed out.
SHEPPARD
Yep. Got the control crystal back in the DHD. Rodney's trying to figure out the last address dialed, but, uh, well, you know how long that takes.
WEIR
Yeah, I'm sure Michael immediately dialed elsewhere to cover his tracks.
SHEPPARD
Yeah, you can count on that.
WEIR
We need to find him. He's already too dangerous.
SHEPPARD
And I'm sure we're number one on his hit list.

FADE OUT

END CREDITS
