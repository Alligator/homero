Episode Guide

SGA 3.07 "Common Ground" Episode Guide

Précis

An ambush leaves Col. Sheppard a prisoner of the Genii. While Dr. Weir tries to figure out who Atlantis's allies and enemies really are, Sheppard must risk an unlikely alliance to gain his freedom.

Guide | Transcript

KOLYA

Three hours.

WEIR
So does my answer.
KOLYA
(considering)
Then you're effectively ending his life.
WEIR
I'm not gonna go there.
KOLYA
(over video)
Is Ladon there?
LADON
I am.
KOLYA
(over video)
I can't help but wonder what you've told them so they'd choose you over one of their own.
LADON
That I betrayed you. That I took for myself what you believed to be rightfully yours.
KOLYA
(over video)
The truth. I must say, I'm surprised.
LADON
If you release him, you may return to our people with my promise of amnesty.
KOLYA
(over video)
Please, Ladon. I've trained you better than that. There are things that cannot be undone.
WEIR
That's not true! You can end this!
KOLYA
(over video)
Strange, Doctor. I was just about to say the same thing.

[Behind Kolya, the Wraith can be seen being moved into position.]

INT—GENII CAMERA ROOM

KOLYA
(to Wraith)
Take your fill.

[The Wraith feeds.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[Everyone watches in horror. Ronon cries out and walks away, unable to watch.]

INT—GENII CAMERA ROOM

[The Wraith suddenly stops feeding and pulls his hand away.]

KOLYA
Who told you to stop?
WRAITH
He is near death. Shall I finish him?

[Kolya stares at Sheppard, who is even more visibly aged. He looks away.]

KOLYA
(to guards)
Get it out of here.

[The guards drag the Wraith away.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

KOLYA
(over video)
Now it's two hours.

[The video feed and the Stargate shuts off.]

BECKETT
If he's fed upon again, he's as good as dead.
MCKAY
We don't know that.
BECKETT
Aye, Rodney, we do. He may already be past the point of no return. We know that a large percentage of those that survive a partial feeding die anyway…due to complications as a result of strain on the system. What appears as physical aging is actually a byproduct of a complex process we barely understand. I can't imagine him surviving another session—whether it's two hours or ten hours from now.
RONON
(to Ladon)
If you were half the man Sheppard is, you'd volunteer to make the trade yourself.
LADON
Truth is, I'm not sure whether or not I would've made the trade if our roles were reversed.
(to Weir)
So I ask you, allow what you have done for me to be worth something. There's nothing more I can do here, but I can get you the information you need to find Sheppard if you allow me to return and interrogate the men who stole the codes from me.

[Weir exchanges looks with the others.]

INT—GENII CELLS

[Sheppard wakes up in his cell. He moves with difficulty. He looks up to see the Wraith observing him from its own cell.]

SHEPPARD
You know, I could've sworn I was gonna wake up dead today.
WRAITH
You are strong. Stronger than any human I have ever fed upon.
SHEPPARD
You stopped yourself.
WRAITH
Yes.
SHEPPARD
Why?
WRAITH
Because, the longer I feed, the weaker you become…and we will need what strength you have left…to escape.
SHEPPARD
Now he wants to escape. Hmm.

INT—ATLANTIS GATE ROOM

[The Stargate is active. The Genii guards get back their weapons. Weir and Sheppard's team escort Ladon down the stairs.]

LADON
I lack the words to thank you, Doctor Weir.
WEIR
I didn't do this for you.
LADON
Well, nevertheless, I'll be in touch the moment I have the information that you need.
WEIR
Thank you.

[Ladon and his men leave.]

MCKAY
You know, I have the sinking feeling we've just made a terrible mistake.
RONON
Because we have.
TEYLA
I'm not so sure, Ronon.

INT—GENII CELLS

[The guards approach to get Sheppard and the Wraith. They pull Sheppard to his feet and drag him out. Other guards pull the Wraith out and begin to attach the restraints. The feeding arm is sheathed. Sheppard pretends to collapse, catching the men offguard, and slamming them both into the bars. The Wraith backhands one of his guards and uses the chain restraining him to choke the other, slamming the feeding arm lock against the wall until it releases. He starts feeding on the guard he chokes.]

[Sheppard gains the upper hand on his two stunned guards. Meanwhile the other guard with the Wraith pulls his sidearm and shoots several rounds into the Wraith. The feeding Wraith screams in pain. Sheppard notices and grabs the knife on the belt of the guard he's fighting and throws it at the shooter, hitting him in the chest. The Wraith continues to feed on the guard. Sheppard knocks his guard out and rummages at the guy's belt. The Wraith approaches and notices Sheppard now has a sidearm. Sheppard glances at the Wraith, then throws him the keys. The Wraith frees his other hand. Sheppard grabs the other guard's sidearm and warily hands it over to the Wraith.]

SHEPPARD
Which way?
WRAITH
This way.

INT—GENII CORRIDORS

[Sheppard and a Wraith stand sheltered at a corner near guarded areas. The Wraith studies the Genii sidearm, distractedly holding it near Sheppard's face. When Sheppard notices, he knocks the weapon aside so it's not aimed so near him. He holds a Genii radio in his hand with a meaningful expression and activates it.]

[The Genii guard lifts his radio when he hears squelch coming from the receiver. Sheppard immediately grabs him from behind, knife at ready. The other guard moves to help, but the Wraith grabs him and breaks his neck. Another guard arrives and shoots the Wraith. He starts to shoot at Sheppard, but the guard Sheppard holds acts as a shield. The rounds kill that guard. Sheppard is able to fire his own sidearm and kill the guard. Sheppard glances at the rounds in the Wraith's side questioningly.]

WRAITH
It will heal. This way.

[He indicates the staircase leading up. Sheppard replaces his empty gun for a new one and follows the limping Wraith.]

[Later, Kolya and other guards find the bodies.]

KOLYA
They did this together.
GUARD
They've taken weapons.
KOLYA
I want ten armed men at the Stargate. The rest of you, hunt them down.

EXT—FOREST, NIGHT

[A round vertical access tunnel is in the forest. The hatch opens, and Sheppard and the Wraith climb out. Both move with difficulty.]

SHEPPARD
How far is the Stargate?
WRAITH
It will be guarded.
SHEPPARD
We've got guns.
WRAITH
(irritated)
They will be waiting for us!
SHEPPARD
Don't be so negative.

[The Wraith groans and half-collapses into a sitting position on the edge of the hatch.]

SHEPPARD
Think you're gonna make it?
WRAITH
(nods)
If I feed.
SHEPPARD
Well, don't look at me.

[The Wraith tries to stand, reaching out with his feeding hand. Sheppard steps back and raises his pistol. The Wraith finally stands and sees Sheppard, thus raising his own gun.]

SHEPPARD
When we make it to the Stargate, we both go our separate ways. Until then, we're gonna need each other. Deal?

[The Wraith nods. He then stumbles into the forest, Sheppard following.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[Weir stands at the balcony. Chuck the technician approaches.]

CHUCK
Doctor Weir?
WEIR
How long has it been?

[Chuck checks his watch.]

CHUCK
It's coming up on two hours. We should receive the next transmission any minute now.
WEIR
Yes, I'll be there in a moment.

[Chuck nods and leaves her alone.]

EXT—FOREST, NIGHT

[The Wraith and Sheppard continue to stumble through the woods.]

SHEPPARD
I was blindfolded all the way to the bunker. It wasn't this far.
WRAITH
If I could just move faster!

[The Wraith falls to the ground.]

SHEPPARD
We'll rest here a few minutes.
WRAITH
You should go on without me.
SHEPPARD
No! The 'Gate's guarded. We'll have a better chance of taking out the guards in a crossfire. I still need you.
WRAITH
Very well.

[He struggles to rise.]

SHEPPARD
That is, even if we're going in the right direction…. You have no idea where the Stargate is, do you?

[The Wraith sits back down, defeated.]

WRAITH
It was many years ago.
SHEPPARD
Way to go, John! Listen to a Wraith.
WRAITH
It was not my intention to deceive you, Sheppard.

[The radio in Sheppard's hand squawks.]

GUARD
(over radio)
Our reinforcements have arrived at the Stargate, Commander.
KOLYA
(over radio)
Kill the Wraith on sight, but I want Sheppard alive.
SHEPPARD
Well, we learned two things. One, he likes me better than you.

[The Wraith laughs.]

SHEPPARD
Two, we probably would've never made it to the Stargate anyway.
WRAITH
Then it is over.
SHEPPARD
No. Our people don't leave each other behind. That's three things you've learned.
WRAITH
You still believe that.
SHEPPARD
Kolya doesn't know where we are. He's wasting manpower that could be used searching for us guarding the 'Gate. The odds of my people finding us are going up and up.
WRAITH
You are more like Wraith than you know.
SHEPPARD
I'm not sure I like the sound of that.
WRAITH
There is much about Wraith that you do not know, Sheppard.

[Sheppard studies the Wraith.]

INT—WEIR'S OFFICE

[The rest of Sheppard's team, Beckett, and Weir wait in her office.]

MCKAY
Kolya's late.
TELYA
That is not like him.
MCKAY
We haven't heard anything from Ladon, either.
WEIR
We will.
TEYLA
Is it possible they were in on this from the beginning?
WEIR
No, I don't think so. Eh…why would Ladon come here in the first place?
RONON
Throw us off the scent.
MCKAY
What kind of a plan would that be?
RONON
Genii.
MCKAY
True.
TEYLA
Either way, there must be a reason why Kolya has not contacted us.
BECKETT
Perhaps he knew it would take Colonel Sheppard more time to recover.
MCKAY
None of us are saying what we're all thinking here.
WEIR
(warningly)
No, Rodney, we're not.

INT—FOREST, NIGHT

[The Wraith lies face up on the ground. Sheppard is crouched, listening to the radio.]

GUARD
(over radio)
Tell Kolya there's nobody around the 'Gate.
SHEPPARD
Sounds like they're concentrating their search around the 'Gate. They must think we knew where we were going.

[The Wraith laughs, then sighs as he looks at the three moons in the sky.]

WRAITH
Oh, it was worth it…if only to see the sky again.
SHEPPARD
I've got slightly higher expectations.
WRAITH
My wound is deep. If I do not feed soon…I will die.

[He partially sits up and looks at Sheppard.]

SHEPPARD
Buck up. We got a deal, remember. We both go home…alive.
WRAITH
And if we were to meet again in the future…what then?
SHEPPARD
(after a pause)
All bets are off.
WRAITH
(chuckling)
Then let us hope we do not meet again.
SHEPPARD
Try to get some sleep. I'll take the first watch.

[The Wraith lies down again. With a groan, Sheppard gets to his feet.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[The team, Beckett, and Weir enter the room. The Stargate is active.]

CHUCK
I'm receiving the new Genii IDC along with a video feed.
TEYLA
Ladon.
WEIR
Put it on the screen!

[Ladon's face appears on the monitor.]

LADON
(over video)
Are you receiving me?
WEIR
We were starting to get worried.
LADON
(over video)
Yes, I'm sure you had doubts. But the truth is, the interrogation took longer than I hoped. We've secured the location of Kolya's base. I'm forwarding you the dialing coordinates right now.
WEIR
Are you certain?
LADON
(over video)
Certain enough to send a full brigade through the Gate. It will take us an hour to marshall our forces. I thought you might want that time.
WEIR
What will we be up against?
LADON
(over video)
Nothing one of your cloaked jumpers and my good friend Ronon couldn't handle. I ask only that you leave Kolya for my own forces to find.
WEIR
No promises.
LADON
(over video)
I understand.

[The video feed ends.]

WEIR
(to team)
All right, get a cloaked jumper and a team of marines.

[McKay takes off. Beckett raises his hand.]

BECKETT
I'd like to reserve one of those seats for a member of my medical team.
WEIR
Done.
(looking at Ronon)
And if you do find Kolya…?
RONON
(amused)
No promises.
WEIR
Exactly.

EXT—FOREST, DAY

[Sheppard and the Wraith both sleep. Genii soldiers approach their position. The Wraith awakens, hearing the guards. He gets up, clutching his side, and goes over to Sheppard, Sheppard awakens, but is groggy.]

WRAITH
(in low voice)
They're coming.

[The Wraith feeds on Sheppard. Sheppard becomes almost skeletal, and very aged. The guards arrive, finding Sheppard's fed upon body, but bypassing it. The Wraith jumps down from the tree above. He is strong, knocking guards away and taking fire at point blank range. He knocks down the remaining two shooters, then feeds on one while holding down the other.]

EXT—GATE AREA, PLANET, DAY

[The Stargate activates. Several Genii soldiers watch as a puddle jumper comes through and instantly cloaks as they fire on it.]

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

[McKay flies the jumper, Teyla in the copilot seat. Beckett, Ronon, and marines are also in the jumper. The HUD display is in front of them.]

TEYLA
The facility is straight ahead.

[Dots appear on the screen. Some disappearing as they talk.]

MCKAY
We're detecting life signs on the surface, miles from the bunker.
RONON
How many?
MCKAY
When it first came up, it was eight, then four…I don't get it. Now, it's just the two.
BECKETT
What's going on down there?
MCKAY
Well, one of them's definitely Sheppard. His subcutaneous transmitter's still broadcasting.
TEYLA
Could the other one be Kolya?
RONON
Let's hope so.
(to McKay)
Land a hundred meters to the south of their position.
MCKAY
What?

[He does so, confused.]

EXT—FOREST, DAY

[The soldiers are all dead. The Wraith stands over Sheppard. He is barely alive. The Wraith bends down.]

SHEPPARD
(defiant)
Finish it!
WRAITH
As I told you, John Sheppard, there are many things about Wraith that you do not know.

[He reaches out with his feeding hand.]

[In another area of the forest, Sheppard's team and the marines approach. Ronon leads, signaling to hold. They hear Sheppard cry out in agony. Everyone races forward. Ronon pulls the Wraith off of Sheppard and raises his gun.]

SHEPPARD
Wait!

[The Wraith stands. Sheppard gets up. He looks fine, as he did before the first feeding. Everyone surrounds the Wraith, weapons raised.]

SHEPPARD
Leave him. That's an order!

[Ronon grudgingly complies and lowers his weapon.]

BECKETT
I don't understand. We all saw what he did to you.

[They stare at Sheppard in shock. Sheppard also looks confused as he approaches the Wraith.]

SHEPPARD
He just undid it. Lower your weapons.

[The marines comply.]

TEYLA
How is this possible?!
SHEPPARD
Don't ask me.
WRAITH
The gift of life is reserved only for our most devout worshipers…and our brothers.
SHEPPARD
Well, I guess there's a lot about the Wraith we don't know.
WRAITH
Sheppard gave me back my life. I merely repaid the debt.
MCKAY
(incredulous)
What debt?! Are you kidding? I mean, he looks younger than he did before!
RONON
What about Kolya?
SHEPPARD
Oh, god.

[Sheppard feels his pockets for the radio. He finds it on the ground where he'd been lying.]

SHEPPARD
(into radio)
Kolya?

EXT—STARGATE, DAY

[Kolya stands beside an active wormhole. He picks up his radio.]

SHEPPARD
(over radio)
This is Sheppard. Kolya? I figured you'd run! Next time, I kill you on sight, do you hear me?!

[Kolya looks dismayed, and doesn't respond. He walks through the event horizon.]

EXT—FOREST, DAY

[Sheppard paces angrily.]

SHEPPARD
Let's get off this rock.
RONON
(nodding to Wraith)
What about him?

[The Wraith watches nervously.]

SHEPPARD
We had a deal, right?
WRAITH
I did not truly expect you to honor it.

[He chuckles. Ronon hands Sheppard his stunner. Sheppard looks at the gun, grabs it and shoots the Wraith. Ronon snatches the gun back and checks the setting.]

RONON
(disappointed)
You didn't kill him?
SHEPPARD
No, I didn't.
(to marines)
Get him in the jumper. We're gonna drop him off somewhere.

[The marines start dragging the unconscious Wraith. Sheppard follows.]

SHEPPARD
(to McKay as he passes)
Thanks for showing up.
MCKAY
Yeah, but…

EXT—FOREST CLEARING BY STARGATE, NIGHT

[The Wraith awakens with a start. He sees Sheppard, geared up and holding a P-90 aimed toward him. The Stargate is active in the distance. The Wraith feels his chest for injury.]

WRAITH
Ah, Sheppard. I thought you…
SHEPPARD
There's a lot you don't know about humans.

[The Wraith looks around, realizing he is on a different planet.]

WRAITH
(nodding)
Ah, I see. Next time we meet?
SHEPPARD
All bets are off.

[They stare at each other. A Wraith dart is heard flying overhead. While the Wraith looks at it, Sheppard backs into the cargo hold of the cloaked jumper, disappearing when the Wraith looks around. He feels a breeze.]

FADE OUT

END CREDITS
