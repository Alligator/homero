SGA 3.03 "Irresistible" Episode Guide

Précis

The team searches for Stargates to harvest for an intergalactic bridge, and encounters a charming alchemist who beguiles Dr. Beckett.

Guide | Transcript

Transcript

TEASER

EXT—PLANET, DAY

[A puddle jumper flies over the fields and forests of an alien world.]

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

[Sheppard sneezes and coughs.]

TEYLA
Are you all right, Colonel?
SHEPPARD
(hoarsely)
It's nothing, just a cold. No big deal. It's just annoying.
MCKAY
Talk about annoying.
SHEPPARD
What?
MCKAY
We should be focusing on harvesting space gates.
SHEPPARD
That's what we're doing, Rodney. The MALP detected life signs. We have to check it out. That's the protocol.
MCKAY
I am just saying that identifying potential space gates to complete the intergalactic bridge should be our priority, not making contact with the locals of some backwater hamlet.
TEYLA
Are you that eager to return to Earth, Rodney?
MCKAY
This isn't just about me. It is about the ability to go back and forth between Earth and Atlantis conveniently…and whenever I want to.
RONON
How's that work again?
MCKAY
(turning in his chair back to Ronon, enthusiastic)
Okay, once we've seeded enough Stargates across the void between Pegasus and the Milky Way, we'll be able to take a jumper from one to the other to the other till we reach the other side. That way we won't tax the ZPM, we won't be reliant on the Daedalus, and travel time between Earth and Atlantis will be cut from three weeks to thirty minutes.

[He turns back to face forward, very proud.]

TEYLA
Very clever.
MCKAY
(smugly)
Yes.
(more reluctantly)
It was Samantha Carter's idea.
SHEPPARD
Backwater hamlet, dead ahead.

EXT—VILLAGE, DAY

[Several brightly dressed people smile and greet Sheppard's team warmly as they enter the village.]

MCKAY
(low, to team)
Okay, let's just make contact, buy our souvenirs, and…get out of here.

[McKay is distracted by two particularly attractive young women who smile.]

WILLA
Fair day to you.
SHEPPARD
(to Willa)
Fair day to you.
(to McKay, distracted)
Nothing here. We should probably go.

[They watch another attractive young woman walk by, smiling at them.]

MCKAY
(distracted)
Eh, it probably wouldn't hurt to, uh, make contact with the locals though, no?

[Teyla and Ronon exchange looks.]

SHEPPARD
No, no. When you're right, you're right.
MCKAY
No, no, well we're here now, and uh…

[A man with a wide grin yells from the length of the street.]

LUCIUS
Hello! Hello, new people! Why didn't anybody tell me the new people were here yet!

[Willa embraces Lucius with a wide smile.]

WILLA
Lucius, I've missed you!

[Lucius returns the hug distractedly, not breaking stride as he approaches the team.]

LUCIUS
Oh please. Well, I was just out for a walk. Have you met the new people yet?

[Willa shakes her head negatively as Lucius arrives in front of the team.]

SHEPPARD
We just got here.
LUCIUS
Oh, great! Great! Then I haven't missed anything. I hate missing things. Have you met my wife, Willa? Isn't she gorgeous?
(pointing to other beautiful women behind them)
My other wives are just making lunch.
HELEEN
Fair day, Lucius!
LUCIUS
Hello, sweetheart.
(blows her a kiss)
I love ya. I love ya.
(waves to villagers)
Fair day, everybody.

[The team exchange looks.]

VILLAGERS
Fair day, Lucius.
SHEPPARD
(nonplussed)
I'm…John…Sheppard. Rodney McKay, Ronon Dex, Teyla Emmagan.

[He gestures to his teammates in turn, who nod to Lucius in greeting. Lucius's eyes light on Teyla. He pushes Willa aside to get closer to her.]

LUCIUS
Teyla. And you are from?
TEYLA
(cautiously)
Athos.

[McKay and Sheppard exchange looks.]

LUCIUS
(seductively)
Athos. Yeah, Athos. I've never been there. Sounds nice. Is it nice?
TEYLA
(unamused)
Until it was culled by the Wraith.
LUCIUS
Oh, I hate them. What do you say you and me get together, we have some lunch, we talk about this, huh? Maybe have some wine?
MCKAY
(piqued)
We don't even know your name.

[Lucius turns to McKay. He and the villagers start to laugh as if McKay said something hilarious.]

SHEPPARD
We're serious.
WILLA
(shocked)
You mean you truly have not heard of Lucius?
HELEEN
Well everyone knows.
WILLA
He's the wisest and kindest man among the stars.
LUCIUS
(with mock humility)
Aw, people just say that. I mean, you can't prove it until you've met everybody, am I right? Hmm?
SHEPPARD
(diplomatically)
Well then…Lucius, we're glad we met you.

[Lucius leans closer to Sheppard.]

LUCIUS
And if you aren't yet, you will be! Come on! Let's have some lunch, huh? Everybody has to eat. Right? Come on, right this way, please. Please.

[Lucius's wives escort McKay and Sheppard.]

LUCIUS
(gesturing to Teyla and Ronon)
After you, please.

[Teyla and Ronon follow the others, looking back curiously at Lucius. He remains behind, smiling and waving at the other villagers.]

LUCIUS
Fair day. Fair day. Fair day.

[He takes a small flask out of his jacket and drinks a large swallow, wincing at the taste.]

LUCIUS
Here I come!

EXT—VILLAGE, DAY

EXT—VILLAGE SQUARE, DAY

[Several villagers smile and watch as Lucius and the team enjoy a meal in the center of town.]

(bored)

SHEPPARD
What are those, uh…gifts?

Exploration. Alchemy. Medicine. I have, I have to say, some of the best ointments around.

[Sheppard sneezes.]

LUCIUS
See, I could take care of that just…
(snaps his fingers)
like that.
SHEPPARD
Ah, it's just a cold.
LUCIUS
Nevertheless, I have a potion that could get rid of that in six or seven days.
MCKAY
Mmm.
SHEPPARD
(deadpan)
That's impressive.
LUCIUS
(shrugging)
You get used to it. We'll do a trade. So…where you all from?
SHEPPARD
No place in particular. We move around a lot.
LUCIUS
In that wonderful machine of yours. Yeah, I saw it. I was on a walk, I look up, and-and I heard…somebody said that-that you flew through the ancestral ring?

[Sheppard shakes his head dismissively.]

RONON
Yeah, that was us!

[Teyla glares at him.]

LUCIUS
How does it fly?
MCKAY
It's complicated. One has to—
LUCIUS
(interrupting)
I mean, where do you get a machine like that? I-it I mean, it's a wonderful way to travel. Can I get one? Are they hard to fly?
SHEPPARD
Yes. They are hard to fly, and…no, you can't have one.
LUCIUS
(insistent)
But I want one. Please.
SHEPPARD
(shrugging)
Sorry.

[The villagers begin to murmur in dismay.]

WILLA
Well, perhaps a trade for some of his wonderful medicines.
MCKAY
Or perhaps not.
LUCIUS
(insulted)
What? You come all this way, you don't even want to do a trade? Look, it doesn't even have to be the ship. Forget the ship. I don't even want the ship. Okay? You gotta have some other stuff that you want to trade, right? Okay? Like-like…how do you get your hair to go like that?

[Lucius looks expectantly at Sheppard. Sheppard appears nonplussed for a moment.]

SHEPPARD
Lunch was great…but I just realized we're running late.
MCKAY
For what?
SHEPPARD
For that important thing!

[He rises, the others follow suit.]

TEYLA
Yes! Um, as much as we would love to stay, we really must go.
LUCIUS
No, no, no, no, no. I don't want you to go. You just got here, come on! Come on! We haven't even had the second course. Please! I want-I want you to see some of my medicines.
(to Willa)
Go get the ointments, the good ones. Go! Run! Go!

[She runs to get his wares.]

SHEPPARD
Another time, Lucius. We gotta go.
LUCIUS
(in a tantrum)
No. No, I don't want you to leave!

[Sheppard's team finds their path blocked by the villagers, who close in at Lucius's words. They turn back to Lucius.]

LUCIUS
Okay, okay, okay. All right. Let them go. We're all friends here, right?

[The villagers break apart, letting the team pass.]

SHEPPARD
Tell you, what. As soon as we get back, we'll tell our medical people to come, see what you have to offer. Then we'll talk about trade.
LUCIUS
(hopeful)
Later today, maybe?
SHEPPARD
Maybe.
LUCIUS
I'll have everything ready.
SHEPPARD
Thank you.
LUCIUS
(calling after them)
You won't be disappointed!

[The team leaves. Lucius smiles.]

LUCIUS
Nice people.
VILLAGERS
(in agreement)
Mm-hmm.

(excited)
Yes! Right away.
SHEPPARD
It's not a race, Rodney.

INT—LUCIUS'S APOTHECARY SHOP

[Lucius leads Beckett around his shop.]

LUCIUS
Some of the herbs were gathered from this planet on my walks, but most were collected from my many excursions to other worlds. I have to confess, I have concocted remedies for almost every known ailment.
BECKETT
(skeptical)
Every single one?
LUCIUS
Well, every one in town. The sick have been cured, the lame can walk again…well, some still crawl, but they crawl a lot faster than they used to.
BECKETT
I see.
LUCIUS
Nothing pleases me more than when someone comes up to me, they say: "Thank you, Lucius. You have cured me. You are a wise and kind man."
BECKETT
(humoring him)
Hmm.
LUCIUS
Young, old… women… Do you like women, Doctor Beckett?
BECKETT
What? Yes, of course.
LUCIUS
Yeah, I have a potion that will have them lined up around the square just waiting to be with you. You know what I mean when I say "be with you", right?
BECKETT
(dryly)
I think so, yes.
LUCIUS
I will trade you that potion, for what you are holding in your bag…right now.
BECKETT
My medical kit?
LUCIUS
(nodding)
Mm-hmm.
BECKETT
But I thought you cured every known ailment.
LUCIUS
Every known ailment in town. I added that, remember?
LUCIUS
Well, it is tempting. But I'm afraid, uh, I couldn't do that, Lucius, sorry.
LUCIUS
(chuckling)
Not yet, anyway, huh? Come on, you must be starving. I have a great meal lined up for us.

EXT—ATLANTIS, NIGHT

INT—CONTROL ROOM

[Weir enters, and heads to where Zelenka, McKay, and Sheppard are.]

How did the scout go?

What quota?

Daedalus is still under repair anyway, Rodney. We won't be able to harvest 'gates till it can fly.

[McKay crosses his arms and pouts.]

SHEPPARD
Did Beckett bring back anything worth anything?
WEIR
No, he hasn't returned yet. He radioed in earlier, said he'd like to stay a little longer. Apparently, he's found something of interest.

[McKay scoffs.]

EXT—VILLAGE SQUARE, NIGHT

[Lucius sits telling stories to all the villagers, who sit spellbound by his tales.]

LUCIUS
The baby was frozen solid—like a statue. Like a frozen statue. It must've been in the water for I don't know, what must—it seemed like an eternity. I didn't know what to do. And I was a kid myself. I take the baby, and I open my coat, and I tuck the baby inside, and I start stroking the baby's back…gently. Now, I don't know whether it was warmth of my body, or the steady insistent rhythm of my own life force urging this tiny baby's heart to begin beating again. But beat it did.

[Beckett is just as enthralled as the other villagers. They all gasp in wonder.]

LUCIUS
And a cry arose, piercing the cold night air, as if to say…I am alive!

[Beckett starts the applause.]

BECKETT
(laughing in delight)
Oh! Astonishing!
LUCIUS
And that baby…that baby…grew up to be…

[Lucius gestures to the side. Beckett sees a woman pouring a tankard.]

BECKETT
That beautiful young lady?

[She smiles at Beckett's compliment.]

LUCIUS
No, no, no, not her. Behind her.

[The woman leaves with her tray of tankards. A young man turns a spit over the fire. He obviously is brain damaged.]

LUCIUS
Uh, maybe a little brain damage, but just a little. But the story is true.
BECKETT
(enraptured)
And thank you for sharing it with me.
LUCIUS
(standing)
Oh, please, Doctor Beckett. That's what I like doing most. To share. To share me.

BECKETT
Oh. And I can see that now.

Carson, this-uh, this city that you keep going on and on about?

Atlantis.

Yeah, Atlantis. Um, on a scale of one to ten, this-this village being a three…

You mean the jumpers?

Yeah.

Oh, aye, we have several.

LUCIUS
Okay!

[He moves to hop off the bed, but gets dizzy and has to sit back.]

BECKETT
Whoop! It takes about an hour to be effective. In the meantime, just rest easy, dear friend.

[Beckett leaves McKay and Weir to tend to Lucius, who lies back on the inclined bed.]

MCKAY
Are you feeling okay? Can I get you anything?
LUCIUS
(weakly)
No, Rodney. I'm fine.
MCKAY
Are you sure?
LUCIUS
Absolutely.
MCKAY
Because it would be no trouble…?
LUCIUS
(weakly)
Mess hall. Blue Jell-o. Just a little bit of whipped cream.
MCKAY
Right away.

[McKay runs out of the infirmary. Lucius reaches over and clasps Weir's hand.]

LUCIUS
We really should talk about marriage.

[Weir simpers with delight.]

EXT—ATLANTIS, DAY

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[Zelenka, Weir, McKay and the rest of the control room staff are all grinning with expectation.]

MCKAY
(into radio)
Jumper One, this is Flight. The bay doors are open! You are go for launch!

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

[Lucius sits at the pilot's chair punching buttons.]

LUCIUS
Go for launch. That's so exciting!

[Beckett pats him on the shoulder and sits in the co-pilot's chair.]

LUCIUS
Thanks, Rodney! All right, here goes!

[He starts to concentrate.]

SHEPPARD
I'll take it from here.

[Sheppard surprises Lucius from behind, snatching the radio from his ear, then manhandling him out of the pilot's chair, cuffing his hands behind him.]

LUCIUS
Wha-? You're supposed to be in jail! Carson…?
BECKETT
(unsympathetic)
Save your breath. Your charm no longer has any effect on me.

[Beckett gets up. Sheppard shoves Lucius into the vacated seat.]

LUCIUS
No. No, but…Carson! We're supposed to be the best of friends.

[Sheppard sits in the pilot's chair.]

SHEPPARD
Carson didn't give you the ATA gene.
LUCIUS
What?
BECKETT
I'm afraid it's a serum that effectively neutralizes the chemical in that herb of yours. Took some myself.
LUCIUS
Oh.

[Beckett leaves the jumper. Sheppard powers up the ship.]

LUCIUS
I don't know what that means.
SHEPPARD
It means we can have that face-to-face conversation you always wanted us to have. The only thing that will happen is you'll catch my cold.

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[Everyone waits expectantly, starting to worry.]

MCKAY
What is taking him so long? Okay…I should go up there.

[He starts to the jumper bay stairwell, Weir and Zelenka see a change on the monitor.]

WEIR
Wait! Wait! They're taking off!

[Everyone runs to the balcony to witness the flight.]

EXT—ATLANTIS BALCONY, DAY

[They watch the jumper fly away from Atlantis, many overcome by emotion.]

MCKAY
It worked!
WEIR
He's flying it!
ZELENKA
(tearfully)
He says something in Czech.
MCKAY
(tearfully)
Fly, Lucius. Fly.

EXT—ATLANTIS OCEAN

[The jumper flies over the ocean.]

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

LUCIUS
Where are you taking me?
SHEPPARD
A little vacation. Just enough time to let Doctor Beckett give the serum to the rest of my people without you interfering.
LUCIUS
(defeated)
And then what?
SHEPPARD
Then maybe I'll take you back home. I'm sure your people will be thrilled to see you again.
LUCIUS
(hopeful)
You would do that for me? Without even being under the effect of the herb? After all I did?
SHEPPARD
Well, I'm a nice guy.
LUCIUS
Oh Colonel, I can't thank you enough. Thank you so much—
SHEPPARD
After I give them the serum.
LUCIUS
Oh.

[Lucius's face falls.]

EXT—ATLANTIS, DAY

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[The wormhole shuts down as Sheppard's team finishes mounting the steps to the control room. Weir and Beckett meet them.]

WEIR
So, how was Lucius's homecoming?
SHEPPARD
Well…they didn't kill him, although I do see a string of divorces in his future.
RONON
That's if they haven't strung him up first.
MCKAY
Well, the townspeople are over their withdrawal and on the mend, so I doubt that will happen.
TEYLA
That is, unless we discover he's revealed the location of Atlantis to anyone.

[She looks to Ronon.]

RONON
In which case, I said I would track him down, hang him by his feet, and cut off his—
WEIR
(interrupting)
Thank you. We get the idea.

[They exchange smiles.]

SHEPPARD
So, everyone here, uh…back to normal?

[Weir looks to Beckett, who smiles and nods.]

WEIR
We're still fine, John.
SHEPPARD
(teasing dramatically)
No lingering…desires? Secret longings for his touch?

[Weir and Beckett glare at Sheppard.]

BECKETT
It's embarrassing enough without you constantly reminding us, thank you.
SHEPPARD
All right. All right.

[He turns and slaps McKay good-naturedly on the chest.]

SHEPPARD
Hey, buddy. I better get back and clean your quarters before the next scout.

[He rushes away. Looking guilty, McKay quickly turns.]

MCKAY
Right.

[Teyla and Ronon close ranks, blocking McKay's path as they glare at him.]

WEIR
Rodney!
MCKAY
It was a—one teeny-tiny taste, for research purposes.
WEIR
(sternly)
Burn it.
BECKETT
(sternly)
All of it.
WEIR
Right now.
MCKAY
Fine.
(under his breath)
Story of my life.

[He turns to go, this time his teammates let him pass. He gives Teyla a charming smile as he passes. She rolls her eyes in response. ]

FADE OUT

END CREDITS
