O'NEILL
Well, I was planning to retire, but, man, is that overrated. I mean, it's not like I'm a workaholic or anything, but, you know, I like to stay active…with the community. It's-it's a health maintenance sort of thing, you know?

[The replicators hold Woolsey and O'Neill as Talus and Cetus approach.]

O'NEILL
I don't like where this is going.
WOOLSEY
What are they doing?

[Talus and Cetus place their hands into O'Neill's and Woolsey's heads.]

INT—O'NEILL'S SUBCONSCIOUS

[O'Neill and Talus stand on the ramp of the Gate room, an active wormhole beside them.]

O'NEILL
Whatcha doin'?
TALUS
Probing your mind.
O'NEILL
Kinda roomy, ain't it?
TALUS
Your ability to resist is quite remarkable, General O'Neill.
O'NEILL
(smiling)
It's not the first time I've had a hand in my head, as it were.
TALUS
If you do not give me what I am looking for, I am sure Richard Woolsey will.
O'NEILL
I'll tell you what, you look around, and I'll tell you if you're gettin' warmer or colder, alright?

[Talus's expression changes to one of satisfaction.]

TALUS
Ah. There it is.
O'NEILL
(unhappily)
So…hot, I guess.

INT—ATLANTIS BRIG

[They release Woolsey and O'Neill, who cry out in pain.]

WOOLSEY
That was the worst thing I've ever experienced.

[O'Neill puts a hand on Woolsey's shoulder.]

O'NEILL
Yeah. It gets worse.

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

MCKAY
Okay, if I…that should… Yeah. Okay, yes. Stand by.
(into radio)
I've got his power levels about as high as I'm comfortable with. I'm gonna try to get him to …

[McKay suddenly senses something and turns around. Niam stands behind him, fully conscious.]

NIAM
You've been warned about tampering with our base code, Doctor. You won't get a second chance.

[McKay fumbles for the ARG and is able to reach it and fire before Niam can touch him.]

SHEPPARD
(over radio)
McKay? Come in, McKay.
MCKAY
(panicked)
Fall back to the Jumper! We're gonna need a new plan.

EXT—ATLANTIS, NIGHT

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

[The rest of the team have joined McKay in the jumper. Sheppard studies the broken replicator blocks from where Niam disintegrated.]

WEIR
He just came to life?
RONON
They must have got to O'Neill and Woolsey.
SHEPPARD
O'Neill would've never given us away.
TEYLA
Obviously their minds were probed.
SHEPPARD
Well, they know we're here and how we got in.
MCKAY
Which is how they were able to reactivate Niam, which pretty much screws Plan A.
BECKETT
Could you activate the freezing program another way?
MCKAY
No. I need to load the program directly into a Replicator and spread it that way.
WEIR
All right, so that plan's out the window. We need another one.
RONON
Well, these weapons blow them apart, right? I say we just start killing `em.
MCKAY
Well, normally I would share your run-and-gun enthusiasm, but these weapons are only gonna work for so long before they manage to identify the frequency they use to disrupt the bonds that hold the Replicators together.
RONON
What'd he say?
SHEPPARD
They build up an immunity.
MCKAY
Exactly. Look, the freezing plan would have worked because they would be unable to communicate with each other while we were blasting them, but the more we shoot, the greater the likelihood these weapons are gonna become ineffective.
WEIR
Okay, so what are our options?
RONON
I just gave you one.
SHEPPARD
We'll call that…Plan B.
WEIR
Anyone for a Plan C?
MCKAY
(snapping his fingers)
C-4. How much do we have?
SHEPPARD
A bunch. Why?
MCKAY
Well, it's desperate…
WEIR
Well, so are we.
MCKAY
We need to split into groups.
SHEPPARD
I was gonna suggest that anyway. Why? What are you thinking?

INT—ATLANTIS WORKSTATION

CETUS
We far outnumber them. If we conduct a search…
TALUS
We will be able to locate them more effectively once repairs are complete.
CETUS
The damage was severe. We had to dismantle our ship to provide the raw materials.
TALUS
Once repaired, Atlantis will be all the ship we need.

INT—ATLANTIS BRIG

[Woolsey and O'Neill sleep. McKay and Sheppard enter the room, destroy the Replicator guard, and approach the cell.]

O'NEILL
Well, it's about time!
WOOLSEY
Oh, thank God!
SHEPPARD
Alright, get 'em out of here. I'll stand guard.

[Sheppard stands by the entrance to the room. McKay uses his handheld computer and tries to work on the door controls. O'Neill appears very irritated, Woolsey relieved.]

O'NEILL
Hey! "Thanks for the rescue!"
MCKAY
Oh, hey! No problem.
O'NEILL
No! You should be saying: "thanks for the rescue"!

[He points to himself.]

MCKAY
(contrite)
Oh! Uh, right. Thanks. Should have you outta there in no time. This won't take long at all.
(His face falls.)
Oh, my. They've changed the codes.
WOOLSEY
But you can crack it, right? You need to get us out of here.
MCKAY
Yeah, of course I can. Just give me like one…two seconds.
O'NEILL
So I guess that frozen Replicator plan didn't pan out?
MCKAY
Yeah, not-not so much.
O'NEILL
(still irritated)
How's that Plan B working for ya? Good?
MCKAY
Actually, it's more like Plan C. "C" as in C-4, if you catch my drift.
O'NEILL
No.
MCKAY
Let's just say that we're concerned the Daedalus won't be able to carry out your standing orders.
WOOLSEY
What?!? To destroy Atlantis with nuclear weapons? I thought we were past that! Why are we going back to that?
MCKAY
Yeah, it's the only way.
O'NEILL
Can you do that?
MCKAY
Yeah. If we plant charges in all ten of the shield emitters before the Daedalus arrives, that way when the Replicators try to activate the shield…
WOOLSEY
The C-4 detonates, the Daedalus beams a warhead into the city, and we all get vaporized.
O'NEILL
Sounds more like a Plan F, doesn't it? As in: "we are totally f—
MCKAY
(interrupting)
If we can fight our way back to the bay, then we'll be able to fly the jumper to a safe distance. That is, if we can get the underwater bay door open.
O'NEILL
You can't even open this door!
MCKAY
(defensively)
Hey, I'm workin' in it!
O'NEILL
Yes. I see that.
SHEPPARD
We got company.
(to O'Neill)
Sorry, sir, we're gonna have to come back for you.
MCKAY
No-no-no-no-no-no, we can't leave them here. They know too much.
SHEPPARD
What'd you tell `em?
MCKAY
Uh, uh, I mean, they guessed most of it.

[Sheppard smacks McKay in the back of the head.]

SHEPPARD
What, and you filled in the rest?!
MCKAY
I thought I would have time to get them out of here!
(to O'Neill)
Look, just forget what I said.

[O'Neill has been watching this exchange shrewdly.]

O'NEILL
Sheppard, get out of here while you still can. That's an order.
SHEPPARD
Sorry it has to be this way.
O'NEILL
Yeah, I get it. Go.
WOOLSEY
(resigned)
So, we're back to being vaporized.

EXT—CHEYENNE MOUNTAIN, DAY

INT—SGC CONTROL ROOM

[The Stargate is active. Landry enters the room and stands by Harriman's station.]

LANDRY
What is it, Chief?
HARRIMAN
We're receiving a subspace data burst via midway in the Alpha Site. Daedalus is holding station at their failsafe point and awaiting your order.
LANDRY
Reply to Colonel Caldwell that General O'Neill's order stands. Atlantis is to be destroyed at all costs.
HARRIMAN
Yes, sir.

EXT—ATLANTIS, DAWN

INT—ATLANTIS SHIELD EMITTER

BECKETT
Done … and done.
TEYLA
Good. We are well ahead of schedule.
BECKETT
Perfect. Now let's find some place out of the way and wait for this thing to be over.

INT—ANOTHER ATLANTIS SHIELD EMITTER

RONON
Come on. One more to go.
WEIR
Almost done. We haven't run into a single Replicator.
RONON
I know. It's weird.
WEIR
Woolsey and O'Neill said that there are hundreds of them. Where are they?
RONON
Let's go.

INT—ANOTHER ATLANTIS SHIELD EMITTER

MCKAY
Okay, that should do it.
SHEPPARD
One more to go…
MCKAY
…And it's all over but the crying.
SHEPPARD
Hopefully them, not us.

[There is a sharp noise and then a humming.]

MCKAY
What the hell?

[He starts looking at the readout screens.]

MCKAY
No wonder they weren't coming after us. They've been busy repairing the city.
SHEPPARD
What is that?
MCKAY
They brought their ZPMs to power the stardrive.
SHEPPARD
So that sound is..?
MCKAY
The city about to take off.

EXT—ATLANTIS, DAWN

[The city starts to rise.]

INT—ANOTHER ATLANTIS SHIELD EMITTER

MCKAY
Time to start crying.
SHEPPARD
They'll still have to activate the shield to take off, won't they? We got most of their emitters.
MCKAY
Yeah, "most" ain't gonna cut it. For this plan to work, it is all or nothing.
SHEPPARD
What do they want to fly around for?
MCKAY
Well, maybe they wanna fly home, back to their homeworld.
SHEPPARD
And take on the Daedalus. Well, how are we gonna stop 'em?
MCKAY
Well, I…
SHEPPARD
Drones. They fired drones at us. That means the Chair is active and powered.
MCKAY
You wanna fire drones at the city? We're nowhere near the Chair!
SHEPPARD
But Teyla and Beckett are.

[He reaches for his radio.]

INT—ANOTHER ATLANTIS SHIELD EMITTER

[The scene switches between Teyla/Beckett and SHeppard/McKay.]

BECKETT
(into radio)
You want me to do what?!
SHEPPARD
(into radio)
Just do what I say.
BECKETT
All right, I'll give it a try. But no apologies if I happen to destroy the entire bloody city.
SHEPPARD
Stardrive, Carson. Concentrate on the stardrive.
BECKETT
(sighing)
Right.
SHEPPARD
And, Teyla, you may have to fight your way in.
TEYLA
I'm ready, Colonel.
SHEPPARD
Use your ARG sparingly. They may build up an immunity. You may have to go to Plan … B.
BECKETT
Okay. Which one's that, again?

[McKay rolls his eyes.]

SHEPPARD
(into radio)
Never mind. Just go.

INT—ATLANTIS CHAIR ROOM

[Teyla enters and destroys the four guards there with two ARGs.]

TEYLA
Clear.

[Beckett enters and approaches the chair.]

BECKETT
Thank you, dear.
TEYLA
Hurry.

[He sits in the chair and activates it.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[The hole in the window is still present where the Puddle Jumper escaped, but another window has appeared behind it. Everything else looks fine. The Replicators return to the controls.]

CETUS
They've taken out the guards in the Chair Room.

[They notice a display lighting.]

TALUS
What are they doing?

INT—CHAIR ROOM

[Beckett scrunches his face in concentration.]

EXT—ATLANTIS, DAWN

[Drones come out through a tube in the city. The three drones rise and then fall.]

INT—CHAIR ROOM

[Beckett concentrates.]

EXT—ATLANTIS, DAWN

[The drones go underwater and head underneath the city.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[The room rocks from an explosion. Talus indicates some Replicators should go respond.]

CETUS
They've hit the stardrive.
TALUS
A search and destroy team is on their way to the Chair Room now.
CETUS
Make sure they understand we need them alive.

[Telus leaves.]

INT—CHAIR ROOM

TEYLA
Beckett!

[Beckett fumbles to get out the chair and raise his sidearm, but by the team he positions himself, Teyla has been stunned, and Beckett is outnumbered. He slowly lowers his sidearm and kneels in surrender.]

BECKETT
Okay, lads, easy. Easy.

[Replicators grab Beckett and manhandle him and Teyla out of the room.]

INT—ATLANTIS SHIELD EMITTER

[Ronon and Weir finish as Replicators arrive. One grabs Weir as Ronon loses a fight with two others.]

INT—ANOTHER ATLANTIS SHIELD EMITTER

[Sheppard and McKay finish up and are instantly surrounded by Replicators.]

SHEPPARD
And I was so sure we were gonna survive this one.
MCKAY
Me too.

[They surrender and are led away.]

INT—ATLANTIS BRIG

[McKay and Sheppard are thrown into a cell where the rest of the rescue team are.]

REPLICATOR
Bring the prisoners from the other cell.

[Some Replicators leave.]

SHEPPARD
Everyone okay?
WEIR
So far.
BECKETT
I'll be fine.
RONON
Why aren't we dead yet?
SHEPPARD
Probably saving us for a little recreational mind-probing. Did everyone get the…?
TEYLA
(quickly speaking)
We completed our task.
WEIR
So did we. In the nick of time.
RONON
(to Sheppard)
You?
MCKAY
Yeah. Just under the wire.

[Woolsey and O'Neill are led into the cell. Talus stands at the doorway.]

TALUS
Did you really think you'd be able to stop us?
SHEPPARD
Well, of course we did. Why else would we be here?
O'NEILL
Colonel? Am I gonna have to fire you?
SHEPPARD
No, sir. I think you'll have that to look forward to when we get back.
TALUS
I'm afraid your plan has been discovered, Colonel Sheppard. It is over.

[Talus glances at Woolsey, and everyone stares at him.]

WOOLSEY
(protesting)
He put his hand in my forehead. How can you resist that?
O'NEILL
Well, I like to close my eyes and think of England.
TALUS
Mr. Woolsey informed us of everything we need to know. All of your C-4 has been removed from the emitters. I'm afraid they won't be destroyed when the Daedalus arrives…which should be in any moment. Your plan has failed.

[Another Replicator holds out the C-4 they've collected. Weir looks disappointed.]

CETUS
(over intercom)
Talus. The Daedalus is approaching the planet.
TALUS
(over intercom)
As expected. Activate the shield.

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[Cetus presses the button.]

REWIND EFFECT TO EARLIER

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER, EARLIER

MCKAY
(snapping his fingers)
C-4. How much do we have?
SHEPPARD
A bunch. Why?
MCKAY
Well, it's desperate…
WEIR
Well, so are we.
MCKAY
We need to split into groups.
SHEPPARD
I was gonna suggest that anyway. Why? What are you thinking?
MCKAY
If we're gonna destroy these guys using Replicator disruptors, we have to hit them all at once. We'll probably only get a dozen or so shots off before they manage to figure out the frequency.
SHEPPARD
So, what, you gonna get 'em all to stand in one place at the same time?
MCKAY
Clever, yes. Or we could figure out a way to send one massive blast through the city. Look, follow me.

INT—ATLANTIS SHIELD EMITTER, EARLIER

[McKay is showing the inside of an emitter to the others, demonstrating while he speaks.]

MCKAY
Atlantis has ten of these emitters with which it creates the city's shields. So, if we can interface the crystals from the disrupter weapons with those of the shield emitters, we should be able to trigger a massive anti-Replicator wave throughout the city.
SHEPPARD
Okay, that's where splitting into teams comes in.
MCKAY
Exactly. Now, I need to show you how to do this, so listen carefully. You slide out the crystal tray, you take the crystal from the Replicator weapon, you place it in the third slot from the right. Third symbol from the left activates the crystal tray. Clear on that? Great.

[While McKay speaks, he replaces the crystal and activates the tray.]

[The team starts walking away from the emitter.]

TEYLA
They are bound to find one of our groups working on the shield emitters.
MCKAY
I already thought of that. That's what the C-4's for.
RONON
We blow them up?
MCKAY
No. We feed them misinformation.
SHEPPARD
All right.
(holding up an ARG)
Once you guys have made all your alterations, avoid using your shiny new toys even if that means getting caught, okay?

INT—ATLANTIS BRIG, EARLIER

MCKAY
If we plant charges in all ten of the shield emitters before the Daedalus arrives, that way when the Replicators try to activate the shield…
WOOLSEY
(realizing)
The C-4 detonates, the Daedalus beams a warhead into the city, and we all get vaporized.
O'NEILL
That sounds more like a Plan F, doesn't it?

INT—CORRIDOR OUTSIDE ATLANTIS BRIG, EARLIER

[Sheppard and McKay move away from the brig.]

SHEPPARD
General O'Neill didn't buy it, but I think Woolsey did.
MCKAY
Well, I did win a Sears drama festival award when I was a kid. Could have made it a career if I wanted to.
SHEPPARD
Wish you would've.
MCKAY
Hmm?

INT—ATLANTIS BRIG, EARLIER

[Cetus and Talus again probe Woolsey's and O'Neill's minds.]

CETUS
We need to get to the shield emitter stations. Remove the C-4 explosive you find there.
O'NEILL
Way to resist.
WOOLSEY
I said I wasn't good at this!

INT—ATLANTIS BRIG, PRESENT

CETUS
(over comm)
Talus, the Daedalus is approaching the planet.
TALUS
As expected. Activate the shield.

INT—ATLANTIS OPERATIONS CENTER

[Cetus activates the shield.]

EXT—ATLANTIS, DAY

[The shield raises, and also sends an anti-replicator wave flowing down through the levels of the city. Everywhere replicators disintegrate, including those in the brig.]

INT—ATLANTIS BRIG

MCKAY
Ha! It worked! I can't believe it worked!
WEIR
(proudly)
Rodney, amazing.
MCKAY
Well, it was-it was a group effort.
SHEPPARD
Good old Plan D. Works every time. You guys stay here a sec.

[Sheppard and Ronon leave to investigate.]

WOOLSEY
Excuse me, but what just happened?
MCKAY
We turned the shield into a giant Replicator weapon.
O'NEILL
I knew that!
WOOLSEY
You knew?
O'NEILL
Well, I've seen Carter crack enough codes to know that McKay was faking the door thing.
WOOLSEY
He was? I bought it completely.
TEYLA
I believe that was the point.
WOOLSEY
Wait a minute, you-you used me?
BECKETT
You're alive, and you're welcome.

[Ronon reenters the room.]

RONON
Hey, we're clear.
WEIR
Okay, we've gotta get to the Control Room and contact the Daedalus.

[The start leaving.]

O'NEILL
You see, Richard? You were good for something.
WOOLSEY
(mildly comforted)
Thanks.

EXT—SPACE

[The Daedalus moves through hyperspace.]

INT—DAEDALUS BRIDGE

KLEINMAN
Sir. I have an incoming message from Atlantis.
CALDWELL
Atlantis? Is it Replicators?
KLEINMAN
No, sir. It has Colonel Sheppard's authentication code.
CALDWELL
Open a channel.
(into comm)
Colonel, I'd like to believe this is you.
SHEPPARD
Well, it is. Authentication code Alpha Delta Charlie Niner Six.
CALDWELL
That code is no longer valid, Colonel.

INT—ATLANTIS STARGATE OPERATIONS CENTER

O'NEILL
Hey, Caldwell. General Jack O'Neill here. That valid enough for you?

INT—DAEDALUS BRIDGE

CALDWELL
You may have been compromised, sir.

INT—ATLANTIS STARGATE OPERATIONS CENTER

WEIR
We're lowering the city's shields, Steven. You can send a team to come check us out if you need to. We'll explain everything.

INT—DAEDALUS BRIDGE

CALDWELL
Get a team of marines ready to beam down.
KLEINMAN
Sir.
CALDWELL
Should be an interesting explanation.

INT—WEIR'S OFFICE

[Weir looks around the empty room. O'Neill enters.]

O'NEILL
Getting all settled in?
WEIR
Easily. Although their decorating..? Little bit spartan for my liking. But the Replicators did do a fine job on rebuilding Stargate Operations back to its original state. How about yourself? Do you check out okay?
O'NEILL
Apparently I don't have any little tiny robots floating in my bloodstream, which is always nice to hear.

[O'Neill and Weir share commiserating looks.]

WEIR
Huh! That it is. So what now?
O'NEILL
Oh, a jumper ride home to the SGC, followed by a hot shower, followed by a hot toddy, followed by…
WEIR
I was thinking a…little more selfishly. What are the chances of my team staying here in Atlantis?

[O'Neill sighs.]

O'NEILL
Oh. Elizabeth, if it were up to me, I'd—
WEIR
It is up to you.
O'NEILL
Right.
(after a pause)
You can stay.
WEIR
Really?
O'NEILL
You're probably still gonna have to get the rubber stamp from the IOA, but since you saved Woolsey's ass and you did a fair job on mine, I don't think that'll be a problem.
WEIR
Well, I think I speak for all of us when I say thank you very much.
O'NEILL
Hey, you came back to rescue me.
WEIR
Hmm! Yes, we did.
O'NEILL
Of course, next time, consider bringing Carter with you.

[She smiles.]

O'NEILL
Welcome home.

[Weir is overcome with emotion and hugs O'Neill. O'Neill is taken aback at first, then hesitantly returns the hug.]

EXT—ATLANTIS, DUSK

FADE OUT

END CREDITS
