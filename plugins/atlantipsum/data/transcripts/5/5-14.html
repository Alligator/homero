SGA 5.14 "The Prodigal" Episode Guide

Précis

Michael uses his stolen puddle jumper to infiltrate Atlantis and take it over from the gate room. He is driven to take Teyla's son, and Teyla as well if she's willing. He intends to destroy Atlantis in vengeance for what was done to him. Sheppard and the Lanteans must stop Michael once and for all.

(contrite)
It was the next thing on my list.
SHEPPARD
You said you were going to fix them!
MCKAY
I know, and I was getting to it, I swear.
SHEPPARD
(angrily)
And you had two years!
MCKAY
I was busy!
ZELENKA
(soothingly)
Perhaps we could go down there and see if we can fix one of them.
SHEPPARD
That's a good idea.
(to Zelenka)
But I want you to stay here to try to override Michael's lockouts.
MCKAY
Well, why don't I stay here and work on that? I mean, Zelenka can go with you to the jumper bay.
SHEPPARD
Because I want you to come with me and fix those jumpers like you said you were going to do two years ago. Let's go.
MCKAY
(resigned, to Zelenka)
Please tell me there's a transporter?
ZELENKA
Yes. Well, there's not one by the puddle jumper bay. But there's one two levels down.
SHEPPARD
That'll do.

[They leave. Zelenka smiles smugly.]

INT—ATLANTIS LOUNGE, CONTROL TOWER

[Banks gets the door open. She smiles at Ronon]

RONON
Nice.

[A hybrid standing guard outside enters. Ronon throws him against the wall, knocking his weapon out of his hand, but the hybrid fights back. The hybrid tosses Ronon to the floor in the corridor. He turns into a kick by Banks, who knocks him to his knees with a series of punches and kicks. When he stands again, she looks worried, but while she's been fighting, Ronon has skidded across the hall to the fallen weapon. He fires and stuns the hybrid.]

RONON
(surprised, breathless)
You can fight.
BANKS
(breathless)
Five years of kick-boxing.
RONON
(impressed)
That's good to know.
WOOLSEY
(from in room)
What happened?

INT—ATLANTIS CORRIDOR OUTSIDE GATE ROOM

[Ronon moves from pillar to pillar, performing reconnaissance. He spots several men moving about, including Michael. He heads back.]

INT—ATLANTIS LOUNGE, CONTROL TOWER

[A staff person and Banks pull the unconscious guard into the room. Others have awakened as Ronon returns.]

RONON
It's Michael and his hybrids. I counted three of them. I'm not sure how many more there are. Probably not too many. They all had to fit in that jumper.
WOOLSEY
Unless more came through on foot afterward.

[Ronon shrugs.]

BANKS
What do we do?
WOOLSEY
Try to get to the armory, come back, and retake the Gate Room.
RONON
Sounds like a plan. You two come with us. Banks, stay here with them. Keep everyone calm. And uh, if any more hybrids show up, just…do what you did earlier.

[They exchange smiles as Ronon, Woolsey and two of the burlier personnel go with them.]

INT—ATLANTIS CORRIDOR

[Ronon and the others reach a door. Ronon is about to try to open them when he hears voices.]

LEAD HYBRID
(in distance)
…speak with you again. It's urgent.
RONON
(low, to guards)
Stay with him.

[Ronon pulls his stunner and moves forward. From below, he spots Michael and the lead hybrid speaking on the catwalk to Woolsey's office again.]

MICHAEL
What is it?
LEAD HYBRID
We were finally able to decipher the encryption on the self-destruct device.
MICHAEL
Good. Prepare the ship to leave. We'll arm the self destruct and switch the power back to Atlantis's systems a split second before we leave. The timing of the switch must be precise. I don't want to give them any opportunity to disarm the device.
LEAD HYBRID
I will make the calculations.

[They part ways. Ronon heads back to the others.]

RONON
Change of plans. We're not going to the armory.
WOOLSEY
Why? What happened?
RONON
They armed the self-destruct. Mr. Woolsey, would you please head back to the others?

[Ronon and the two men continue while Woolsey pauses, then heads another way.]

INT—WOOLSEY'S OFFICE

[Teyla watches for Michael, then reaches over and grabs the headset.]

TEYLA
(into headset)
Colonel Sheppard, this is Teyla, can you hear me? John, this is Teyla, please respond.

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[Ronon and the two Atlantis personnel storm the control room, engaging in a firefight. The unarmed men are stunned but Ronon is able to fight his way to Michael.]

INT—WOOLSEY'S OFFICE

[Teyla hears something in the control room and looks over, curious.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[Ronon and Michael continue to fight hand-to-hand.]

INT—MCKAY'S LAB

[Zelenka spots the fight going on via the monitor.]

ZELENKA
(into headset)
Colonel Shep—
(he realizes his mistake)
Uh…
(into walkie-talkie)
Colonel Sheppard…Ronon is in the control room. He's fighting with Michael!

INT—ATLANTIS CORRIDOR

SHEPPARD
We're almost at the jumper bay. Keep us posted.

[Sheppard and McKay break into a jog.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[Ronon and Michael continue to fight, yelling and groaning in pain and rage. Teyla heads across the catwalk. Ronon spots Teyla standing there during a momentary pause in the battle.]

RONON
Teyla, run!

[They continue to fight, Michael blocking a few of Ronon's blows and able to land some of his own. Teyla runs with her child out of the room.]

INT—ATLANTIS CORRIDOR

[Teyla runs down the hall with Torren. Woolsey spots her passing from where he's hiding in a side corridor.]

WOOLSEY
Teyla! Are you all right?

[She stops.]

TEYLA
Yes. But Ronon needs our help. We need to get back up to the control room.

[A hybrid appears at the end of the hall, shooting his stunner at the pair.]

TEYLA
Run!

[Teyla and Woolsey run off. The hybrid chases them.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM AND GATE ROOM

[Ronon and Michael's fight has moved to the control room balcony. One of Michael's blows ends up throwing Ronon over the rail. He falls several feet onto the Gate Room floor, where he ends up at the bottom of the steps.]

INT—UNDERWATER PUDDLE JUMPER BAY

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

SHEPPARD
(low, into walkie talkie)
Radek? Tell us what's going on up there.

INT—MCKAY'S LAB

[Zelenka studies the surveillance cameras, looking at Ronon's unmoving form sprawled at the bottom of the steps.]

ZELENKA
I—I can't tell. Ronon is down. He might just be unconscious.

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

SHEPPARD
(into walkie-talkie)
Tell me when he comes to.
(to McKay)
What have we got?
MCKAY
It's like I said. I mean, both the jumpers are toast. This one's got a whole whack of problems starting with zero power.
SHEPPARD
All right, you said the other jumper's got power, right?
MCKAY
Yeah, but not much else. I mean, the drive pods won't extend. There's no drones.
SHEPPARD
And this one's got drones. So, if we bring power over to this, we can get the drive pods working, right?
MCKAY
(dubious)
Okay, you want to harvest parts from both jumpers, cobble together a fix, so one of them will work?
SHEPPARD
(insistent)
Is it possible?
MCKAY
Doubtful.
SHEPPARD
But maybe.
MCKAY
It's a long shot at best!
SHEPPARD
Perfect, get to it. Whaddya need? Fifteen minutes?

[Sheppard walks out of the jumper. McKay grimaces and sighs, reaching under the jumper console. His computer cables get caught and they tumble to the floor around him.]

MCKAY
(muttering to self)
Great. Just great.

INT—ATLANTIS CORRIDOR

[Woolsey and Teyla, carrying Torren, run through the corridor. Woolsey tries a transporter door.]

WOOLSEY
Must've locked out the transporters.
TEYLA
We need to get to the control room.
WOOLSEY
(shaking his head)
It's too dangerous. Not without weapons. Where's the nearest stairwell?
TEYLA
Around the corner. This way.

[She starts to move.]

WOOLSEY
Let me lead.

[Woolsey leads the way down the corridor and down the stairs. He starts down the steps. As he lands on one, he hits the stun bubble. He falls to the bottom of the steps. Panicking, Teyla runs back the way she came, but hears the hybrid approaching. She backs away. The hybrid arrives, standing at the top of the steps, looking down at Woolsey. As he turns, Teyla kicks the hybrid down the steps where he too is stunned as he falls through the field.]

[Teyla runs, checking the wall panels until she finds an access panel. She hurriedly opens it and lowers the ladder, placing herself and Torren inside. She tries quickly and quietly to close the doors behind her as another hybrid approaches the area. She does so just in time as he patrols.]

[Inside the panel, Torren gives a weak cry. The hybrid hears and pauses. Hearing nothing further and unable to pinpoint the source, it moves away.]

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

[McKay works in the back of the jumper, with wires hanging down and using his hand-held. Sheppard sits at the pilot's chair.]

MCKAY
Okay, give 'er…

[The system powers up.]

SHEPPARD
(pumping his fist)
Drive pods are go!
MCKAY
(pleased)
All right, now we're getting somewhere. I got juice, I got life support, I got…oh come on!
SHEPPARD
What?
MCKAY
(exasperated)
It's the weapons won't come online.
SHEPPARD
Why not?
MCKAY
I don't know!
SHEPPARD
Well, figure it out! We need those drones.

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[Michael works at a console. The hybrid group returns to the room.]

MICHAEL
Where is she?
HYBRID
We don't know. We searched everywhere.
MICHAEL
(angry)
Are you telling me she just disappeared?
HYBRID
I'm saying that we searched every room and corridor within the stun field. We couldn't find her anywhere.
MICHAEL
She knows this city well. She's hiding somewhere. She's toying with me!

[He slams a computer across the room, shattering it.]

HYBRID
We'll keep looking.
MICHAEL
No, I'll handle this!
HYBRID
What are you going to do?
MICHAEL
Flush her out.

[He starts typing on a console. The self-destruct klaxon begins to blare.]

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER, JUMPER BAY

[The self destruct klaxon can be heard.]

MCKAY
(worried)
That's Atlantis's self destruct. Michael's going to blow up the city!
SHEPPARD
How much time do we have?
MCKAY
(checking his computer)
He's set it for ten minutes.
SHEPPARD
How much time do you need before you get those drones working?
MCKAY
Well, if I can get them working at all, a lot more than ten minutes!
SHEPPARD
All right, forget about it.

[He turns back to his controls.]

MCKAY
What are you…what are you doing?
SHEPPARD
I'm gonna fly the jumper. I'm crashing it into the tower.
MCKAY
Crash it?!
SHEPPARD
Look, it's our best chance at taking out Michael's jumper.
MCKAY
Not to mention taking out yourself!
SHEPPARD
I have crashed jumpers into the tower before.
MCKAY
Not like this, you haven't! Look, in addition to the weapons, the inertial dampeners are out. You'll need to impact Michael's jumper with enough force to do some serious damage, which means a lot of speed when you hit that tower. Look, this is a suicide mission.

[Sheppard looks troubled.]

INT—ACCESS PANEL

[Teyla continues to soothe her baby while Michael's voice carries over the intercom.]

MICHAEL
(over intercom)
Teyla! I know you can hear me. That alarm, if you're not aware, is Atlantis's self destruct device. I've armed it—set it for ten minutes. That is the amount of time you have to consider the offer I am about to make. If you will surrender yourself and your child to me, I'll disarm the device, sparing the lives of everyone on this base. If not…you, your baby, and everyone else…will die.

[Teyla looks very frightened.]

INT—JUMPER BAY

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

[McKay makes some final adjustments.]

MCKAY
Okay, you're good to go.
SHEPPARD
Make sure that strike team's ready to move as soon as that field drops.
MCKAY
I will. You make sure you don't accelerate too fast coming out of the jumper bay. I mean, if you hit the gas too hard, you're going to…flatten like a pancake.
SHEPPARD
Right.

[McKay looks upset. Sheppard glances over and notices his expression.]

SHEPPARD
What?
MCKAY
Well, it's just…I'm…what you're about to do is…
SHEPPARD
(with false levity)
Yeah. It's not like it's the first time. How many suicide missions have I flown?
MCKAY
I don't know. I lost count.
SHEPPARD
All right, well, there you go…
MCKAY
Well right, well, you know…here's to many more.

[McKay stands and puts out his hand. They exchange looks and Sheppard takes his hand.]

ZELENKA
(over walkie-talkie)
Rodney?

[McKay sighs, then pulls out his walkie-talkie from his pocket.]

MCKAY
(into walkie)
Yeah, Radek, go ahead?
ZELENKA
(over walkie)
I've finally figured out how to override some of Michael's lockout codes.
MCKAY
(into radio, excited)
That's great. Can you shut down the self-destruct?

INT—MCKAY'S LAB

ZELENKA
No, no, no. That will take a little bit more time. I have however, managed to get the access doors to the Gate Room open.

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

[The conversation continues to switch between the lab and the jumper.]

MCKAY
Well, that does us absolutely no good. I mean, th-the stun field is still in effect.
SHEPPARD
What about the transporter on the Gate Room level?
ZELENKA
Uh…no, no. I-I still don't have control of that either.
MCKAY
For crying out loud!
ZELENKA
I'm sorry. I'm doing the best I can. Michael has placed several highly encrypted codes—
MCKAY
Look, just—what about the gate shield? Can you lower it?
ZELENKA
(checking)
Yes. That, I can do.
MCKAY
Great.
(to Sheppard, excited)
You don't have to do your kamikaze run.
SHEPPARD
(surprised)
Why not?
MCKAY
Well, you just have to fly up to the tower, get in proximity, dial the DHD to a random planet. We'll have Radek lower the gate shield, allowing the vortex to obliterate Michael's puddle jumper…or most of it.
SHEPPARD
Now, that's a good plan.

[Sheppard starts to leave.]

MCKAY
Where are you going?
SHEPPARD
You're gonna fly it. That frees me up to lead the strike team.

[McKay stammers unintelligibly.]

SHEPPARD
You said it yourself; it's simple enough. You just dial the 'gate.
MCKAY
(dubious)
I guess I can do that.
SHEPPARD
Go easy on the accelerator.
(into walkie talkie)
Radek. get ready to drop that shield on my mark.

[Sheppard runs out.]

MCKAY
Right…

[McKay heads to the pilot's chair.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

LEAD HYBRID
We don't have much time. We need to leave this place.
MICHAEL
(into intercom)
Time is short, Teyla. I don't understand…I would think the choice is an easy one. If nothing else, consider your child. By remaining in hiding, you are sentencing him to die. What kind of mother would do such a thing? He has the chance to live. Both of you do. Come with me, Teyla. Trust me…I will not harm either of you, and I will not harm Atlantis. You have my word.

INT—ACCESS PANEL

[In the access panel, Teyla is upset, listening to Michael's offer. These last words seem to give her an idea.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

TEYLA
(over radio)
Turn off the self-destruct system.
MICHAEL
Teyla!

INT—ACCESS PANEL

TEYLA
(into headset)
You want me to trust you? Then show me you mean what you say. Turn off the self destruct system, and I will come with you.

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL PANEL

[Michael sighs.]

INT—ATLANTIS STAIRWELL

[Sheppard's strike force heads up the stairwell.]

EXT—ATLANTIS OCEAN, NIGHT

[McKay's jumper flies up from underwater and slowly approaches the city.]

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

MCKAY
(into walkie talkie)
All right, I'm in the air, approaching the central tower.

INT—ATLANTIS STAIRWELL

[The strike force pauses.]

SHEPPARD
(to McKay over walkie)
Copy that.
(to Zelenka, over walkie)
Zelenka, you ready?

INT—MCKAY'S LAB

ZELENKA
(into walkie)
Ready when you are.

INT—ACCESS PANEL

TEYLA
(into headset)
Michael, can you hear me?

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

LEAD HYBRID
(concerned)
Less than two minutes. We need to go, now.

[Michael ignores his minion.]

MICHAEL
(into intercom)
Yes, Teyla. I hear you. You disappoint me. So I will leave you to the fate you have chosen. But I'm not leaving alone. You should know that I've already collected your son's DNA. Cloning him will require additional time, delay the implementation of my plan. But as you well know, I will not be deterred. Goodbye, Teyla.

[He starts to leave, with his hybrids following.]

INT—ATLANTIS GATE ROOM

[As the hybrids head to the jumper, Michael pauses next to Ronon.]

LEAD HYBRID
What are you doing?
MICHAEL
Collecting one last prize.

[He bends over Ronon, wielding a knife.]

EXT—ATLANTIS TOWER

[The jumper hovers near the Gate Room.]

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

MCKAY
I'm in position, ready to dial.

INT—ATLANTIS STAIRWELL

SHEPPARD
(into walkie-talkie)
All right, Radek, drop the shield.

INT—MCKAY'S LAB

[Zelenka types on his computer.]

INT—ATLANTIS GATE ROOM

[Michael leans down to cut Ronon's throat. He hears a noise behind him as the Stargate's shield lowers.]

INT—MCKAY'S LAB

ZELENKA
(into walkie-talkie)
The shield's down. Go, Rodney!

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

[McKay dials the jumper's DHD.]

INT—ATLANTIS GATE ROOM

[The Stargate begins to dial.]

MICHAEL
No!

[The Stargate activates, the kawhoosh obliterating 2/3 of the jumper, including the hybrids inside.]

[A door opens and Sheppard's strike force rushes inside, exchanging fire with the remaining hybrids. Overwhelmed, the hybrids begin to fall.]

INT—ATLANTIS CORRIDOR

[Teyla scrambles out of the access panel and runs, still carrying Torren.]

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

ZELENKA
(over walkie talkie)
The stun field is neutralized.
MCKAY
(into walkie talkie)
What about the self-destruct? Is it disabled?

[The conversation switches between McKay's and Zelenka's locations.]

ZELENKA
Yes, yes. It shut off when the jumper's power was cut.
MCKAY
Good. I'm heading back. Go ahead and reboot the city's power.
ZELENKA
Yes. I'm already doing it.

EXT—ATLANTIS, NIGHT

[The lights begin to come back on in the city.]

INT—GATE ROOM

[Power is restored. Sheppard's team moves cautiously. One man is stunned. Sheppard spots Michael in the control room. Michael tries to stun him, but Sheppard has taken cover behind the jumper wreckage. He returns fire, wounding Michael in the shoulder. Michael runs away.]

[Sheppard pauses as he gets to Ronon.]

SHEPPARD
Get a medic!

[Sheppard continues after Michael, who runs towards the jumper bay.]

SHEPPARD
(into walkie-talkie)
Close the jumper bay doors, Radek!

INT—ATLANTIS LOUNGE

[Teyla runs to where the control room crew have been waiting.]

TEYLA
Amelia! Will you take care of Torren for me?
BANKS
Of course.

[Banks takes the child from Teyla's arms.]

TEYLA
Make sure no one leaves.

[Teyla rushes out.]

INT—JUMPER BAY

[Michael runs into the jumper bay, searching for a means of escape. He sees the bay doors of the ceiling closing. Sheppard enters and fires, but misses. Michael runs up to the top of the tower via a very long and shallow spiral staircase.]

EXT—ATLANTIS TOWER, NIGHT

[Michael runs out to a small balcony surrounding the top of the tower. He sees no way out except the door he's just come through. Michael leans into the shadows as Sheppard appears in the doorway, P-90 at ready. Michael ambushes him, taking his gun and knocking him off the walkway.]

[Sheppard struggles, falling to a lower awning and able to grab hold of a buttress before falling. He struggles and pulls himself back up to the lower awning. Michael jumps down to join him. They exchange blows. Michael gains the upper hand, knocking Sheppard on his back where Michael tries to choke him. Sheppard grabs at Michael's wounded shoulder, making Michael lose his grip. Sheppard throws Michael, but he again gains his balance and gets the upper hand. He knocks Sheppard against the metal buttress, stunning him and knocking him against the tower in a semi-consciousness fugue. Teyla joins the battle, catching Michael by surprise. She lands several blows, and Sheppard is able to strike back once as well. Their combined attacks knock Michael off the awning, but he is able to grab hold with both hands to the edge.]

[Teyla looks down at Michael, studying him for several moments. She kicks Michael's hand, making him lose his grip.]

MICHAEL
Ah! Teyla!

[Sheppard stares in surprise at Teyla but does nothing as Michael continues to hang by one hand. Teyla stares coldly down at Michael for several more moments, then kicks his other hand. Michael falls into the depths of the city. Teyla watches for a long moment as Sheppard sits behind her, stunned.]

EXT—ATLANTIS, DAY

INT—ATLANTIS INFIRMARY

[Ronon lies on top of the covers and shifts restlessly on a bed. He is dressed in scrubs. Woolsey, wearing a sling on his right arm, approaches.]

WOOLSEY
How's Dr. Keller's most cantankerous patient?
RONON
You gotta be kidding me. You get released, and I gotta stay here another day?
WOOLSEY
Because I only fell down a flight of stairs. You, my friend, were hurled from a much greater height. You're lucky you only suffered a concussion and didn't break your neck.
RONON
(dismissive)
It's nothing. I'm fine.
WOOLSEY
I know you are, but listen to the doctor. Anyway, I just wanted to drop by and commend you for your exemplary work. Your courage in the face of danger continues to amaze, and…dare I say, inspire me.
RONON
(uncomfortable)
Thanks.
WOOLSEY
Get some rest. And don't worry, your team's not going anywhere without you.

[Woolsey begins to walk away.]

RONON
That reminds me…

[Woolsey returns to his side.]

RONON
I know Michael attacking the city isn't really a mission, but, uh…I filed a report anyway.
WOOLSEY
(startled)
Oh. Well. Splendid. Thank you.

[He takes the dictaphone from Ronon and heads out of the infirmary, clicking play.]

RONON
(on tape)
Mission report….Michael invaded Atlantis—tried to blow it up. We stopped him. End of report.

[Woolsey shakes his head with a rueful chuckle.]

INT—ATLANTIS CORRIDOR

[Sheppard and McKay renew their car race, setting up at the end of the corridor. Sheppard has a wound on his right cheek from the fight with Michael.]

MCKAY
Take a good, long look. 'Cause soon you're gonna see nothing but tail lights.
SHEPPARD
Nice try, Speed Racer.

[They start their cars. McKay's takes an early lead.]

MCKAY
Ha ha ha!

[At the far end, Teyla enters from a side corridor, carrying Torren. She smiles expectantly at seeing the toys. Sheppard and McKay quickly veer their cars and stop them, then jog over to her.]

MCKAY
Whoa. I'm sorry. Sorry, we were just…you know.
TEYLA
(smiling)
Who's winning?
SHEPPARD AND MCKAY
I am!
MCKAY
Still colicky, huh?
TEYLA
He was awake all day, refused to take a nap.
SHEPPARD
That's not surprising. He got a full night's sleep.
TEYAL
Yes, it's remarkable. He slept through the entire ordeal. He hasn't slept through a whole night in um…ever!
MCKAY
(smug)
Well, he can rest easy now, cause, uh, Michael's finally out of the picture.
TEYLA
Unfortunately, there will be somebody else to take his place, of this, I am certain.
SHEPPARD
Well he's got SuperMom watching his back. If you ask me, he's in good hands.

[Teyla looks amused. Sheppard leans close and jiggles his finger at the alert child.]

SHEPPARD
Torren, do you want to watch me kick Rodney's ass?
MCKAY
Ha, ha. Actually, you may want to cover the child's eyes, because this could get ugly.

[Teyla chuckles as her teammates turn and ready their controllers.]

SHEPPARD
Are you ready?
MCKAY
Mm-hmm.
SHEPPARD
One…two…

[He starts his car moving.]

MCKAY
Hey! Hey!

[McKay starts his own car and races to catch up. Teyla continues to bounce her son and laughs at her teammates' antics.]

FADE OUT

END CREDITS
