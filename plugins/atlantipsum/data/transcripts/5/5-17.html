SGA 5.17 "Infection" Episode Guide

Précis

A distress call from a hive ship over Atlantis leads the team to discover a Wraith crew in stasis, with all hands stricken with a disease caused in part by Dr. Keller's gene therapy.

[McKay checks a terminal.]

MCKAY
Okay, so navigation's acting up. Look, whatever we decide, we gotta do it quickly because I cannot stay ahead of these malfunctions for much longer.

[Sheppard bites his lip.]

INT—HIVE HIBERNATION POD AREA

[Ronon, Sheppard, and Teyla stand outside Todd's pod.]

RONON
You sure about this?

[Sheppard readies his rifle. Teyla deactivates the chamber. Todd awakens and takes in the situation.]

TODD
Sheppard…
SHEPPARD
How are you feeling?
TODD
I did not think you would come.
RONON
(deadpan)
What are friends for?
TEYLA
Your ship is malfunctioning. We need you to fix it.
TODD
(caustically)
You woke me for this?
SHEPPARD
That's right. We've got work to do.

INT—HIVE BRIDGE

[Todd works some controls. McKay approaches. The rest of the team is nearby.]

MCKAY
(hesitantly)
You know, I did…I tried that already.

[Todd snarls at McKay, who steps back, hand raised.]

MCKAY
(sighing)
Okay, all right. You know what? Just go ahead. Boy, somebody woke up on the wrong side of the pod this morning.
TODD
This makes no sense. These systems are behaving erratically.
MCKAY
(annoyed)
Well, that's what I've been trying to tell you!
TODD
What have you done to them?!
MCKAY
I haven't done anything! They've been acting all screwy since the moment we came on board!

[Todd stares at McKay, thinking quickly.]

TODD
Nothing's responding the way it should. It's almost as if…
SHEPPARD
What?
TODD
No. It's not possible.
RONON
What's not possible?
TODD
I believe I know why this ship is malfunctioning.

EXT—SPACE

[Todd's hive ship floats in orbit.]

INT—HIVE SCIENCE LAB

[Todd and Sheppard's team, except for McKay, have joined Keller and marines in the science lab.]

KELLER
(incredulous)
The disease is in the ship?
TODD
It is the only explanation that makes sense.
SHEPPARD
All right, if you say so.
KELLER
How is that even possible?
TODD
During the process of…hibernation, there is a continuous exchange of fluid between Wraith and hive. The disease must have been transmitted in this manner.
SHEPPARD
(sarcastic)
Great. So we're flying around in a giant tumor.
RONON
With no way off.
TODD
As time goes on and the disease progresses, more and more systems will become affected: inertial dampeners, life support, hull integrity…
TEYLA
Can we land it?
TODD
Landing a hive is a tricky proposition in the best of circumstances. Under these conditions, I would not recommend it.
RONON
So, what do we do?
TODD
There is only one thing we can do. We must cure this disease.
KELLER
Wait a second. I've barely scratched the surface on what went wrong here. I mean, it could take months to analyze the data.
TODD
At the current level of the ship's degradation, I estimate we have four days before it becomes uninhabitable.
SHEPPARD
Well, you better get to work.

[Todd growls and sniffs, then turns away.]

INT—HIVE BRIDGE

[Sheppard's team and Lorne rejoin McKay. Some marines stand guard outside.]

SHEPPARD
How's it going, Rodney?
MCKAY
(annoyed)
Well, aside from the fact that every time I try to fix something, something else breaks? Fine.
SHEPPARD
So we can expect more of these power fluctuations?
MCKAY
I think it's more than likely. It's probably going to get worse.
SHEPPARD
Then we've got a problem.
RONON
The other Wraith.
SHEPPARD
We can't afford to let them get out anymore.
MCKAY
Well, I have been able to identify that safety protocol I was talking about. I won't be able to override it completely, but I should be able to make a few key alterations.
SHEPPARD
Yeah? Like what?
MCKAY
Well, I mean the simplest thing to do would be to make it so the pods don't release the Wraith when they shut down. That would kill them. Basically, suffocate them.
RONON
Great! They're Wraith!
MCKAY
Yeah, but are they? I mean, they've taken the treatment. They no longer feed.
LORNE
Tell that to Lieutenant Williams.
TEYLA
What about Todd? If significant numbers of his crew are killed, he will demand to know why.
SHEPPARD
Just another malfunction. Do it.
MCKAY
All right.

[McKay enters some commands.]

INT—HIVE SCIENCE LAB

[Todd groans and huddles over a console.]

KELLER
You all right?
TODD
It will pass.
KELLER
I'm sorry. I never meant for any of this to happen. I honestly thought the treatment would make things better for everyone.

[Todd laughs heartily.]

KELLER
What's funny?
TODD
I find it amusing. Human apologizing to Wraith.
KELLER
(caustic)
That's because you don't understand compassion. You just view it as a sign of weakness.
TODD
Isn't it?
KELLER
No. Not by a long shot.
TODD
Ah. I'm not sure Colonel Sheppard would agree with you.
KELLER
Colonel Sheppard's a soldier. I'm a doctor.
TODD
(patronizing)
Ah, yes. His job is to take lives, and yours is to save them.
KELLER
No, his job is also to save lives. He just has a different…set of tools.
TODD
Hmm.

INT—HIVE BRIDGE

MCKAY
All right…I've created a program to override the safety protocol. You sure you want me to do this?
SHEPPARD
Go ahead.
MCKAY
All right.

[He types some commands. The monitor shows power shutting down in all but one small area.]

TEYLA
What is that?
MCKAY
It's one of the hibernation chambers. It's not accepting the new command.

[He tries again.]

MCKAY
No, it's no good. I can't access it from here.
SHEPPARD
How many of them are in them?
MCKAY
Well, it's one of the smaller auxiliary chambers, so…a-a couple of dozen at the most?
SHEPPARD
All right. We're going to have to figure out another way to deal with these guys.

[He reaches into a pack and pulls out some C-4.]

INT—HIVE CORRIDOR

[Sheppard, Lorne, Ronon, and Teyla head down the corridor. Teyla pauses, sensing something.]

TEYLA
Colonel…

[A Wraith warrior appears behind them. They fire on the Wraith, killing it.]

SHEPPARD
Well, that's not a good sign. Let's move.

INT—HIVE HIBERNATION POD AREA

[The lights flicker as they arrive.]

LORNE
Power fluctuations.
RONON
More of them might be out.
SHEPPARD
Oh, this is what I was afraid of.
(keying radio)
McKay?

INT—HIVE BRIDGE

SHEPPARD
(over radio)
We just figured out why you weren't able to access the last chamber.
MCKAY
(into radio)
What? What is it?

INT—POD CHAMBER

SHEPPARD
We're too late. The pods are opened.

INT—HIVE BRIDGE

MCKAY
How many got out?

INT—POD CHAMBER

[Sheppard looks at the levels of empty chambers, about 22 in all.]

SHEPPARD
All of them.

INT—HIVE CORRIDOR

[Sheppard briefs his team, sans McKay, Lorne's team, and the other marines.</blockquote>

SHEPPARD
All right, we're going to split into teams. We're going to divide each deck into four sections. We're going to sweep through starting from this position. Don't take any chances. Shoot anything that moves. Everyone clear?

[They nod.]

SHEPPARD
All right, let's move out.

[Ronon and a marine move along one area.]

[Teyla spots a Wraith and shoots it. She and Ramirez continue on.]

[Sheppard and his marine run into two more Wraith and kill them.]

INT—HIVE SCIENCE LAB

TODD
(intrigued)
It says here you attempted to use the Iratus DNA in your treatment.
KELLER
Yeah, I thought using the pure DNA of an Iratus bug would help prevent the deterioration of your Wraith abilities. Got the idea from Michael if you can believe that, but unfortunately it didn't work, so…

[She notices Todd's distracted expression.]

KELLER
What is it?
TODD
I have an idea.

INT—HIVE CORRIDOR

[Sheppard patrols with his marine, checking all the Wraith are dead.]

KELLER
(over radio)
Colonel Sheppard, come in please?
SHEPPARD
(into radio)
This is Sheppard.

INT—HIVE SCIENCE LAB

KELLER
(over radio)
You need to come down to the lab right away. Todd needs to speak to you.
SHEPPARD
(irritated, over radio)
I'm a little busy right now. Can it wait?
KELLER
Apparently not.

INT—HIVE CORRIDOR

SHEPPARD
(to marine)
Meet up with Teyla and Ramirez until I get back.

INT—CORRIDOR OUTSIDE HIVE SCIENCE LAB

[Keller meets Sheppard outside the science lab.]

SHEPPARD
What's so damn important?
KELLER
He says there's a way to fix this.

INT—HIVE SCIENCE LAB

SHEPPARD
Fix what? Cure the disease?
TODD
Not cure it. But undo it.
SHEPPARD
(impatient)
What are you talking about?
TODD
As you know, healthy Wraith have an inherent ability to heal themselves of almost any ailment. But this has not always been the case. Hundreds of thousands of years ago, there was…a treatment of last resort.
SHEPPARD
All right, spare me the history lessons.
TODD
A terminally ill Wraith…could…occasionally …restore himself to perfect health…if he allowed an Iratus…"bug", as you call them, specifically a queen, to feed on him.
KELLER
And you're just bringing this up now?
TODD
(speaking with difficulty)
The process was abandoned long ago because it is dangerous, almost always resulting in the death of the Wraith in question. I only…consider it now because…it would appear that we are…beyond…hope. Now there is…a planet less than three days journey where we will find what we need.
SHEPPARD
(incredulous)
McKay can barely keep this thing in orbit, and you want to take it into hyperspace?
TODD
(with bravado)
Oh, I can manage it.
SHEPPARD
Well, forget about it. We're going to come up with a solution right here.
TODD
This is the only way to save my crew.
SHEPPARD
Well, we're not leaving.

[Sheppard and the other Atlantis team members start to leave.]

TODD
At least do me the courtesy of being honest. This is not about you not wanting to risk a jump to hyperspace in a damaged ship. You do not want me to go because I'll be restoring the lives of hundreds of unhealthy Wraith, including myself.
SHEPPARD
Well, I got to admit, that did cross my mind.
TODD
This disease is your responsibility. If I had not agreed to meet with you to discuss this "innovation" of yours, my crew and I would not be in this predicament!
SHEPPARD
Well, I might be feeling a little more charitable if you hadn't tried to kill half my people last time we decided to trust each other!

[Todd leaps forward, raising his feeding hand instinctively. The marines raise their rifles. Sheppard stands his ground as Todd realizes he cannot feed.]

TODD
You…owe me this, Sheppard.
SHEPPARD
(coldly)
I don't owe you anything. Get him out of here.

[Two marines escort the shocked Todd out of the room.]

KELLER
Well, I guess I can't count on his help anymore.

[The ship rumbles and falters.]

KELLER
(fearful)
What was that?
MCKAY
(over radio)
Sheppard. You're going to want to get up here.
SHEPPARD
(to Keller)
Keep working.

[Keller gets back to work.]

INT—HIVE BRIDGE

[Sheppard joins McKay on the bridge just as there's another shaking rumble.]

SHEPPARD
What the hell was that?
MCKAY
It looks like Todd's estimate of how long the ship's going to last is a little off.
SHEPPARD
What are you talking about?

[McKay shows Sheppard a schematic of the ship on a monitor.]

MCKAY
You remember that hole we saw? It's getting bigger, it's beginning to threaten the hull integrity, but that's not the biggest problem. See that?
(he points at another terminal)
That is a line of structural weakness right across the width of the hive. If there's an explosive decompression, this entire thing could be ripped in half.
SHEPPARD
(worriedly into radio)
This is Sheppard. All teams fall back. Fall back immediately.

INT—HIVE CORRIDOR, TEYLA'S AREA

[Teyla's in a firefight with Wraith.]

TEYLA
(shouting into radio)
We are not finished dealing with the Wraith yet!

INT—HIVE BRIDGE

SHEPPARD
(into radio)
Just leave 'em. The hull's failing. Get the hell out of there!

INT—HIVE CORRIDOR, LORNE'S AREA

LORNE
All right, you heard the man. Let's move!

INT—HIVE CORRIDOR, TEYLA'S AREA

TEYLA
Let's go! Let's go!

INT—HIVE CORRIDOR, RONON'S AREA

RONON
Come on! Hurry up!

[They are ambushed by Wraith. The marine goes down. Ronon turns to help but he too is tackled. His gun is knocked away and he struggles to keep a Wraith from biting him, punching it several times in the face. Teyla's team arrive and shoot the Wraith.]

TEYLA
Are you all right?
RONON
Ach. Yeah.

[He looks over to where a marine checks Ronon's colleague and shakes his head.]

RONON
Let's get the hell out of here.

[He gets to his feet with a groan. They run and meet up with Lorne and another marine struggling with a closed door.]

TEYLA
What is it?
LORNE
The door's jammed. It won't open.
TEYLA
(into radio)
Rodney, this is Teyla, We are stuck behind a door that will not open. Can you help us?

INT—HIVE BRIDGE

MCKAY
(into radio)
Look, the controls are malfunctioning. I can't seem to override them. Just give me a minute.

INT—HIVE CORRIDOR

[They hear sounds of movement coming towards them.]

TEYLA
We might not have a minute, Rodney.

[Wraith descend on their position again and they are locked in a firefight.]

INT—HIVE CORRIDOR, BRIDGE SIDE

[Sheppard jogs to the other side of the door. He hears gunfire from his friends, but he can't get it open either.]

SHEPPARD
Oh, crap.

[He tries to pry the door open.]

INT—HIVE SHIP BRIDGE

[An alarm begins to sound. McKay checks the monitors.]

MCKAY
(panicked)
Oh, no. Uh, guys? You need to get out of there now. You hear me? Get out of there now!

INT—HIVE CORRIDOR

[Teyla, Lorne and the others provide cover fire. Between Ronon and a marine's efforts on one side, and Sheppard's on the other, the door is able to be pried open.]

SHEPPARD
Go, go, go, go, go, go, go! Go!

[The others back away into the door, giving each other cover fire as they reach safety. Sheppard also provides cover fire, then escapes with them. The door closes before a Wraith can follow.]

EXT—SPACE

[The hive ship breaks in half horizontally across the ship.]

INT—HIVE BRIDGE

MCKAY
(frantic)
Sheppard! Come in! Please!

INT—HIVE CORRIDOR

[Everyone catches their breath in relief.]

SHEPPARD
(breathless)
Yeah, we're here.

INT—HIVE BRIDGE

[The conversation switches between the bridge and corridor.]

MCKAY
(relieved)
Oh, thank god. Then I take it the bulkhead held?
SHEPPARD
Yeah, for now.
MCKAY
Look, I was able to seal off the section so we won't depressurize, but you need to get down here…

[Alerts sound on the bridge.]

MCKAY
Oh, no, no, no, no, no, no, no, no!
SHEPPARD
Oh, what now?
MCKAY
The force of the ship's tearing itself apart. It's shifted our trajectory. We're headed straight for the planet!

[Everyone exchanges worried looks as the ship shudders.]

EXT—SPACE

[The front half of the hive ship tumbles out of orbit towards the surface of Atlantis, nearly nose-down.]

INT—HIVE BRIDGE

[The rest of the Atlantis team arrives on the bridge; Keller is still in the science lab.]

SHEPPARD
What's our status, Rodney?
MCKAY
I would say "screwed" is an apt description.
SHEPPARD
Are we going to burn up?
MCKAY
No, we're going to survive reentry. In fact, it'll even slow us down a little. But nowhere near enough.
RONON
Can you get us over the water?
MCKAY
Land, water—at the speed we're traveling, it's not going to matter. We're going to be vaporized on impact!
SHEPPARD
We've got to do something!
MCKAY
Like what?
SHEPPARD
Now think!
MCKAY
(focusing)
I don't know…okay, right. Forward maneuvering thrusters. They should be unaffected, but they're not getting any power, because the propulsion conduits have been ripped in half. All right, look, I may be able to reroute power to them, so they can slow our descent.
SHEPPARD
All right, now you're talking.
MCKAY
(despairing)
The only problem is, I haven't a clue how to do it! Look, I mean, even if the systems weren't all malfunctioning, it would still take me a few hours to create that kind of work-around.
SHEPPARD
All right, just keep working on it.

[Sheppard starts to run out of the bridge.]

RONON
Where the hell are you going?
SHEPPARD
(not stopping)
To go get some help.

[McKay sighs and grumbles to himself as he turns back to the controls.]

INT—WRAITH BRIG AREA

[Sheppard jogs up to Todd's cell. Todd sits on a bench, facing away from the door.]

SHEPPARD
Get up.
TODD
(not facing him)
You can't quite leave me be.
SHEPPARD
Look, I don't have time to play games. You know what that shaking is?
TODD
Oh, I can guess.
SHEPPARD
We've lost orbit. We're entering the atmosphere, which means in a few minutes, we're going to slam into that planet!
TODD
(shouting)
A quick death will be a welcome reprieve!
SHEPPARD
Aw, I didn't think you were such a quitter. Look, you get this ship on the ground, I'll take you to your bug planet.
TODD
And you expect me to believe that?
SHEPPARD
My word.

[Todd rises and faces Sheppard.]

TODD
And my crew?
SHEPPARD
(reluctantly)
They're dead.

[Todd growls.]

SHEPPARD
The ship sheared in two. We lost the entire rear section.
TODD
(furious)
If we had gone to the planet when I suggested it, none of this would've happened!
SHEPPARD
We would've never made it there, and you know it. The hull was too badly damaged. Now, I understand you're pissed off about your crew, but we're about to join them unless somebody manages to slow us down, so are you going to help us out?

[Todd snarls at Sheppard.]

INT—HIVE BRIDGE

[Sheppard and Todd enter the bridge. Teyla is manning the helm, McKay next to her.]

TEYLA
We're in the atmosphere. Altitude on hundred thousand feet. It is of no use. I can't control it.
TODD
Move aside!

[McKay stumbles back. Teyla lets Todd take the helm and moves to another station.]

RONON
(to Sheppard)
You sure about this?
SHEPPARD
What can he do? We're in a free fall.
TODD
I believe I can successfully create a bypass that will deliver power to the forward thrusters, but it will take a few moments.
MCKAY
Hey, you just take your time.
TEYLA
Impact in fifteen seconds.
TODD
Not if I can help it. Firing thrusters!

EXT—ATLANTIS ORBIT

[The ship begins to slow as the thrusters fire.]

INT—HIVE SHIP BRIDGE

TEYLA
Forty thousand feet, still descending too fast.
MCKAY
It's not enough!
TODD
(roaring)
Instead of criticizing, perhaps you could work at getting me some more power!
MCKAY
I'm on it!

[McKay rushes to another station.]

TEYLA
Twenty thousand feet, still too fast.
SHEPPARD
Angle the thrusters, try to get the ship level. We can fly her across the water. Trust me, I've done this before.

[Todd growls skeptically.]

TEYLA
Ten thousand feet.
MCKAY
That's it. That's all the power we've got!

[Todd hisses.]

EXT—ATLANTIS SKY, DAY

[The ship starts to level off, now flying less steeply but still descending over the water.]

INT—HIVE SHIP BRIDGE

[Everyone looks anxious as the ship vibrates around them.]

TEYLA
Two thousand feet!
MCKAY
This is it! We're about to lose inertial dampeners.

[They all brace for impact, including Keller still in the lab.]

EXT—ATLANTIS OCEAN, DAY

[The ship skims the water before settling into a relatively gentle crash, the impact wave moving outward around the ship.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[A monitor shows the crash site. Woolsey stands by Banks's station.]

BANKS
They just hit the water. I've got the coordinates.
WOOLSEY
Relay them to the rescue jumpers immediately.

EXT—ATLANTIS, DAY

INT—WOOLSEY'S OFFICE

[Woolsey sits in a chair reading. Sheppard enters, moving slowly.]

WOOLSEY
Colonel. Good to see you back on your feet. How's the rest of your team?
SHEPPARD
You know, recovering. We took quite a jolt when we hit the water.

[Sheppard sits in a chair next to Woolsey.]

WOOLSEY
I can imagine. You were lucky. By the time Major Kersey and his team were able to blast their way down to your position, the hive, what remained of it, was already half full of water. It sank minutes after they extracted you.
SHEPPARD
Mmm. Wish I was awake to see that.

[Sheppard glances at the monitor near them. It shows Todd in an isolation ward in the infirmary.]

SHEPPARD
How is he?
WOOLSEY
Not well. He hasn't responded to any treatment.

[Sheppard looks troubled. Woolsey closes his book.]

WOOLSEY
I received your request.
SHEPPARD
And?
WOOLSEY
Forgive me if I'm wrong, but…weren't you the one who wanted to destroy his ship when it first appeared?
SHEPPARD
Mmm, sounds vaguely familiar.
WOOLSEY
And now you want me to go against protocol and let him go.
SHEPPARD
Well, he is the reason we're alive.
WOOLSEY
I realize that Colonel. He's also the reason you were in danger in the first place, not to mention an incredible security risk.
SHEPPARD
All right, look. He's probably going to die anyway.
WOOLSEY
And if he survives?
SHEPPARD
If he survives, he's going to remember what we did. Like it or not, he's the best ally we've had in the fight against the Wraith. If we ever get around to perfecting this gene therapy, we're going to need his help to deploy it.
WOOLSEY
You realize this is the kind of decision that could cost me my job?
SHEPPARD
(shrugging)
Well, aren't they all?

INT—ATLANTIS GATE ROOM

[Sheppard stands before the active Stargate. Todd is escorted to him by some marines.]

TODD
I appreciate your decision to let me go.
SHEPPARD
A deal's a deal.
TODD
I will remember this, John Sheppard.

[Sheppard studies Todd as Todd gives him an enigmatic smile then steps into the wormhole alone. Sheppard looks on as the gate shuts down.]

FADE OUT

END CREDITS
