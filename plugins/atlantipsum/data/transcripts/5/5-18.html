SGA 5.18 "Identity" Episode Guide

Précis

An Ancient device on another planet causes Dr. Keller to swap bodies with a woman named Neeva, who hides her true identity from the Atlantis expedition. Once they realize what has happened, Sheppard’s team, with Dr. Carson Beckett along, must find and deactivate the device that caused the body swap. They must rescue Keller from people who believe she is Neeva, and who have troubles of their own.

NEEVA
(defensively)

Nothing.

Hi.

(sultry)
Yeah. Come on. Please? Just you and me…alone. Oh, you could take me somewhere secluded. We can…we could make love underneath the stars.

Would you like that?

Well…yeah! I mean, I'd have to get a blanket though, because the mainland's groundcover is loaded with allergens.

Forget about the infirmary. I need to be with you.

How is that even possible?

JANNICK

INT—ATLANTIS CORRIDOR, EAST PIER

NEEVA

I have no idea how this happened or why.

The Ancient communication terminal?

Exactly!

Of course. The stones, the psychic link.

Wh-wh-what. What terminal? What stones?

Okay, look. SG-1 discovered an Ancient device. I think so, yes.

Ah, my first question as well. Then I remembered Janus's lab….

We have to go to the planet, find the communications terminal, and shut it off.
MCKAY
It was activated on that end, it's the initial transmitter. The only way to sever the connection is to shut it down at the source.
WOOLSEY
And have you figured out how to safely do that?
MCKAY
(reluctantly)
No, not yet.
SHEPPARD
We're not going to know until we get a good look at this thing.
BECKETT
Well, the sooner the better. I've been studying the SG-1 report. The device connects beyond merely the psychological. There's also a physiological connection as well.
TEYLA
(worried)
What does that mean?
BECKETT
It means…should Neeva's body be killed, Jennifer's mind would perish along with her.
MCKAY
(disgusted)
And we'll be stuck with this…other woman in Keller's body?
RONON
We need to get moving.
WOOLSEY
Agreed. Do we know which planet this device is on?
SHEPPARD
We will.

INT—INFIRMARY ISOLATION WARD

[Sheppard and McKay talk to Neeva.]

NEEVA
I would be happy to take you to the planet. I'll lead you straight to the device, on one condition.
MCKAY
Heh, you're in no position to make any demands—
SHEPPARD
What is it?
MCKAY
(weakly)
—young lady.
SHEPPARD
When you sever that connection, you have to agree not to pursue me or my accomplices.

[McKay scoffs.]

SHEPPARD
(stiffly)
Fine.
NEEVA
And I want you to give me one of those puddle jumpers.
MCKAY
What?! Forget it!
NEEVA
Why not? I've been out there. I know how many you have. You have more than enough.
SHEPPARD
Well, you're not get anything.
NEEVA
Weapons, then.
SHEPPARD
You take us to the planet; we agree not to pursue you. That's it. Take it or leave it.
NEEVA
(sighing)
All right, all right. Let's go.

WORMHOLE EFFECT

EXT—PLANET STARGATE, DAY

[Sheppard's team, Beckett, and Neeva exit the wormhole.]

SHEPPARD
Which way?
NEEVA
This way. It's not far. A few hours' walk.
MCKAY
(protesting)
A few hours?! You could've told us that before!
SHEPPARD
Wouldn't have made any difference, Rodney. We can't get a jumper in this forest.
TEYLA
And the Daedalus is back on Earth.
MCKAY
Yeah, but I could've packed some extra power bars, some water, not to mention proper insoles.
NEEVA
He complains a lot.
SHEPPARD
Only when he's awake.
NEEVA
Is it true that Doctor Keller and he are…?
SHEPPARD
Hard to believe, huh?

[Neeva glances back at McKay.]

MCKAY
(annoyed)
Yeah, keep walking!

INT—JAIL CELL

[Keller sits on her small cot. The magistrate arrives.]

MAGISTRATE
It's time.

[Guards approach and pull Keller out of her cell.]

EXT—FOREST, DAY

[The team arrive at the outskirts of the village.]

NEEVA
That's it. The vault containing the artifacts is near the center of the village, so we'll have to be quiet. Maybe only a few of us should go in? And I'm going to need a weapon in case we run into some—
SHEPPARD
No. We're not breaking into the room.
NEEVA
What?
SHEPPARD
We're just going to go talk to these people and ask them for help.
NEEVA
Oh, they're not going to help us. I tried to rob them.
MCKAY
They won't know you. You're in Jennifer Keller's body, remember?
TEYLA
If you do not say anything, it should not be a problem.
NEEVA
Okay, but what if they've captured her…me? Once we sever the connection, what's to stop them from keeping me locked up?
SHEPPARD
(unconcerned)
Umm…nothing.
NEEVA
Ahh, nothing.
SHEPPARD
You said we couldn't pursue you, you didn't say anything about them.
NEEVA
So you tricked me.
SHEPPARD
Hey, just take it easy.
NEEVA
I…listen, I am not going to let you disarm this device without first knowing where my body is.
MCKAY
You don't have a choice.
BECKETT
We're wasting valuable time here, people!
RONON
(indicating Neeva)
Maybe she should stay here.
NEEVA
(peeved)
No…I'll go.
SHEPPARD
Ronon…?
RONON
Yeah, I got her.
SHEPPARD
Let's go.

[They head out, Ronon near Neeva.]

INT—VILLAGE STORE

[The team enters a small store where a woman and young man stand behind the till.]

SHEPPARD
Hi. It's okay, we're friends. We're, uh, we're from Atlantis. I'm Colonel John Sheppard.
WOMAN
(primly)
What is it you want?
SHEPPARD
Well, we have a problem, and we need some help.
MCKAY
We hear that this village holds a number of Ancient artifacts, most notably a communication terminal about yay big, roundish…
(he slows his words as he sees the couple's wary looks)
…grooves along the outside? Yes?
WOMAN
Why do you ask about this device?
SHEPPARD
So you have it. Where is it?
WOMAN
It's hidden…and well protected. So if you have any designs on removing it—
MCKAY
No, no, no, no, no. Look, we just want to take a look at it. This may sound strange, but we think it may have been inadvertently activated, and it's…well, it's causing some problems for us on Atlantis.
SHEPPARD
Long story short, we're just going to shut it off, and…well, we'll be on our way.
BECKETT
We're not here to cause any trouble. We'd just like…some assistance.
WOMAN
I do not have the authority to grant such assistance. For that, you will need to speak to the village magistrate.
SHEPPARD
All right, let's um, let's talk to him.
WOMAN
He's not here.
MCKAY
(muttering to himself)
Oh, for god's sake.
SHEPPARD
How long is he going to be gone? This is urgent!
WOMAN
He will return when the execution is completed.

[The team look at her with dawning horror.]

EXT—FOREST, DAY

[Keller is marched between two guards, her hands tied behind her back. In the distance she sees more guards, the magistrate, and a hooded executioner waiting with a big axe near a stump. She balks.]

KELLER
Oh…oh…oh my God!

[In another part of the forest, the team run through the woods.]

EXT—EXECUTION SITE

[Keller is forced to kneel before the stump.]

MAGISTRATE
Neeva Casol…you have been found guilty of the crime of murder, and so you shall be put to death.
KELLER
(desperately)
I am not Neeva Casol!

EXT—FOREST, DAY

EXT—EXECUTION SITE, DAY

[Keller shakes her head in desperation. She's forced to lean over the stump.]

MAGISTRATE
Don't worry. The executioner's blade is strong, his blade sharp. Your death will be far less painful than those of your victims.
KELLER
(tearfully)
Please. Please, don't do this!

EXT—FOREST, DAY

[The team continues to run through the woods.]

EXT—EXECUTION SITE, DAY

[The executioner looks through the one patch to the magistrate, who nods. He touches his blade to Keller's neck, then raises it. He is shot in the chest.]

MAGISTRATE
Ambush!

[The guards and magistrate spread out, trying to pinpoint the gunfire, and raising their own guns. Keller takes the opportunity to run away. She runs into the forest, her hands still tied behind her. Keller trips over a branch. She gets back to her feet to find herself confronted by two men, Neeva's accomplices. Jannick approaches, flicking a dagger in his hand. She stands nervously as he comes closer. He reaches behind and cuts her hands free. She stares at him, nonplussed.]

JANNICK
What? No "thank you" for the rescue? Let's go.

[On the opposite side, the Atlantis team is just reaching the general area. They stop as they hear yelling ahead, pausing to listen.]

MAGISTRATE
(yelling out)
She went that way! Find her! Go back to the village! Gather more men! I want that ancestral ring guarded! No one leaves this planet!
NEEVA
(to team)
We're too late.

EXT—FOREST, DAY

[The team cautiously moves through the woods.]

MCKAY
I don't believe this. How did she escape?
NEEVA
My accomplices no doubt intervened. This is not the first time we've been in this situation.
BECKETT
How many accomplices do you have?
NEEVA
Two.
RONON
(incredulous)
That's all?
NEEVA
That's more than enough, trust me. You don't want to challenge them.
RONON
Yeah? We'll see about that.
TEYLA
Where would they have gone?
NEEVA
(sighing innocently)
I have no idea.
SHEPPARD
All right, Chewie, start tracking.
RONON
Yeah, I'm on it.

INT—CAVE

[Bordal, Jannick, and Keller enter the cave where they've camped out. The two men make themselves comfortable while Keller nervously stands against the wall near the entrance]

JANNICK
You looked frightened out there. What, you didn't think we'd save you?
KELLER
(stammering)
What? Di-no. I just—
BORDAL
After how many times you saved our hides? We weren't going to let you down.
JANNICK
You all right? You look confused.
KELLER
(gulping)
No, I'm just…you know, happy to be out of there.
BORDAL
So what's the plan?
KELLER
(blankly)
Plan?
JANNICK
Yeah. What do we do now?
KELLER
Uh…I don't know…

[Bordal and Jannick exchange wary looks. Keller notices their reaction.]

KELLER
…yet. Yet! I don't know yet. I—I just need…more time…to think!
JANNICK
Well, think faster. Because sooner or later, those villagers are going to find this cave. And I don't want to be here when they do.
KELLER
Right. Good point.

EXT—FOREST, DAY

RONON
This way.

[They pause as they spot villagers patrolling nearby.]

BECKETT
That's the third patrol in the last ten minutes.
TEYLA
It seems as if the entire village is out looking for her.
MCKAY
(rueful)
Let's hope we find her before they do.

[Neeva notices all their attention is on the villagers, and makes a break for it.]

MCKAY
Hey! Hey!

[Neeva races through the woods, Ronon taking the lead of the pursuit behind her. He pulls up short in an empty area.]

SHEPPARD
What the hell happened?
RONON
(frustrated)
I lost her.
TEYLA
Are there no tracks?
RONON
There's nothing! She's good.

INT—CAVE

KELLER
Listen, I think the best plan of action is to leave this planet. Now, I know of another planet. We'll be safe there.
JANNICK
They'll be guarding the ring.
KELLER
I know. Maybe we could create some kind of diversion?
BORDAL
Well, how do we do that?
KELLER
I-I don't know. Um…Maybe…maybe we could we set an explosion.
JANNICK
And why would they run toward an explosion?
KELLER
(brightly)
Or something else?
JANNICK
I say we wait 'till nightfall…then we'll launch an attack on the ring. Catch them by surprise.
KELLER
(biting her thumb nervously)
Uh, an attack?
JANNICK
We'll kill the guards, activate the ring, and we make our escape.
KELLER
Um…
BORDAL
I agree.
KELLER
Okay…uh, but if I think of something else before then, maybe we could…

[Bordal and Jannick raise their pistols and cock them. Keller raises her hands. Neeva enters the cave, her hands raised. Keller looks at Neeva in shock.]

NEEVA
All right, don't shoot.
JANNICK
Who…are…you?

[Neeva is distracted by looking at Keller.]

NEEVA
I'm…Neeva Casol. All right, let me explain. Back at the artifact vault, I accidentally activated some kind of Ancestral communications device that caused my mind to transfer into this woman's body and hers into mine.

[Keller expression changes from bemusement to understanding.]

JANNICK
You expect us to believe that?
NEEVA
Yeah, Jannick I do. I expect you to believe that, just as you did on Vara Sol when I brokered that deal with the fish merchant to save your life? And you, Bordal. If you didn't trust me back when you got caught at that brothel, those women would've made sure the thought of procreating never again entered your mind.
BORDAL
How do you know about that?
NEEVA
Because I'm Neeva! There's a link between this woman and me. Her mind is in my body, and mine is in hers.
KELLER
Look, whatever's happening here, I had nothing to do with it.
NEEVA
No, she didn't, but if we're going to fix this, we need to move fast. We need to go back to the village, break into the vault, and turn off that device. We need to be really careful, because not only are the villagers out looking for us, but so are the people from Atlantis.
KELLER
W-w-wait a second, they're here?! Who? Colonel Sheppard?
NEEVA
Among others. But they're going to turn us into the villagers if they catch us, so we need no problems from you. Do you understand me?

[She glares at Keller threateningly. Keller nervously nods.]

NEEVA
All right, we need to find this device…

[Jannick brandishes his gun.]

JANNICK
Stop! I don't know what's going on here.
NEEVA
I just told you, all right. I am Neeva.
JANNICK
I've never seen you before!
NEEVA
(frustrated)
Jannick, listen to me. This is not the time to be stupid!
KELLER
Uh, I wouldn't make him angry…
NEEVA
You're going to put down the gun, and you're going to listen to what I say.
JANNICK
No. I'm not. I'm going to take the both of you with me. We're going to attack the ring now. And we'll sort out who's really who on another planet. And if you're lying to me, you're as good as dead.

[Neeva glares as Keller nervously looks from one to the other.]

EXT—FORREST

[The four walk toward the gate.]

NEEVA
This Doctor McKay…?
KELLER
What about him?
NEEVA
You can do better.
JANNICK
Be quiet.
NEEVA
Jannick, I know this is difficult to understand, and I was equally confused by it at the beginning, but you have to…

[Gunfire just misses them and they dive for cover near a tree and stump. Jannick and Bordal blindly return fire.]

JANNICK
How many?
BORDAL
I don't know.
NEEVA
I count four!
NEEVA
We'd be a lot better off if you could give us weapons.

[Bordal rises to shoot, but is downed by a gun blast. Both Neeva and Keller reach for him. Keller sees the wound and checks his pulse.]

KELLER
He's dead.

[Neeva looks stricken by the news. Jannick fires back.]

[In the distance, the Atlantis team hears the firefight and runs towards it.]

[While Jannick is distracted, Keller runs away.]

JANNICK
Hey! Come back here! Stop!

[Jannick aims at Keller.]

NEEVA
Jannick, no! Don't!

[Neeva jumps up to pull down his arm. She's shot in the gut by one of the soldiers. Jannick too is wounded, but runs away. One soldier runs after Jannick, the other reloads as Neeva writhes in pain. He aims his shotgun, and Neeva wriggles to get away. The soldier is stunned by Ronon's pistol and the team arrives as Neeva collapses.]

MCKAY
Is she going to be all right?
BECKETT
Aye, for now, but I need to stop the bleeding. Here, hold her steady, Rodney.

[McKay grasps Neeva by the shoulders as Neeva winces in pain and Beckett begins to treat the wound.]

INT—VILLAGE STORE

[The magistrate has joined the woman store owner and they look over some papers. The Atlantis team bursts in, McKay carrying Neeva. Teyla and Beckett shove some items off a table.]

BECKETT
(to villagers)
You two, give us a hand here, please.
(to Ronon)
On the table, Ronon.

[Ronon lays Neeva on the table.]

WOMAN
(to magistrate)
These are the people from Atlantis I was telling you about.
MAGISTRATE
What happened?
SHEPPARD
(angrily)
She was shot by one of your men.
WOMAN
I warned you it was dangerous to venture out there.

[Sheppard glares.]

MAGISTRATE
What, is she dead?
BECKETT
No, but she's badly wounded. Teyla, I'm going to need a hand, here. We need to remove the bullet and stop the bleeding.

[Teyla nods.]

MCKAY
And we need access to that communication terminal. We need to shut it down right now!
MAGISTRATE
Why, what's it doing?
MCKAY
Well, simply put, it makes a psychic link between two people. So Doctor Keller and this…."Neeva Casol" have switched places. They're in each other's bodies.
SHEPPARD
You know about Atlantis, right?
MAGISTRATE
(nonplussed)
Yes.
SHEPPARD
(threateningly)
Then you know you're better off being our friend than our enemy. So why don't you be our "friend", and show us where that terminal is?

[The magistrate looks troubled, but makes a decision.]

MAGISTRATE
This way.
MCKAY
All right.

[Ronon, McKay, and Sheppard follow.]

EXT—FOREST, DAY

[Keller continues to run through the woods.]

INT—VILLAGE VAULT

[The magistrate unlocks the door and leads them in.]

MCKAY
(worriedly)
It's still on!
SHEPPARD
Well, obviously. So turn it off.
MCKAY
Well, there's no switches. But it looks almost identical to the one SG-1 found.

INT—VILLAGE STORE

[Beckett works with surgical equipment to pull out the bullet from Neeva's side.]

BECKETT
There's the bullet. Now to stem the bleeding.

[He drops the bullet into a tray Teyla is holding. Teyla looks on worriedly as she holds up an IV bag in her other hand.]

EXT—FOREST, DAY

[Keller comes to a sudden stop as she sees Jannick appear before her.]

JANNICK
I don't know who you are, but you are definitely not Neeva.
KELLER
No, I'm not. But I'm not your enemy either, and if you'd just let me…I think I can help…

[Jannick raises his gun to Keller's head.]

JANNICK
You're not helping anyone.

INT—VILLAGE VAULT

[McKay frantically types at some controls on his scanner, which is hooked up to the device.]

SHEPPARD
Come on, hurry up!
MCKAY
Believe me, I'm trying. Look, there's no way to access the control crystal. There doesn't seem to be a way to shut this thing off!
SHEPPARD
All right. What if…what if we blow it up?
MCKAY
Well, I don't know. I mean, maybe? I just…I'm just not sure if the explosion will cause a-a sudden power surge…
SHEPPARD
What the hell'd the other guys do?
MCKAY
Well, SG-1 dialed the 'gate and tossed the device into the vortex of a forming wormhole. It disintegrated instantly. But, you know, it's too far to carry it back to the 'gate!

EXT—FOREST, DAY

JANNICK
(furious)
You got Bordal killed, and you've almost got me killed. I'm going to return the favor.

INT—VILLAGE VAULT

SHEPPARD
All right, well, we may not have much of a choice, so…give me a hand with this.

[He moves to pick up the device. McKay goes to help even as he protests.]

MCKAY
I'm not sure we've got the time…
RONON
Hold up…Move!

[Ronon unholsters his gun while speaking. Sheppard and McKay back up, and Ronon fires. The energy blast causes sparks and the control crystal's light darkens.]

INT—VILLAGE STORE

[Keller awakens in her own body, lying on the table where Beckett just removed the bullet. She looks around in confusion, but her face brightens in relief as she sees the IV and Teyla standing by her side.]

TEYLA
Doctor Keller?
KELLER
Teyla!
BECKETT
You're back!
KELLER
(relieved)
I'm back.
(pained)
Ow…ow!
BECKETT
Shh. You've been shot, love. Don't worry. We removed the bullet. You'll be just fine.

EXT—FOREST, DAY

[Neeva opens her eyes to find herself staring down the barrel of Jannick's gun.]

NEEVA
Wait, Jannick. It's me, Neeva. They must've turned off the device.
JANNICK
Nice try.

[He cocks his revolver.]

NEEVA
(desperately)
Jannick, no!

EXT—ATLANTIS, DAY

[The echo of a shot is heard from the end of the previous scene.]

INT—ATLANTIS CORRIDOR

[Woolsey speaks to an expedition member.]

WOOLSEY
I appreciate that, Dana. Say hello for me.

[Dana nods and walks off. McKay comes through a transporter, carrying a loose bunch of flowers and a box of chocolates. Woolsey joins him as they walk down the corridor.]

WOOLSEY
Well, well, well. What's the occasion?
MCKAY
Oh, just…visiting a friend in the infirmary.
WOOLSEY
That's very thoughtful of you, Rodney.
MCKAY
I know.
WOOLSEY
Major Lorne and his team just returned. They did a full sweep of the planet. No sign of Neeva Casol. She must've managed to escape.
MCKAY
I don't know about that. According to Jennifer, her accomplice was just about to kill her when we shut down the communication terminal.
WOOLSEY
Well, either he did kill her and disposed of the body before escaping himself, or she got away from him somehow.
MCKAY
Well, I guess we'll never know.
WOOLSEY
Well, we may. Word has gone out to several planets in the coalition. If Neeva is alive and roaming free, she won't be for long.

[They part ways. McKay looks troubled.]

MCKAY
Hmm.

INT—ATLANTIS INFIRMARY

[Beckett stands by Keller's bedside.]

BECKETT
You'll promise you'll take it easy?
KELLER
(patronizing)
Yes, Doctor.

[McKay arrives, flowers and candy in hand.]

MCKAY
(brightly)
Don't worry. I will make sure of it!
KELLER
(pleased)
Hey, you.
MCKAY
(pleased)
Hey, you.
BECKETT
Oh, thank you, Rodney. Flowers and chocolates. I'm impressed.

[He teasingly holds out his hands for the gifts.]

MCKAY
(unamused)
Right. No. She's all alone in the infirmary. I just thought I would, you know, brighten the room up a bit?

[Zelenka clears his throat pointedly from where he lies in the bed next to Keller.]

MCKAY
Oh, hey Radek. How're you doing?
ZELENKA
(drily)
Better, thanks.
KELLER
(sympathetic)
Again, Radek, I am so sorry for what happened.
ZELENKA
It's not you it's your fault. I told you, don't worry. I'm fine.

[They exchange smiles.]

MCKAY
(dismissively)
Yeah, yeah, of course he's fine. He's all perky and rosy cheeked and ready to go back to work.

[Beckett shoots him a glare.]

ZELENKA
(with annoyed sigh)
I don't know about that.
BECKETT
He needs to get rest too, Rodney.
KELLER
(admonishing)
I agree. His wound was much more serious than mine.
MCKAY
(surprised)
Really? You were shot. He was just stabbed.

[Beckett heaves an exasperated sigh.]

BECKETT
(to Zelenka)
Perhaps I should wheel you into another room?
ZELENKA
Just until he's gone, please, thank you.
BECKETT
Aye. Right.
(to McKay, in farewell)
Rodney.
MCKAY
Hmm.

[Beckett unlocks the wheels on Zelenka's bed and starts to wheel him out.]

ZELENKA
Cursing in Czech.
BECKETT
Yes, yes. I completely agree.
ZELENKA
Continues grumbling in Czech.

[Keller smiles as she watches them leave.]

MCKAY
(to Keller)
Chocolates?
KELLER
Thank you.

[He sits in the chair next to her.]

MCKAY
So, I was thinking that, uh, when you were feeling better, we could maybe, um…you know, go for a ride.
KELLER
A ride?
MCKAY
Yeah, you know, take the puddle jumper, go to the mainland, maybe have a picnic, maybe…lay out under the stars?

[Keller blinks at him.]

MCKAY
What?
KELLER
You did…turn off that communication terminal, right? There's no one else in there?
MCKAY
No, it's really me.
KELLER
(dubious)
'Cause a puddle jumper ride? And the picnic under the stars? I mean, that's just so…
MCKAY
So not me. Right. Okay. No. Bad idea.
KELLER
No…
(she takes his hand)
I think it's a great idea.

[They smile at each other.]

FADE OUT
