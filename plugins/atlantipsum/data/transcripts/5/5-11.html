SGA 5.11 "The Lost Tribe Part 2" Episode Guide

Précis

McKay and Daniel are caught in the middle of the war between the Wraith and another powerful race in the Pegasus Galaxy. Sheppard teams up with the Travelers to rescue the two after the Daedalus falls into enemy hands.

Guide | Transcript

PREVIOUSLY ON STARGATE ATLANTIS

[Excerpts from 5.10 "First Contact" are shown.]

END EXCERPTS

TEYLA
Move quickly! We have to get everyone to the lower levels of the tower to make sure that everyone will be—

(into radio, worried)
Colonel Sheppard, are you all right? John?!

MARIE
Teyla, how many were up there?

Two.

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[Debris still falls and sparks fly across short circuits. The room is unrecognizable.]

TEYLA
John?! They were right here when I left them.
MARIE
If the explosion was hot enough, there might not be anything left for us to find.

[In the distance can be heard a weak groan.]

TEYLA
Did you hear that?

[Marie nods. The groan is louder and seems to be coming from Woolsey's office.]

INT—WOOLSEY'S OFFICE

[Sheppard has bodily covered Zelenka as they both lie prone on the floor. Shards of glass cover the room, including Sheppard. Teyla and Marie rush in with the team.]

TEYLA
John!
SHEPPARD
(groaning, confused)
We need to take cover.

TEYLA
Are you all right?
SHEPPARD
Huhn?
TEYLA
(louder)
Are you all right?

TEYLA
Marie…

ZELENKA
We lost the Stargate. We lost the control room. That's bad.
TEYLA
But we did not lose you. That's good.

[Teyla nods to another member of the rescue team.]

TEYLA
(smiling)
Okay.

[The man helps Teyla lead Zelenka away.]

EXT—SPACE

[Daedalus floats near Todd's hive ship.]

INT—DAEDALUS BRIDGE

[Woolsey sits propped against a bulkhead, unconscious. Todd sits in the commander's chair. Kenny enters.]

KENNY
All the troops you've requested have beamed on board.
TODD
Have them round up the rest of the humans and confine them to one of the hangar bays.

[Another Wraith nods and does as Todd bids, leading a troop of soldier Wraith out.]

TODD
Inform our hive to wait for us here. And under no circumstances are they to activate their hyperdrive. Is that understood?
KENNY
Yes. Where are we going?
TODD
Atlantis.

END TEASER

OPENING CREDITS

INT—ATLANTIS INFIRMARY

[Power is on in this area. Marie picks several small shards of glass out of Sheppard's back with forceps. Sheppard grunts in pain. Teyla approaches.]

SHEPPARD
(to Teyla)
I need you to get Banks and her team to set up an auxiliary control room below the damaged areas.

(nodding)
They've already begun. How are you?

Ach! Well, I'm alive. You know, that's good for now.
TEYLA
I still do not understand what happened.
SHEPPARD
The 'gate blew up.
TEYLA
(drily)
That much I surmised. But why?
SHEPPARD
(shaking his head)
I don't know. Where the hell's McKay when you need him, huh?

EXT—ANCIENT FACILITY, DAY

[The center of the facility is lit up in a blue glow from the active Attero device.]

INT—ANCIENT SEALED ROOM

[Daniel sits, leaning against a wall in their latest makeshift prison cell. McKay paces nervously in front of him.]

MCKAY
All right, so the device creates a very specific subspace "turbulence" that makes it impossible for the Wraith to operate their hyperdrives.
DANIEL
(resigned)
You said that already.
MCKAY
But there's a side-effect.
DANIEL
Yes, the unforeseen one that Janus mentions.
MCKAY
Yeah, well, it's certainly not "seen" here, because there's no Stargate on this planet.
DANIEL
(aggravated)
What's the point, Rodney?
MCKAY
Look, although the subspace turbulence only affects the Wraith's hyperdrive systems, it creates a very dangerous type of radiation that, uh, well…that messes with the basic operation of the Stargates.
DANIEL
(alert)
Stargates? What, as in all of them?
MCKAY
Well, all of the Stargates in the Pegasus network, yes.
DANIEL
And when you say "messes with," you mean what?
MCKAY
Look, when dialed, power will build up inside the 'gate until it overloads.

[Daniel stands.]

DANIEL
So, you're saying that as long as this Janus device is running, every time someone dials a 'gate in the entire galaxy…
MCKAY
It'll explode. I mean, millions of people will die if we don't shut that thing down.

[They exchange very worried looks.]

EXT—SPACE

[Daedalus flies through hyperspace.]

INT—DAEDALUS BRIDGE

[Woolsey awakens to find Todd hovering over him.]

TODD
Good timing, Mister Woolsey.

[A Wraith soldier drags him to his feet.]

WOOLSEY
(with bravado)
Look, I don't know what it is you think we've done, but I assure you—
TODD
(to Kenny)
Take us out of hyperspace.
KENNY
We haven't arrived in Atlantis yet.
WOOLSEY
(shocked)
Atlantis?
TODD
We're within communications range. We don't want to get too close. Take us out.
(to Woolsey)
I was hoping you'd be awake for this.

INT—ATLANTIS AUXILIARY CONTROL ROOM

[Zelenka and Banks's team have set up computers on tables and are bringing in more equipment. Power is restored to this area. Sheppard and Teyla enter.]

SHEPPARD
Well, are we up and running?
BANKS
Yes, sir. We've routed all the city's main controls into this room.
SHEPPARD
At least we're not blind anymore.
ZELENKA
No, but we're severely crippled. And without the 'gate or the ability to reach the Daedalus, we have no way of getting to M6H-987 or rescuing Rodney and Doctor Jackson.
TEYLA
What should we do?
SHEPPARD
(loudly)
Five bucks to anybody who can figure out how to contact the Daedalus.

[Banks sits at her laptop. It blinks an alert.]

BANKS
Uh, Colonel Sheppard?
SHEPPARD
You got an idea, Banks?
BANKS
Well, no, but I am being hailed by the Daedalus on subspace. Does that count?
SHEPPARD
(surprised)
Technically, that's not your idea, but good enough. Put 'em on screen.

[He and Teyla move to the communications monitor. Sheppard taps Banks on the shoulder as he passes.]

SHEPPARD
Don't worry, I'll pay you later.

[They wait for screen to stop being static.]

SHEPPARD
(into videocom)
Daedalus! It's good to, uh—

[The image clears. They are shocked to find Todd on the screen.]

TODD
Colonel Sheppard.
SHEPPARD
What the hell's going on? Where's Caldwell?
TODD
I'm afraid I had to relieve him and his crew of duty.
SHEPPARD
(low, to Zelenka)
How close are they?
ZELENKA
(low, to Sheppard)
Not close enough to go by jumper, if that's what you're thinking.

INT—DAEDALUS BRIDGE

[The conversation switches between the Daedalus bridge and Atlantis.]

TODD
Don't worry. I'm more than willing to return this ship and its crew to you.
SHEPPARD
(suspicious)
In exchange for what?
TODD
You've activated the Attero Device. I need you to shut it down and give me its location immediately.
SHEPPARD
I don't know what you're talking about!
TODD
It was very clever of you, luring me and my ship out into the open so you could see for yourself if it worked.
SHEPPARD
If what worked?
TODD
I have to admit, in a way I'm impressed. I was there ten thousand years ago when the Ancients first tried to use it, but they didn't have the stomach to leave it on once they realized how many of their precious Stargates it would destroy…how many humans it would kill.
ZELENKA
(low, to Sheppard)
Sounds like something Janus would've worked on.
TODD
Give me the location of the Attero device, and once I have destroyed it I will return this crew to you!
SHEPPARD
Look, it's not what you think. We have nothing to do with this!
TODD
Every minute that goes by without you telling me where this device is, I will kill a member of your expedition.

[A Wraith soldier pulls Woolsey into view of the video camera. Teyla and Sheppard gasp.]

ZELENKA
Oh…

[Todd puts his arm around Woolsey in a falsely familiar pose. Woolsey looks nauseous and frightened.]

TODD
Your time starts now.
SHEPPARD
Give us a second.

[Sheppard gives a signal to Banks, and the monitor on the Daedalus reads "transmission suspended."]

WOOLSEY
I don't think this should count toward the minute. I-I mean, he didn't say "no"…yet.

[Todd slowly turns to Woolsey and growls.]

INT—ATLANTIS AUXILIARY CONTROL ROOM

SHEPPARD
What are the odds that—that…what's this thing called?
TEYLA
Uh…Attero device?
SHEPPARD
What are the odds that this thing was at the other end of the subspace link that Rodney opened when he went to that lab?
ZELENKA
M6H-987? I'd say it's very likely. It's also very likely that's where he and Dr. Jackson were taken.
TEYLA
It must be a weapon that Janus developed during the Wraith war.
SHEPPARD
A weapon to blow up Stargates?
ZELENKA
I doubt that was its original design. It doesn't seem like an effective strategy.
SHEPPARD
So, a nasty side effect. That's probably why they stopped production.
TEYLA
Whatever the aliens took from the lab allowed them to reactivate it again.
SHEPPARD
Whatever it is, Todd seems pretty keen to stop it—enough to risk taking the Daedalus.
ZELENKA
Yes, but we can't give him the address. If he gets it, he'll destroy everything in his path, including Rodney and Doctor Jackson.

[Sheppard rubs his chin and sighs.]

TEYLA
(heaving a sigh)
John, we don't have a choice. Our shield was able to take the brunt of the explosion, but if this is happening on other worlds, it could kill millions.
SHEPPARD
(waving to Banks)
Put him back on.

[The video link is reestablished and the conversation again switches between the bridge of the ship and Atlantis.]

WOOLSEY
(relieved)
Oh, thank God.
SHEPPARD
We're not the ones behind this.
TODD
Very well.

[Todd turns to Woolsey. A soldier holds him in place as he raises his hand to feed.]

SHEPPARD
W-w-wait! Wait! I said we're not the ones behind this, but we may have what you need.
TEYLA
Atlantis was attacked yesterday by a race we have not encountered before. It stole a piece of Ancient technology. We believe this could be a component to this…Attero device.

[Todd's hand slowly moves to Woolsey's chest, where it clutches his lapel. Woolsey watches the proceedings with alarm.]

SHEPPARD
We managed to track them. Swing by here, pick me up, I will take you there myself.
TODD
And give you the chance to take out my hyperdrive with one of your drones? I think not.

[Todd straightens his hand as if to feed.]

SHEPPARD
Why would I do that? Right now, you're one of the few people in the galaxy with a ship capable of dealing with this problem.

[Todd again embraces Woolsey in this falsely companionable manner.]

TODD
And I intend to keep it that way. John Sheppard, transmit the coordinates, or I will feed on Mister Woolsey before your eyes!

[Sheppard gives Todd a shrewd look. Not taking his eyes off Todd, he turns his head towards Banks.]

SHEPPARD
(tightly)
Do it.

[One of the Daedalus consoles blinks.]

KENNY
We have it.
TODD
We'll be in touch.

[The link is severed. Teyla, Sheppard, and Zelenka exchange concerned looks.]

EXT—SPACE

[Daedalus enters hyperspace.]

INT—DAEDALUS CORRIDOR

[Ronon tries to force the doors open with his bare hands. Keller watches with her arms crossed as he groans.]

KELLER
You really think that's going to work?
RONON
(groaning)
You got a better idea?
KELLER
We're on a spaceship. Those doors are designed to be airtight. That means something as tiny as air can't even get through. How do you think you will?
RONON
Yeah, well, I'm a lot bigger than air.
KELLER
Yeah…that's kinda my point.

[Ronon strains again, and this time his efforts are rewarded. The door opens. He turns back to Keller.]

RONON
(triumphant)
See?

[Keller's eyes widen in alarm as she spots the two Wraith in the corridor beyond.]

KELLER
Ronon!

[She crouches as Ronon ducks and spins, shooting the two soldiers.]

RONON
You okay?
KELLER
(shaken)
Yeah.
RONON
All right, come on.
KELLER
Wait. Wh-what's our plan? Where are we going?
RONON
We're going to get to an access ladder. We head down two floors to the engineering rooms, then we cripple the ship.
KELLER
(surprised)
You…know how to do all that?
RONON
Yeah, don't you?
KELLER
Not really.
RONON
Watch and learn.

[He leads the way.]

INT—ATLANTIS AUXILIARY CONTROL ROOM

BANKS
Hyperspace window opening.
SHEPPARD
Think Todd changed his mind?
ZELENKA
He might have realized just how crippled we are.
BANKS
We're being hailed.
SHEPPARD
Put it up.

[A woman appears on the video screen.]

KATANA
My name is Katana Labrea. I'm a Traveler. Larrin sent me.
SHEPPARD
Well, is everything all right?
KATANA
No. As a matter of fact, it's not. Larrin was hoping you could tell us why the Stargates are blowing up.

INT—ANCIENT SEALED ROOM

[McKay studies the panels on the wall, trying to figure out how to open one. The door to the room opens and one of the armored aliens walks in. He points at Daniel.]

ALIEN
You. Come with me.

[Daniel points at himself, surprised.]

DANIEL
Me? Why?

[The alien unholsters his stunner. Daniel and McKay immediately raise their hands in surrender, McKay stepping in the line of fire.]

DANIEL
Hey!
MCKAY
Whoa! We'll both go! No need for that.
ALIEN
No.
MCKAY
(weakly)
Well…I guess it…just must be your turn to use the fitness room.
DANIEL
Right.

[Daniel walks past McKay, keeping his arms raised.]

MCKAY
(worried)
Good luck.
DANIEL
(with false brightness)
You too.

[He steps out of the room at gunpoint.]

INT—ATLANTIS AUXILIARY CONTROL ROOM

[Katana has joined the Lantians.]

KATANA
It was the first colony we'd built on the surface of a planet in a hundred generations, and it was leveled in an instant. I was in orbit when it happened. We could see the explosion from space. I've never witnessed anything that powerful.
ZELENKA
Mmm. The 'gates are made of naquadah, and when they become unstable, the size of the explosion is unimaginable.
KATANA
We lost three thousand people and two ships, including our Ancient vessel. Larrin would've come herself, but…
SHEPPARD
No, I'm sure she's got her hands full. Look, these aliens or whoever they are, they have two of our people hostage. They need to be stopped. We have coordinates but…we have no ship.
KATANA
Then I guess I got here just in time.
SHEPPARD
That's what I wanted to hear. Zelenka, you're with me.

[He, Katana, and Zelenka start to leave.]

TEYLA
(protesting)
John…!
SHEPPARD
Teyla, I need you to stay here, all right? The second Todd's done, there's nothing stopping him from turning that ship around and making a run at Atlantis. I need someone I trust to stay in charge here.

[Teyla nods.]

TEYLA
Good luck.

[They leave. Teyla looks worried and frustrated.]

EXT—SPACE

[Daedalus flies through hyperspace.]

INT—DAEDALUS ENGINEERING

[The door to engineering opens. Before the Wraith in the room can react, Ronon shoots them both. He enters. Keller follows, closing the door behind them and pressing buttons on the panel.]

KELLER
I locked the door.
RONON
This won't take long.
KELLER
So what do we do? Bring up the main control systems and disable them one by one?
RONON
That's one way to go.
KELLER
Well, you'd have to lock them out of the mainframe though. Otherwise they could just turn them back on again. Do you have…some sort of a pass-code to make sure they're locked out once and for all?

[Ronon opens up a panel under one of the consoles.]

RONON
Nope.
KELLER
Well, then what are you thinking?

[Ronon looks at Keller and shoots the panel he's just opened behind him.]

EXT—SPACE

[Daedalus drops out of hyperspace and comes to a dead stop.]

INT—DAEDALUS BRIDGE

KENNY
We just dropped out of hyperspace.
TODD
I see that, yes. Why?
KENNY
I'm not sure. The computer seems to think it's due to battle damage.
TODD
Get our troops down to the engineering room. Someone's sabotaging this ship!

INT—DAEDALUS ENGINEERING

[Ronon continues moving around the room, opening crystal trays and shooting them.]

KELLER
Wh-wh-what are you doing?!
RONON
Disabling the ship.
KELLER
You can't just blast away at vital systems like that!

[Ronon casually shoots another tray.]

KELLER
What if you take out the life support system?!
RONON
Don't worry, the life support system's that tray.
(he points to a tray under the one he just shot to pieces)
At least, I'm pretty sure it is.
KELLER
(stammering)
Okay, we're out of hyperspace. That's enough. C'mon.

[Keller opens the door. Ronon shoots one final tray as Keller winces. They leave.]

EXT—ANCIENT FACILITY, DAY

INT—ATTERO DEVICE CONTROL ROOM

[Daniel is shoved into the room by his guard. The head alien stands before the console.]

HEAD ALIEN
You wish to speak to me.
DANIEL
You're the leader…? You need to shut this device down immediately.

[The head alien turns to face Daniel.]

HEAD ALIEN
No, I do not.
DANIEL
Okay, look, I-I understand wanting to destroy the Wraith. I do. But, uh, this…isn't the way.
HEAD ALIEN
I disagree.
DANIEL
Okay, I don't know if you're aware of this or not, but this device has a serious side effect. It makes Stargates explode when they're activated.
HEAD ALIEN
We do not use the Stargates.
DANIEL
(nonplussed)
Okay…but lots of other people do.
HEAD ALIEN
They are of no concern to us.
DANIEL
(biting his lip)
So you're just going to let them die, millions of people across the galaxy…You're no better than the Wraith.
HEAD ALIEN
Perhaps. But we have little choice.
DANIEL
Why?
HEAD ALIEN
Our planet is dying. It can no longer sustain us. For the first time in countless generations, we must venture out into this galaxy again—a galaxy controlled by the Wraith. If we do not destroy them, they will destroy us. This is the way it must be.

EXT—SPACE

[Katana's Traveler ship flies through hyperspace.]

INT—TRAVELER SHIP CORRIDOR

[Katana walks with Sheppard and Zelenka.]

SHEPPARD
The Wraith are ahead of us. As soon as they get to that planet, they're gonna open up with everything they've got. Trust me, they ain't going to be worried about collateral damage.
ZELENKA
The only chance we have to save our friends is to get there first.

[Katana pauses by a bulkhead door.]

KATANA
You want speed? You've come to the right place.

INT—TRAVELER SHIP ENGINEERING

[Katana opens the door. The room is a mess of cables and greasy machinery. They step inside.]

ZELENKA
(dubious)
This is your engineering room?
KATANA
That's right. It may not look like much, but trust me, we can outrun most anything in the Traveler's fleet. Mila?

[An adolescent appears from behind them.]

MILA
Hey!
KATANA
This is Colonel Sheppard and Doctor Zelenka. They're from Atlantis. Mila's our chief engineer.

[Mila passes them and grabs a tool from a cushion before heading to another part of the room.]

ZELENKA
(to Katana)
She can't be more than sixteen years old.
KATANA
Fifteen, actually. She's been working on ships since she was four.
(to Mila)
We need to get where we're going as fast as possible. I want you to do whatever you can to maximize hyperdrive output.
MILA
No problem.
SHEPPARD
You know, uh, Zelenka knows a thing or two about hyperdrives himself. I'm sure he'd be happy to give you a hand.
(to Zelenka)
Who knows? Maybe you'll learn a thing or two.

[He slaps a dubious Zelenka on the back as he and Katana leave. Zelenka moves forward, dodging sparking cables.]

EXT—SPACE

[Daedalus floats in open space.]

INT—DAEDALUS ENGINEERING

[Soldier Wraith drag out the downed Wraith. Kenny and Todd study the damage.]

KENNY
The damage is significant.
TODD
Begin repairs. Every second the Attero device is active, we risk losing more and more of our alliance.
(keys intercom)
It has come to my attention that someone from the original crew has eluded capture…

INT—DAEDALUS CORRIDOR

[Keller and Ronon walk down the corridor.]

TODD
(continuing over intercom)
…Your freedom has become a nuisance. Turn yourself in immediately, or we will begin killing off your friends, one-by-one.

[Ronon looks amused. Keller looks frightened. She grabs his arm and they stop and face each other.]

TODD
(continuing over intercom)
You have three minutes to comply.
RONON
He's bluffing.
KELLER
No, he's not.

[She walks determinedly towards an elevator.]

RONON
Where are you going?
KELLER
I'm turning myself in.
RONON
No, you're not.

[Keller turns back as the elevator opens.]

KELLER
He's gonna start killing people.
RONON
He's going to kill you.

[Keller doesn't seem to have considered this possibility.]

KELLER
Well, I hope not.
RONON
We need to get armed, free these people, and take this ship back.
KELLER
Exactly. I turn myself in, and he'll think he's got us all. It buys you the time you need to do what you've got to do.
RONON
No. No, I'm not letting you do it.
KELLER
Ronon, listen to me—
RONON
Jennifer, no.

[They face each other down in a silent test of wills. Keller looks away in capitulation.]

KELLER
Okay. You're right.
RONON
Okay. Come on.

[Ronon leads the way. As soon as he turns his back, Keller races into the elevator.]

RONON
We're almost there anyways.

[He turns as he hears the door shut. He glowers and growls angrily as he unholsters his gun.]

INT—ATTERO DEVICE CONTROL ROOM

DANIEL
(furious)
We're talking about the deaths of millions of people. How can you be so callous? And what is it you're so afraid of? Why-why-why are you still in your battle armor? I can't hurt you. I know you're not human, and you're definitely not Wraith. And if you were an Ancient, you wouldn't have needed our help to activate the device, so…what are you?

[The head alien turns away from Daniel. The armor retracts in the back. The alien inside steps out of the suit, which seals shut. The small alien steps into the light. Daniel is shocked to find himself looking at an Asgard.]

DANIEL
You're Asgard.
ASGARD
You've encountered our kind before?
DANIEL
No, not just encountered. I've…I've worked with them. Even called a few of them my friends.
ASGARD
Indeed.
DANIEL
Of course, the Asgard I knew were a…noble race who actually helped the human population in my galaxy.
ASGARD
And where are they now?
DANIEL
(sorrowful)
Dead.
ASGARD
Hmm.
DANIEL
But that had nothing to do with us.
ASGARD
Are you certain?
DANIEL
They were clones. Copies of copies of copies, going back generations, and they suffered irreversible genetic degradation as a result. But…you probably already know that.
ASGARD
Of course, because we are clones as well, subject to the same law of diminishing returns. And yet…we are still very much alive.
DANIEL
So, what? You-you solved the problem?
ASGARD
Not entirely. But once we were able to conduct our research without interference, we made significant progress.
DANIEL
(realizing)
Research on humans…because our physiology is similar to what yours used to be.
ASGARD
(impressed)
Precisely.
DANIEL
I take that back, I have met an Asgard like you before. His name was Loki. And he was experimenting on humans too, until he was caught and brought to justice by the Asgard High Council.
ASGARD
Because they did not believe the ends justified the means.
DANIEL
That's right.
ASGARD
And look where it got them.

[Daniel appears shocked by the coldness of the reasoning.]

EXT—SPACE

[The Traveler ship flies through hyperspace.]

INT—TRAVELER SHIP ENGINEERING

[Sheppard enters.]

SHEPPARD
Well how's it coming?
ZELENKA
Well, uh…

[A fire starts on a panel next to Mila. Zelenka and Sheppard look worried, but Mila stamps it out with her bare hands.]

MILA
(dismissively)
It does that sometimes. Don't let it bother you.

[Zelenka guides Sheppard away from Mila so they can speak privately.]

ZELENKA
Listen, what we are doing here is not safe.
SHEPPARD
What else is new?
ZELENKA
She is pushing the ship well past its boundaries.
SHEPPARD
That's kind of what we asked for, no? If we don't get Rodney and Daniel back before Todd gets there, they're goners!
ZELENKA
This is the crudest hyperdrive system I have ever encountered. It is cobbled together from mismatching components.
SHEPPARD
She's got to be pretty smart to put a hyperdrive together with mismatched parts.
ZELENKA
She's going to burn it up. Once we drop out hyperspace, there's a high possibility we won't be able to engage the drive again.
SHEPPARD
(dismissively)
Let's worry about that when we get there?

[An alarm starts to emit a high pitched rapid beeping.]

ZELENKA
What was that? What?

[Mila reaches over and shuts off the alarm.]

MILA
(unconcerned)
Just a radiation warning. The detector's a little oversensitive. I wouldn't worry about it.

Well, I'm not sure, but we did ruin the one plan they had to feel free in this galaxy. They might hold a grudge.
MCKAY
Well, let's…hope not.
DANIEL
(disappointed)
I don't think I'll be around to find out, though.
MCKAY
Yeah, I heard. They're, um, sending you back to Earth.
DANIEL
Yeah, I guess I need some major patching up and recovery, and Landry prefers that I do that there instead of here, so…
MCKAY
Well, the Daedalus has already picked up a 'gate, uh, leftover from the old Gate Bridge, and, uh, my team is installing it as we speak, so we should have you home in, uh, no time at all.
DANIEL
That's good. That is…good.
MCKAY
Yes. So…

[There is an awkward silence, only broken by McKay making distracted popping sounds with his mouth.]

MCKAY
Well, it's been, um…unique.
DANIEL
Yes. Yes, it has. It sure has. But, I do want to say thank you for, uh…saying that you respected me when you thought I was about to die back there.
MCKAY
(affronted)
Excuse me? I said no such thing.
DANIEL
Yeah, you did you were like, um: "um, I know it may not seem like it, but…I really respect you," and that means a great…deal to me.

[Daniel smiles.]

MCKAY
I don't believe I ever finished that sentence.

[Daniel's smile falters.]

DANIEL
How else would you have finished that sentence?
MCKAY
(stammering)
I would've said "regret." I would've said "I really regret you being here because none of this would've happened…otherwise."

[McKay shuffles uncomfortably.]

DANIEL
(incredulous)
So you can only give compliments to the dying?
MCKAY
(flinching)
It's something I'm working on.

[There is another brief, awkward silence. Daniel holds up his tray of food.]

DANIEL
Do you want to try any of this?
MCKAY
Ooh. Fries?
DANIEL
Yeah, yeah. Go—dive right in. Take the-take the deal there.

[McKay munches on some fries. Daniel reaches to the nearby table and gestures for Rodney to eat as many fries as he likes.]

FADE OUT

END CREDITS
