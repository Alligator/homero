SGA 5.12 "Outsiders" Episode Guide

Précis

The Balarans are survivors of Michaelâ€™s release of the modified Hoffan drug and have joined the village of another planet as a place of refuge where Dr. Beckett is working in a clinic. They considered their survival as a blessing until they learn that the Wraith are on their way to their new homeworld to wipe them out.

Transcript

PREVIOUSLY ON STARGATE ATLANTIS

[Scenes from 1.07 "Poisoning the Well", 4.18 "The Kindred Part 1", and 5.08 "The Queen" are shown.]

END EXCERPTS

TEASER

EXT—VILLAGE, DUSK

[A village of wooden and metal buildings sits at the foot of some hills. It appears to be at the level of early industrial development. Work carries on in the blacksmith's shop as Elson monitors progress. Several people go about their business in the village.]

INT—CLINIC

[Beckett uses his stethoscope to check a girl's breathing.]

BECKETT
Once more.

[The girl inhales and exhales. Beckett lowers the stethoscope and sighs.]

BECKETT
(deadpan)
Bad news, I'm afraid. You'll have to go back to school tomorrow.

[The girl giggles and Beckett pretends to snatch her nose, smiling. Beckett stands and moves over to Novo, who is standing nearby with a clipboard. They walk over to another man holding his side.]

BECKETT
Maintain the amoxicillin for three more days; that should do the trick. Now, what do we have over here? All these bandages need to be changed. Do you mind going back to the store room and getting some more bandages, please?
NOVO
Yes, Doctor.
BECKETT
Thank you.

[Nola leaves. Beckett pats the man on the shoulder.]

BECKETT
Besides that, you're looking good, my friend.
SHEPPARD
Mail call.

[Beckett spins to find Sheppard and his team entering the home, carrying supplies.]

BECKETT
Colonel! What are you all doing here?

[Sheppard and Teyla grin.]

SHEPPARD
Well, we heard you needed supplies, so we thought we'd come by and see how you were doing.

[Ronon and Beckett good-naturedly pretend to spar. McKay is in the rear, carrying a large duffel bag and looking dour.]

MCKAY
(caustically)
"A hop, skip, and a jump from the 'gate?" That's what you call that?
BECKETT
(grinning)
Nice to see you too, Rodney.
TEYLA
How are you, Doctor?
BECKETT
Well. Thanks. I take it these are the antibiotics I ordered?

[He takes a yellow case from Teyla.]

SHEPPARD
Doctor Keller gave you a whole whack of them. So they should last for a while.
BECKETT
(sighing)
Ah, God. I wish that were true.
RONON
What does that mean?
BECKETT
Well, I've been dealing with a number of infections from the Hoffan plague survivors. I can barely keep on top of it.
MCKAY
Survivors of the Hoffan plague? Here?
TEYLA
This is not one of the planets that Michael targeted.
BECKETT
No, but many of these people actually come from another planet, Balar. They managed to survive the plague, and just when they started getting the pieces back together, the Wraith showed up; tried to wipe them out.
SHEPPARD
(grimacing)
Mm. We heard rumors about that.
MCKAY
Well, the Wraith aren't too pleased to have whole populations out there that are immune to their feeding.
BECKETT
Just a handful of the Balarans survived. The locals were kind enough to take them in.

[Novo returns with the bandages.]

NOVO
Doctor?
BECKETT
Novo! These are my friends from Atlantis. Colonel Sheppard, Teyla, Ronon, and Doctor Rodney McKay. This is Novo. She's been helping me here in the clinic.

[McKay shoots Novo a winning smile and nods.]

SHEPPARD
Pleasure to meet you.
NOVO
You as well. I've heard a lot about you.
(to Beckett, indicating McKay)
Is he the one?
MCKAY
The one what?
NOVO
From all the funny stories.

[McKay's fixed smile falters. Sheppard and Teyla look amused.]

BECKETT
(eyes widening)
Right! Yeah, well, I hope you all can stay here for dinner. I mean, uh, the canteen's not fancy, but they do serve up one heckuva stew, made from a goat-like creature. Delicious!
MCKAY
"Goat-like?"
SHEPPARD
I think it's a great idea. Gives us a chance to catch up and relive all those…"funny stories."
BECKETT
Aye.

EXT—VILLAGE, DUSK

[Beckett and the team walk through the village.]

MCKAY
So…how's life in the wilds of Pegasus? You got to be missing Atlantis by now.
BECKETT
Surprisingly, no.
MCKAY
Oh, come on. The hot baths, Playstation…deodorant?

[McKay wrinkles his nose as they pass close by a villager.]

BECKETT
I know it seems a wee bit rough, but it's not. Really. I mean, wherever I go, people are kind to me. I never want for a meal or a place to sleep. And to be honest with you, I'm really starting to feel like I'm making a difference.

[McKay shrugs. In the distance, a woman screams. Ronon and Sheppard push Beckett and McKay out of the way as they race towards the noise.]

[A patrol of Wraith come marching into the village. Sheppard turns the corner then immediately dives back into hiding, holding back the others who try and see what he's spotted.]

MCKAY
(whispering)
What is it?
SHEPPARD
(low)
Wraith.

END TEASER

OPENING CREDITS

EXT—VILLAGE, DUSK

[The villagers stare as the Wraith arrive in the main square. Sheppard's team remain in hiding, watching and waiting.]

RONON
(whispering)
I count nine of them. We can take 'em.
SHEPPARD
(low, but firm)
No. There's too many civilians.
TEYLA
I does not appear to be an ordinary culling.
MCKAY
How do you figure that?
TEYLA
Well, for one thing, there are no darts. And they do not seem to be attacking the villagers.

[The soldiers part to reveal a lead Wraith standing in their midst.]

WRAITH
Who among you speaks for the rest?

[Elson exchanges looks with another villager.]

ELSON
(calling out)
I am Elson…leader of this village. What do you want with us?
WRAITH
You have recently taken in a group of refugees from another planet. They are survivors of a plague. It is important that you turn them over to me immediately.
ELSON
Why?
WRAITH
The reasons why do not concern you. Provided you cooperate, you have my word we will leave peacefully.

[Elson looks to Jervis and Renni.]

ELSON
And if we don't?
WRAITH
(stepping forward)
That would be a very unwise decision. Our hive will be here before the night is through. If you have not complied by then, I will give the word, and your entire village will be destroyed.
(louder)
Turn over these outsiders, and your lives will be spared. Resist, and you will all die together. The choice is yours.

[The Wraith and his soldiers leave.]

INT—CLINIC DINING ROOM

[Sheppard's team, Novo, and Beckett sit around a table.]

NOVO
I don't understand. They've already destroyed our homeworld. Why would they hunt the rest of us down as well?
SHEPPARD
(to Beckett)
You didn't tell them?
BECKETT
Not the whole story.
NOVO
What are you talking about?
BECKETT
Novo, remember when I told you that, as a plague survivor, you'd be immune to the Wraith feeding?

[Novo nods.]

BECKETT
Well, there's a bit more to it than that. Any Wraith who tries to feed on you or anyone like you will, as a result, die instantly.

[Novo stares at him in shock.]

RONON
You're poison to them.
NOVO
Then we're as good as dead.
SHEPPARD
Not necessarily.
NOVO
They won't tolerate our presence anywhere—not if what you say is true!
TEYLA
Just because they found you on this world does not mean that there is nowhere you can hide.

[Sefaris enters with some others.]

SEFARIS
Novo? Did you hear?
NOVO
Yes. This is Sefaris. He's also from Balar.
SEFARIS
The councillors are meeting to discuss whether or not to heed the Wraith's request.
BECKETT
(standing)
What? Where?
SEFARIS
The mine office. I tried to get in, but they turned me away.
SHEPPARD
Ronon, Teyla, scope out the 'gate. (to McKay and Beckett)
You two stay here, watch these people.
(to Sefaris)
Can you show me where this mine office is?
SEFARIS
Yes.
BECKETT
Colonel, what are you going to say to them?
SHEPPARD
I'm going to give them another way out.

INT—MINE OFFICE

[Sheppard speaks to Elson and the other councillors.]

ELSON
You're telling us that, as a result of this plague, the Balarans are now deadly to the Wraith?
SHEPPARD
Pretty much.
ELSON
So…the Wraith would never risk culling this village.
SHEPPARD
Mmm, that's true, but that's also why they'd be willing to wipe you guys out completely. A village that can't be culled's no good to them.
JERVIS
Did they know?
SHEPPARD
What?
JERVIS
Did the Balarans know they were bringing this curse upon us when they came here?
SHEPPARD
No, they had no idea.
RENNI
It's not their fault, Jervis!
JERVIS
Maybe not, but that doesn't matter. The Wraith won't rest until every last plague survivor in this galaxy is dead.
SHEPPARD
Except the Wraith have no idea who they are unless people like you point them out.

[Jervis bristles.]

ELSON
I'm not happy about this either, Colonel. But what other choice do we have?
SHEPPARD
Well…we may be able to move all your people…to a different planet where they'll be safe.
RENNI
You mean leave our homes?
SHEPPARD
Look, I know it's not ideal, but the only other choice is to turn in a bunch of innocent people. And I know you guys aren't going to do that, right?
JERVIS
Well, as the Wraith said, they're outsiders.
ELSON
(admonishing)
Jervis, we agreed to let them come here.
JERVIS
I didn't agree. Besides, they weren't a threat at the time. Now that's changed.
RENNI
(crying)
But they've become like family. Novo lives under my roof. She eats at my table. My daughter adores her! What am I supposed to tell her?
JERVIS
You'd prefer we all be killed?
TEYLA
(breathless, over radio)
Colonel Sheppard, come in.
SHEPPARD
Excuse me.

[He turns away and checks his earpiece is secured.]

SHEPPARD
(into radio)
Go ahead.

EXT—FOREST, NIGHT

[Teyla and Ronon are hidden in foliage.]

TEYLA
Ronon and I are at the 'gate. It does not appear to be heavily guarded.
RONON
It's the same guys from the village. I recognize the commander.

INT—MINING OFFICE

SHEPPARD
What are the odds of you two getting by them?

EXT—FOREST, NIGHT

RONON
Pretty good.

[Teyla shoots a glare at Ronon.]

TEYLA
Slim, actually. But much better if you and Rodney join us.

INT—MINING OFFICE

SHEPPARD
Yeah, all right. Hold your position until we can get there.

[He turns off his radio and heads back to the councillors.]

ELDON
What is it?
SHEPPARD
How many people are in this village?
ELDON
Uh, six-seven hundred.
SHEPPARD
Well, we'll be tight for time, but we can manage.
ELDON
What are you suggesting?
SHEPPARD
We take the Wraith out, get everybody through the' gate before the hive ship comes. You should be safe in Atlantis until we can relocate you.
JERVIS
Unacceptable.
RENNI
The Balarans don't deserve this. They did nothing but survive a sickness. It could just as easily have happened to us. If we turn these people over, we are no better than the Wraith ourselves.
SHEPPARD
Look, even if you cooperate with the Wraith, there's no guarantee they're going to keep their word. They may kill all of you for being an inconvenience.
ELDON
All right. What do you want us to do?
SHEPPARD
Be ready to move. Take only what you can carry.

INT—CLINIC

[Sheppard enters.]

SHEPPARD
Let's go, Rodney. We're going to hit the Wraith.
MCKAY
We are?
BECKETT
I'll come with you.
SHEPPARD
Not a chance, Doc.
BECKETT
Colonel—
SHEPPARD
Look, I don't have time to argue. Besides, you've gotta get these people ready for transport. Once the 'gate's secure, we're going to have to move fast.

[Sheppard heads out, McKay following.]

BECKETT
(calling after them)
Okay, but be careful.

EXT—FOREST, NIGHT

RONON
All right, if we're going to attack, we gotta do it from over there.
TEYLA
Agreed.

[They move through the foliage to another position. Before they reach it, Ronon spots something and signals Teyla to get down. They hide in the foliage and watch more Wraith soldiers march by. The Wraith commander with them pauses, Teyla raises her weapon. The Wraith continues on.]

EXT—VILLAGE, NIGHT

[Sheppard and McKay head out of the village.]

MCKAY
As soon as we take the 'gate, we should call in some jumpers. It'll make evacuating the village a lot faster.
SHEPPARD
Not to mention we can use the extra firepower in case the hive gets here before we're done.
MCKAY
Oh, let's hope it doesn't come to that.
TEYLA
(breathless, over radio)
Colonel Sheppard, come in.
SHEPPARD
(into radio)
Yeah, go ahead.

INT—FOREST, NIGHT

TEYLA
We have a problem. More of the Wraith have begun arriving back. They must have been out on patrol.

EXT—VILLAGE, NIGHT

SHEPPARD
(into radio)
How many? Can we still get out?
TEYLA
(over radio)
No.

EXT—FOREST, NIGHT

TEYLA
(into radio)
For the time being, we're stuck here.

EXT—VILLAGE, NIGHT

[Sheppard and McKay exchange worried looks.]

EXT—VILLAGE, NIGHT

INT—MINING OFFICE

[Sheppard and McKay have rejoined the villagers.]

JERVIS
You said you could get us out.
SHEPPARD
Well, there are more Wraith than we thought. The 'gate's too heavily guarded.
RENNI
(frightened)
So we're trapped?
SHEPPARD
For the moment. I haven't given up hope. I just need time to develop a new strategy.
JERVIS
What strategy do you propose we use to defeat an army of Wraith?
SHEPPARD
(snappish)
Well, I don't know. I haven't thought of it yet! Look, worst-case scenario, my team and I don't show up, the people back on Atlantis will dial the 'gate and contact us by radio. I can order backup.
ELDON
Will that happen before the hive ship arrives?
SHEPPARD
(conceding)
Probably not.
RENNI
We can't wait that long.
SHEPPARD
For all we know, the Wraith could be lying about the ship.
JERVIS
And why would they do that?
SHEPPARD
(angrily)
I'm just saying, we need to stall as long as possible.
JERVIS
This is madness. Elson, we have to think of the safety of our people.
SHEPPARD
Oh, I'm thinking about the safety of your people. All your people!
JERVIS
(ignoring Sheppard)
We can't rely on their help! We have to make our own decisions.
ELSON
I need time to think about this.
JERVIS
The time for that is past, Elson. We have to act! And if you're not prepared to do so, then I will.

[He storms out.]

RENNI
Jervis!
SHEPPARD
What's he going to do?
ELSON
I don't know.

INT—CLINIC

[Sheppard arrives with Elson. Beckett and McKay are the only ones there aside from the seriously illy Balarans.]

SHEPPARD
Where's Novo and the others?
MCKAY
We sent them home. Why?
SHEPPARD
(gesturing to the infirm)
Can these people walk?
BECKETT
Well, if need be, but…I thought we couldn't get them through the 'gate?
SHEPPARD
Well, we can't for the time being. But…I don't think they're safe here either.

EXT—BLACKSMITH SHOP, NIGHT

[Jervis meets some workers at the blacksmith's shop.]

JERVIS
Grab your weapons! Come with me.

[Someone tosses Jervis a shotgun. Others are armed with flintlocks, scythes, pitchforks, and other weapons. They start storming the village, grabbing Balarans, whether they be men, women, or children, and herding them together. Jervis heads to Renni's house. She blocks their path.]

WOMAN
No, no! Please, please don't kill me!
JERVIS
Get out of my way.
RENNI
She's not here, Jervis.

[Jervis pushes her aside and bursts in. No one is there.]

JERVIS
Where is she?

EXT—VILLAGE, NIGHT

[Novo and Sefaris hide in the village outskirts as Jervis's men walk past.]

SEFARIS
What are we going to do, Novo?
NOVO
We have to get out of the village.

[They start to leave but find their way barred by some of Jervis's men. They turn to run the other way, but are blocked in and surrounded.]

INT—CLINIC

[Teyla and Ronon arrive. The others are packing.]

RONON
What the hell's going on out there?
SHEPPARD
Some of the villagers decided to take matters into their own hands.
RONON
Well, let's go stop them.
SHEPPARD
First things first. We've got to move these people. Grab some gear.

[Teyla and Ronon start to help. The door bangs open and Jervis and some of his men enter with their guns. Sheppard's team raise their own weapons in a standoff.]

JERVIS
We don't want any trouble, just hand over the Balarans.
SHEPPARD
I don't think so.
JERVIS
Don't make things difficult, Colonel. You had your chance to save the village; now it's my turn.
RONON
(scornfully)
Saving the village? That's what you call this?
JERVIS
This isn't your affair! It's up to us to decide how to deal with the situation.
ELSON
(calm but stern)
Yes, it is. But, Jervis, this is not our way.
JERVIS
Our way?! Our way is what got us into this situation in the first place! We've already given them more than they could have ever asked for.
BECKETT
All right. Why not just shoot them right now, and get it over with? It's a death sentence anyway.
(he steps forward, to point blank range of Jervis's gun)
Shoot them yourself, and spare them the torment of being tortured by the Wraith. You'd be doing them a favor!
TEYLA
(warning)
Carson!
BECKETT
Go on! Kill them all then! If you're that desperate to be done with them!

[The Balarans stare in fear. Jervis finally lowers his gun. Elson takes it. The others also lower their guns.]

JERVIS
(sullenly)
You're making a big mistake.
ELSON
Maybe. But at least we do so with a clear conscience.
JERVIS
It doesn't matter anyway.
SHEPPARD
What are you talking about?
JERVIS
My men are already on the way to the 'gate with the first group we captured. It's too late.

[Sheppard looks murderous.]

EXT—STARGATE AREA, NIGHT

[Wraith patrol the area. The Balarans are gathered in a huddle.]

SEFARIS
What are they going to do to us?
NOVO
Be calm, Sefaris, you're scaring the others.
SEFARIS
Maybe they should be scared. We both know there's no escape from this.
NOVO
We cannot give up hope.
SEFARIS
Face it, Novo…we've been betrayed. They don't want us here, they never did. We were doomed the moment we had to leave our homes. And to think, as a survivor, I used to consider myself one of the lucky ones.

EXT—VILLAGE, NIGHT

[Teyla and Ronon meet up with McKay and Sheppard in the village.]

SHEPPARD
Jervis taken care of?
TEYLA
He and his men have been locked up. They will not be giving us any more trouble. Though I do not think it was necessary to stun them.

[She shoots Ronon a look.]

RONON
(unrepentent)
Felt good, though.
MCKAY
So what now?
SHEPAPRD
We've got to get the rest of the Balarans out of the village. There's no telling if anybody else might feel the way Jervis does.
TEYLA
Where should we take them?
RONON
The forest?
SHEPPARD
(reluctant)
It's kind of a long haul at night.
MCKAY
Wait a minute. What about the mine? These tracks lead right down to it. I mean, we just follow them.
RONON
No one gets lost.
MCKAY
Exactly.
SHEPPARD
(to Ronon and Teyla)
All right. Go. Get Beckett and his patients. Bring them here. We're round up everyone else.

EXT—SPACE

[A hive ship drops out of hyperspace and heads to the planet.]

EXT—STARGATE AREA, NIGHT

[The commanders begin to whisper to each other.]

NOVO
What is it?
SEFARIS
Something's happening.
(calling to Wraith)
What's going on?
WRAITH
You'll be moving shortly.
NOVO
Where?
WRAITH
Your new home. The hive ship has just arrived.

[The Wraith starts to walk away.]

SEFARIS
Wait. I have a proposition for you.
NOVO
What are you doing, Sefaris?
SEFARIS
Something we should've done a long time ago.
WRAITH
There is nothing you have to offer me.
SEFARIS
Don't be so sure. There's someone hiding in the village. He's of far greater value than all of us put together.
NOVO
(desperate pleading)
Sefaris, no!
SEFARIS
Spare our lives. I'll take you to him.
WRAITH
(smirking)
A fair attempt…but the answer's no.
SEFARIS
My life then.
NOVO
(disgusted)
How could you do this?
SEFARIS
I promise it'll be worth your while.

[The Wraith looks intrigued.]

EXT—VILLAGE MINE PATH BEGINNING, NIGHT

[Teyla guides villagers to join the others walking along the path. She joins Sheppard and Ronon.]

TEYLA
I believe that is everybody.
RONON
Where's McKay and Beckett?
SHEPPARD
(into radio)
Rodney, what's your status?

INT—CLINIC

[McKay watches Beckett frantically pack more into the full cases.]

MCKAY
(exasperated)
Still at the clinic. Carson decided to pick up a few more supplies.
SHEPPARD
(over radio)
Tell him to get a move on. We can't stay here forever.
BECKETT
(into radio)
Go ahead, Colonel. We'll catch up.

[He throws a duffel over his shoulder.]

SHEPPARD
(over radio)
You sure?
BECKETT
(into radio)
Absolutely. We'll be right behind you.

EXT—MINE PATH BEGINNING, NIGHT

SHEPPARD
Let's get going. Stay together. Follow the tracks.

[The group follow the mine cart tracks towards the mine.]

INT—CLINIC

BECKETT
There. That should do it. Rodney, can you give me a hand here?
MCKAY
Remind me to bring a sherpa the next time I visit.

[McKay helps Beckett gather up the cases. The door opens. Sefaris enters, followed by Wraith. Sefaris points to Beckett.]

SEFARIS
Him. He's the one.

[The Wraith stun McKay and Beckett before they can recover from their surprise.]

SEFARIS
You see? I kept up my end of the bargain.
WRAITH
So you did. Unfortunately, in light of your…condition, keeping you alive is far too risky.

[He points a dagger blade at Sefaris's throat.]

INT—MINE

[The Balarans are led into the mine by Elson. Sheppard, Teyla, and Ronon are in the rear of the evacuation. They can hear distant engines overhead.]

RONON
Darts.
TEYLA
(sighing, concerned)
Rodney and Carson are still not here.
SHEPPARD
(into radio)
Rodney, come in. McKay!
(to Ronon and Teyla)
You guys keep moving. I'm going to go get him.

[Sheppard doubles back at a jog while the others continue down the mine.]

EXT—VILLAGE OUTSKIRTS

[Sheppard reaches the village just in time to spot McKay and Beckett sitting back to back unconscious, surrounded by Wraith. A dart flies overhead, catching them all in its culling beam. Sheppard stares at the empty spot in horror.]

INT—MINE

SHEPPARD
(over radio)
Ronon? Teyla?
TEYLA
(keying radio)
Go ahead.

[The conversation switches from the mine to Sheppard's position in the village.]

SHEPPARD
The Wraith just got Beckett and McKay.
RONON
What? How?
SHEPPARD
Took 'em up in a dart. I think they're on their way up to a hive.
TEYLA
How did the Wraith know that they were in the village?
SHEPPARD
I think I got an idea.

EXT—SPACE

[The hive ship floats in orbit above the planet.]

INT—HIVE SHIP BRIG

[Beckett awakens with a groan.]

BECKETT
Ooh…oh.

[Looking around, he becomes instantly alert—sitting up and shaking the unconscious McKay.]

BECKETT
Rodney…Rodney, wake up!
MCKAY
(groaning)
Oh…God, is it me, or have they juiced up the stun weapons?
(he looks around)
Oh, and they brought us to the hive. That's great.
BECKETT
(panicking)
What are we going to do?
MCKAY
All right, uh, just don't—don't panic. Don't panic. I have…got lots of experience escaping from these things.

[He stands, groaning as he speaks, and joins Beckett at the door.]

BECKETT
You do?
MCKAY
Oh, yeah. Yeah. I've been in this situation dozens of times. Always figured a way out.
BECKETT
How exactly?
MCKAY
Uh, let's see, uh…Last time, uh, Teyla was posing as a queen, so, um…You know, she…had us released.
BECKETT
Not much chance of that happening.
MCKAY
No, I guess not.
BECKETT
How about the time before that?
MCKAY
Ronon's friend Tyre was pretending to be in league with the Wraith, but really he was on our side, so…he let us out.
BECKETT
I'm starting to sense a pattern here. You've actually never really escaped from one of these cells on your own, have you?
MCKAY
(weakly)
First time for everything, right?
BECKETT
(huffing)
Oh, God, Rodney.
MCKAY
Well, just…give me a minute. I'll think of something.

[McKay starts to pace. Beckett looks down the hall.]

BECKETT
I hate to pressure you, Rodney, but I don't think we have a minute.

[McKay turns. The Wraith and a patrol of soldiers approach. The door opens.]

WRAITH
Bring them.

INT—WRAITH LAB
