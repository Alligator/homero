TEASER

INT—QUEEN'S ROOM ON WRAITH SHIP

[Following on from 2.10 "The Lost Boys", Sheppard is on his knees, being interrogated by the Wraith Queen. She walks around him before speaking.]

QUEEN
The ship…where did you get it?

[She uses mind control to force him to answer.]

SHEPPARD
(struggling not to speak)
You mean the Dart? We call them Darts 'cause they're so pointy.
QUEEN
Tell me. Where?
SHEPPARD
I really don't want to say.

[She hits him in the shoulder. Then she caresses his face.]

SHEPPARD
I don't even know your name.
QUEEN
In time…you will tell me every—

[The Queen stops talking and hisses. She leaves abruptly.]

SHEPPARD
"Every" what? What's wrong?

[Two Wraith take him by the arms and start to escort him out.]

SHEPPARD
She didn't even tell me her name.

INT—WRAITH CELL

[Ronon, Teyla, Ford and his men are waiting when Sheppard arrives with his Wraith guards. They open the cell and push him in. He falls to the floor.]

TEYLA
Colonel? Are you unharmed?
SHEPPARD
(sitting up)
Yeah.
RONON
Where'd they take you?
SHEPPARD
A little Q & A with the queen of the Hive.
FORD
What'd you tell her?
SHEPPARD
It was a short conversation. She walked out.
FORD
Why?
SHEPPARD
I don't know. Maybe she was late for a meeting.
TEYLA

SHEPPARD
Why?

EXT—SPACE

[A Hive ship exits hyperspace just beside the first ship.]

INT—WRAITH CELL

TEYLA
Another Hive ship has arrived.

MCKAY
Lock and load.

[McKay attacks the two minders without warning. He throws the bouncer across the room.]

BOUNCER
Ah!

[McKay breaks a bottle over the other one's head then punches him, knocking him down.]

MCKAY
And that's what happens when you back a brilliant scientist into a corner!

[He turns and gets punched by the bouncer. McKay kicks him in the groin, knocking him to the ground.]

MCKAY
Focus. Focus…

[McKay searches through drawers and finds the crystals.]

MCKAY
Control crystals.

[He takes them and leaves, breathing heavily.]

[The two Hives move through sub-space.]

[The team rushes along. Teyla stops them.]

TEYLA

I got it.

Ford!

Move!

This way.

[McKay works on the DHD.]

MCKAY
(talking to himself in a high-pitched, nervous voice)

Like a hot knife through Jell-O. No, wait. Butter. Hot knife through butter. Oh, man, am I hungry. Okay, shut up, Rodney. Focus. Must finish dialing. Please, please work, please please please.

[The gate engages.]

MCKAY
Okay, go home.

[Sheppard and the others find humans wrapped in Wraith webs. One calls for help.]

NEERA
Help us…please…help us.
SHEPPARD
She's alive.

So is this one.
SHEPPARD
Let's get them out of here.

[They start to free the humans.]

We don't have time to stop. This isn't the mission.

There is no mission, Ford. There's just your stupid plan.

If you hadn't screwed up—
SHEPPARD
Look, the sooner you stop arguing with me and help me save these people, the quicker we get out of here.

INT—ATLANTIS GATE CONTROL ROOM

[The Stargate is active.]

TECHNICIAN
Unscheduled activation! Doctor McKay's IDC.
WEIR
Lower the shield.

INT—ATLANTIS GATE ROOM

[McKay comes rushing through the 'gate.]

MCKAY
(holding up one arm)
I know what I need to do!
WEIR
Rodney? What's happened? Hey, where are the rest of—

[McKay is sweating and speaks in a rambling fashion.]

MCKAY
There's no time, no time. The planet, not Ford's, the one the ship's headed towards we need to get there and don't have much time. I was barely able to escape myself, but I managed to take out the guards. Oh, you should've seen me. I was amazing. I wish we got it on camera, because—that's not the point—
WEIR
Rodney, slow down. Are you all right?
MCKAY
Yes. Yes. I mean, I don't know. I mean I had to take out the guards, and they were huge and dumb and stupid—

Ford's guards. Didn't I mention Ford?
WEIR
No, you didn't. You haven't mentioned Sheppard, Teyla, or Ronon either.

Yes! They were there too, and there were the two guards. They were huge and massive, and I had to take them out, so I had to inject some of the enzyme.
WEIR
You took some of the enzyme?
MCKAY
No, no, no, no, no no, no. I didn't take some of the enzyme. I took a lot of the enzyme, because I had to, because I had to take out the guards, which I did. And you should've seen me. I was amazing!
WEIR
Are you insane?
MCKAY
Yes. Yes. Now that I've taken the enzyme, yes!
WEIR
Rodney, focus. Where is Colonel Sheppard?
MCKAY
No, no, no, I had to take the enzyme, because…because I had to take out the guards! But that's not the point. The point is we don't have enough time. We need to stop the ship from getting to where the ship is going.

Come on, come on, let's take a walk down to the infirmary. All right?
MCKAY
Where are we going? What are you doing? No, no, no, no, no. I don't want to go to the infirmary. I want to go to the, uh…the um…

WEIR

Dr. Beckett, we have an emergency. We're headed to you.

Cold turkey.

Yes.

Sure. Why not? I'm sure we can find a spare room.
NEERA
I'd like that. What is it like, your world?
SHEPPARD
It's nice. Comfortable. Good climate.
NEERA
(smiling)
Tell me what it looks like.
SHEPPARD
Like every other world, I suppose. Trees, water, mountains… Starbucks on every corner.
NEERA
What is it called?
SHEPPARD
(hesitating)
Do you mind if we pick this up after I get some sleep?
NEERA
Yes. Of course.

[She settles down, putting her hand over the middle of his chest. He does not close his eyes.]

[Later, they are both sleeping. The ship makes a metallic clanking sound and shudders, waking them up.]

NEERA
What is it?
SHEPPARD
I don't know. Must have just dropped out of hyperspace. Maybe they're getting ready for the culling.

[They stand up. The Wraith arrives and grabs Sheppard, shoving him at the Wraith soldiers.]

WRAITH
Take him.

INT—QUEEN'S ROOM ON WRAITH SHIP

[Sheppard stands in the middle of the chamber. The Queen enters.]

QUEEN
Shall we talk now?
SHEPPARD
About what?
QUEEN
You are stubborn.
SHEPPARD
Yes, I am…and, uh…she's very pretty, by the way.

[The Queen looks at him questioningly.]

SHEPPARD
Neera. The girl you planted in the cocoon for us to rescue, after you let us escape. The one that was supposed to get information from me. That's never going to happen.

[The Queen hisses at him.]

EXT—SPACE

[Daedalus flies through hyperspace.]

INT—DAEDALUS BRIDGE

[Caldwell briefs his crew, including McKay and Lorne. He uses a lighted board to illustrate the plan.]

CALDWELL
We'll drop out of hyperspace directly behind the planet. Use it to shield us from the Hive ship, where you're going to attempt radio contact with Colonel Sheppard.
MCKAY
We won't be able to make radio contact from behind the planet.
CALDWELL
Which is why you and Major Lorne will take a cloaked puddle jumper and get close enough to determine if Colonel Sheppard and his team are still aboard the ship. If they are, we'll move into position around the planet and beam them out.
LORNE
And if they're not there?
CALDWELL
We'll beam a nuke aboard the ship and destroy it.
MCKAY
What if they jam us like they did before?
CALDWELL
Doctor you worry about getting Colonel Sheppard and his team out of that ship. Let me worry about destroying it, okay?

[McKay and Lorne exchange looks.]

INT—QUEEN'S ROOM ON WRAITH SHIP

[Neera enters the chamber.]

SHEPPARD
Oh, there you are. I guess that empty room is off the table, but nice try back there in the cell.
(to Queen)
I knew there must have been a good reason for you to let us break out, but that was quite the performance. Where'd you find her?
QUEEN
(walking around Sheppard)
She is one of many thousands that have come to serve us…even worship us. They do my bidding…and I let them live.

[The Wraith caresses Neera's face.]

SHEPPARD
A Wraith worshiper, huh? Wow.
NEERA
The prophecy of the great awakening has come to pass. The end days have come to the mortal—
SHEPPARD
Yeah, yeah, yeah, I know about that, because I'm one, too. Although in my case, it's not so much worship as it is a meaningful business relationship.
QUEEN
You lie.
SHEPPARD
Okay, tell me this. How else could I get my hands on a Dart? And who else could provide me with specific instructions on how to infiltrate your Hive and destroy it?
QUEEN
Of which queen do you speak?
SHEPPARD
You got to have a window on this boat, don't you? Take a look. The other Hive that joined you for the culling. A lot more people to feed on when you don't have to share.
QUEEN
I will speak to her of this.
SHEPPARD
Well, you don't really think she'd admit to trying to have you killed, do you?
QUEEN
Enough!

[The Queen forces Sheppard to his knees, mentally.]

QUEEN
I shall savor your defiance.

[The Queen is shot with a stun weapon from behind, and cries out. Sheppard turns to see it's Ford. Ford shoots two soldiers and tosses Sheppard Ronon's gun. The Queen fires at Ford, but he doesn't fall. Sheppard fires at the Queen, knocking her out. Neera and other Wraith worshippers run out.]

SHEPPARD
How the hell did you get our weapons back?

[They run out and make their way down a corridor.]

FORD
That was the easy part. Getting some more enzyme, that was hard.

[Sheppard takes out a soldier.]

SHEPPARD
You are one tough son of a bitch.

[Ford stops.]

SHEPPARD
What are you doing?
FORD
Down that hall. Fifty meters. Turn left. It'll take you to Teyla and Ronon's cell. I'll hold the Wraith back for you.
SHEPPARD
(walking back to Ford)
You're coming with me.
FORD
I'll hold the Wraith back, boss. Now, go. I'll be okay.
SHEPPARD
I'm not going without you, Lieutenant.
FORD
John, I'll catch up. Now, go!

[Ford shoves Sheppard and runs off.]

SHEPPARD
Ford!

[A Wraith alarm starts screeching.]

SHEPPARD
Oh, crap.

INT—WRAITH CELL

RONON
Get ready.

[Sheppard arrives.]

SHEPPARD
Hey.
RONON
Hey.
SHEPPARD
How about I bust you guys out of here?
RONON
If you don't mind.

[Sheppard fires at the door panel, destroying it.]

SHEPPARD
I've got to get myself one of these. Where's Kanayo?

[He hands Ronon back his gun. Ronon picks up his coat from inside the cell.]

TEYLA
He is dead. Ford?
SHEPPARD
I don't know. Last I saw, he was alive. I'm sticking with that.

[They rush down the corridor.]

EXT—SPACE

[DAEDALUS drops out of hyperspace above a planet.]

INT—DAEDALUS BRIDGE

CALDWELL
(into radio)
Jumper One, you have a go.

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

[Lorne is at the controls, McKay in the other front seat.]

LORNE
On our way. Engaging cloak.

EXT—SPACE

[The Puddle Jumper leaves the Daedalus hangar bay. After a moment, it disappears, cloaked.]

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

[The heads-up display lights up with two red circles.]

LORNE
Oh, no.
MCKAY
There's two of them!
LORNE
You said there was only one.
MCKAY
Well, there was only one.
LORNE
Now there's two!
MCKAY
I can see that!
LORNE
Let's try and hail Colonel Sheppard.

INT—WRAITH SHIP CORRIDOR

[The three race down the corridor. The alarm is still sounding. They arrive at the entrance to the hangar bay. Many Darts are leaving the hangar.]

TEYLA
The culling is beginning.
SHEPPARD
We gotta stop them.
RONON
How?
SHEPPARD
Stay here. When I give you my signal, get out in the open.

[Sheppard goes into the hangar.]

INT—DAEDALUS BRIDGE

CALDWELL
(into radio)
Jumper One, what's your status?
LORNE
(over radio)
We are unable to establish a radio link with Colonel Sheppard or the others on either of the Hive ships.

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

MCKAY
(into radio)
Which doesn't necessarily mean they're not on either of the ships. They could've had their radios taken from them.

INT—DAEDALUS BRIDGE

CALDWELL
(into radio)
We can't make that assumption. Return to the Daedalus immediately.

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

MCKAY
(into radio, disappointed)
We're almost there now.

INT—DAEDALUS BRIDGE

CALDWELL
(into radio)
Good. We need to take those ships out before they start culling.

INT—WRAITH SHIP CORRIDOR

[Ronon and Teyla watch the hangar activity. A Dart turns and heads toward them.]

TEYLA
Is that his signal?
RONON
It'd better be.

[They go into the hangar and are swept up by a culling beam.]

INT—DART

SHEPPARD
Oh, I sure hope that worked.

EXT—SPACE

[Dozens of Darts leave the hangar bay of both Hive ships.]

[On the other side of the planet, Daedalus gets underway.]

INT—DAEDALUS BRIDGE

[McKay and Lorne arrive back on the bridge.]

CALDWELL
Max power to the shields. Are we within transport range?
AIRMAN
Yes, sir. We have a weapons lock on coordinates inside the first enemy vessel.
CALDWELL
Deploy warhead.

[The display shows the transport failed.]

AIRMAN
The enemy has engaged countermeasures to block our transport.
MCKAY
They're jamming us again.
AIRMAN
We're under fire.

[The ship is taking fire.]

CALDWELL
Forward rail positions. Return fire.

EXT—SPACE

[Both Hives fire on Daedalus, which fires back at both with the forward rail guns.]

INT—DART

[The Dart display shows many ships in the area.]

SHEPPARD
This is not good. Not good. Not good.

EXT—SPACE

[Sheppard's Dart turns toward the side of one of the Hives.]

INT—DAEDALUS BRIDGE

LORNE
How much of this can the shields take?
MCKAY
Not much. We're already down twenty percent.

[Sparks begin flying around the bridge.]

CALDWELL
Our people have been in Wraith hands too long. We have to assume they've been compromised and the Wraith know Atlantis wasn't destroyed. We need to win this.

INT—DART

[Sheppard brings up the weapons display.]

EXT—SPACE

[Sheppard's Dart does a strafing run on the Hive ship, hitting it in the hangar bay and other sections.]

INT—DAEDALUS BRIDGE

[McKay and the others watch the battle out the forward view-screen. They see the explosions on the Hive.]

MCKAY
Look. You see that?
LORNE
What?
MCKAY
It's a Dart firing on one of the Hive ships.
CALDWELL
Why?
MCKAY
(realizing)
Sheppard!

INT—DART

[Sheppard keeps firing.]

EXT—SPACE

[The Darts from the two Hives are firing on each other and each other's Hives.]

INT—DART

[Sheppard keeps flying.]

INT—DAEDALUS BRIDGE

[The display shows massive fire between the Hives.]

CALDWELL
I don't believe what I'm seeing.
LORNE
Sheppard's actually getting them to fire at each other.

EXT—SPACE

[The two Hives fire on each other at very close range. One of the Hives explodes, and the blast takes out the second Hive, resulting in a huge fireball.]

INT—DAEDALUS BRIDGE

[They have to look away from the brightness of the explosion. McKay looks devastated. He rushes to his tactical display.]

MCKAY
(shocked)
The blast has destroyed everything in the fifteen-thousand-meter radius.
CALDWELL
Did any Darts survive?
AIRMAN
Negative, sir.

[McKay steps back to the fore of the bridge, shocked and devastated.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

TECHNICIAN
(into radio)
Copy that. Atlantis out.

[He gets up and goes to find Weir in her office.]

TECHNICIAN
Dr. Weir. We received word from Daedalus. They said they should be back to Atlantis by 1900 hours.
WEIR
They called the search off?
TECHNICIAN
They said there was nothing to search for.
WEIR
(subdued)
Thank you.

[Weir turns away in shock. The Stargate begins to activate.]

TECHNICIAN
Off-world activation!
WEIR
Who is it?
TECHNICIAN
We have an I.D.C. It's Colonel Sheppard's code.
WEIR
Let 'em in!

[Weir rushes down the stairs to the 'gate room.]

INT—ATLANTIS GATE ROOM

[Sheppard, Ronon, and Teyla walk through the active wormhole. Weir jogs over to meet them.]

SHEPPARD
Sorry we're late.

[Everyone smiles.]

EXT—ATLANTIS PIER, NIGHT

[The Daedalus lands on the pier.]

INT—ATLANTIS INFIRMARY

[Beckett examines Ronon, while Teyla sits on the next bed. Sheppard stands between them, and Weir is nearby.]

BECKETT
All right, up you go.

[He helps Ronon sit up as McKay and Caldwell enter.]

MCKAY
Why aren't you dead?
SHEPPARD
It's good to see you, too, Rodney.
MCKAY
No, no, I mean, well you know what I mean. Why aren't you…dead?
SHEPPARD
Well, I knew when the Hives started to shoot at each other, it was just a matter of time before they blew each other up, so I went to the space gate and dialed an address where I could land safely. I didn't think the Dart could fit in our gate room.
TEYLA
The Colonel was kind enough to make us whole again, and we returned home.
CALDWELL
I'm curious, Sheppard. How did you know the Wraith would fight each other?
SHEPPARD
Uh, a little intel from Ford and the gang, plus I kind of goosed things along with the queen.

[McKay appears confused and a bit amazed.]

TEYLA
It would appear the Wraith are becoming more territorial than we had thought. There is definite tension building among them.
RONON
Which is good for us.
WEIR
It certainly is. It also opens a brand-new strategy in fighting them.
MCKAY
Huh. And where is Ford?
TEYLA
He was aboard the Hive ship when it was destroyed.
SHEPPARD
He was last seen aboard the Hive ship.
MCKAY
What does that mean?
SHEPPARD
Well, you know Ford. I wouldn't be surprised if we run into him again.

[Sheppard appears hopeful but unconvinced.]

FADE OUT

END CREDITS
