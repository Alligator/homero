SGA 2.10 "The Lost Boys Part 1" Episode Guide

Précis

As they are exploring a new planet, Sheppard and his crew find Lt. Aidan Ford living in a cave under the influence of a powerful drug and planning revenge on the Wraith.

Guide | Transcript

Well, I've managed to mine about fifty addresses but I have no idea if they're correct or what order they were dialed in.
LORNE
Fifty? That's a lot of planets to check.
ZELENKA
Yes, and that's assuming that they're correct.
LORNE
Okay, so long story short?
ZELENKA
It's going to be next to impossible to find Colonel Sheppard and his team based on what I can get from this DHD.
LORNE
Great. Okay, pack it up. Let's take what you got. We'll go over it on Atlantis. Weir's not going to be happy about this.

[He starts to dial out.]

INT—CAVE ON PLANET

[Ford studies a document at his desk. Sheppard enters.]

FORD
How'd that go?
SHEPPARD
Well, he'll do it…But, we have need conditions.
FORD
What?
SHEPPARD
Dial back McKay's enzyme doses.
FORD
No!
SHEPPARD
Look we're under some time constraints here. I'm not saying the enzyme wouldn't help. I'm just saying we need him standing still and thinking not running laps because it feels good.
FORD
Ronon and Teyla?
SHEPPARD
We stick to the plan there.
FORD
Fine. What else?
SHEPPARD
I'll fly it, that's obvious, but I want McKay, Teyla, and Ronon on the strike team.
FORD
It's not a good mission for McKay. Could get rough in there.
SHEPPARD
Look, if suddenly we need a door open or a computer hacked, McKay should be there to do it. That's why he's on my team in the first place.
FORD
Is that it?

[Sheppard gives him a quick nod. They stand up and shake hands.]

EXT—MEADOW NEAR DART ON PLANET, DAY

[McKay helps one of Ford's men remove the coverings from the Dart. Jace holds up a box.]

MCKAY
No.

BEGIN MONTAGE

[In the cave, Ronon and Teyla spar vigorously. Ronon spars with one of the men, getting beaten to the mat.]

[In Ford's office, Ford goes over the Wraith ship schematics with Sheppard.]

FORD
So that's…

[At the Dart, McKay climbs out from underneath it.]

MCKAY
No, no, no, no. You fire it up now, it'll all go up in smoke.

[In the cave, Ronon and Teyla are forced to take enzyme injections.]

[At the Dart, Sheppard arrives and climbs in.]

[In the cave, Teyla beats her opponent in sparring. Ronon does also.]

[At the Dart, McKay activates a remote control.]

MCKAY
Okay…

[The Dart powers up and lifts off the ground.]

MCKAY
We're close.

END MONTAGE

INT—CAVE ON PLANET

[Ford rolls up the schematic. Sheppard sits.]

SHEPPARD
It's a good plan.
FORD
Couldn't have done it without you, sir.
SHEPPARD
Well, I'm glad you trusted me enough to bring me here.

[Ford looks troubled.]

SHEPPARD
What's wrong?
FORD
I want to go home.

[Sheppard stands and approaches Ford.]

SHEPPARD
Well…then let's go. Give McKay the DHD crystals, and let's get out of here. I can have you back on Earth within an hour.
FORD
No. We have to do this first. I have to prove to them that we can do this.
SHEPPARD
You don't have to prove anything to anyone, Lieutenant. I saw your cousin and your grandparents while I was back there. They miss you…a lot. This whole M.I.A. thing is killing them.
FORD
They're strong. They can wait. If anyone's ever going to take me seriously ever again, we have to do this.
SHEPPARD
Listen, Aiden—
FORD
No. I'm sorry. I don't want to talk about this.

INT—DINING ROOM IN CAVE

[Sheppard talks to Teyla and Ronon, who are eating.]

SHEPPARD
Well… part of me thinks he brought us here 'cause he knew we'd bring him back. I think subconsciously he knows what our plan is, and he's fine with it.

[Ronon and Teyla aren't listening. They are making aggressive expressions to each other across the table. Ronon stands and steals part of Teyla's food with a challenging smile.]

TEYLA
Give it back.

[Ronon puts the food in his mouth and chews.]

RONON
Or what?

[Teyla slugs him in the face, smiling.]

SHEPPARD
Hey!

[Ronon grabs Teyla and pulls her across the table onto the floor. Sheppard is shocked. The two start slugging each other and sparring. Sheppard approaches.]

SHEPPARD
Knock it off, guys. Stop—

[Ronon accidentally elbows Sheppard in the face, knocking him down. They stop fighting.]

SHEPPARD
What the hell's gotten into you two? Oh, right. Never mind.

[Sheppard gets off the floor and sits down painfully.]

RONON
Are we going to talk about this?
SHEPPARD
About what?
RONON
The enzyme works. No one's saying it, but we're all thinking it. It works.
TEYLA
I must admit, I have noticed an increased amount of strength.

[She smiles and moves as if full of energy.]

RONON
Maybe Ford is right. Maybe it's worth looking into, seeing what this stuff can do.

[Sheppard is clearly dubious.]

TEYLA
They were an hour late delivering our doses yesterday, and I felt awful. It frightens me how reliant my body has become on the enzyme always being in my system. What would happen if we suddenly could not receive it?
RONON
If the only negative is that you've got to keep taking it, I'd want to stay on it.
SHEPPARD
What about you?
TEYLA
The fact that I'm even considering it makes me feel—

[McKay enters.]

MCKAY
Hey.
SHEPPARD
So?
MCKAY
It's ready.

EXT—MEADOW NEAR DART

[Sheppard sits in the Dart's cockpit. Jace is beside him. McKay climbs up.]

MCKAY
Okay, now…
(to Jace)
Could you move? Look, this is probably going to make you a little uneasy, but when the canopy forms, it's perfectly opaque.
SHEPPARD
Then how am I going to fly this thing?
MCKAY
The whole thing is one giant heads-up display. It'll show you all the pertinent information that you need.
SHEPPARD
Yeah, you mean in Wraith.
MCKAY
Yeah, which is why I've installed an interface to translate a lot of the readouts.
SHEPPARD
By "a lot" do you mean "all"? Because my Wraith's not all that good.
MCKAY
Enough to get you by.
FORD
(to McKay)
I knew you could do it. Right on time. Kanayo will get you suited up. You should get ready.
MCKAY
Right. Great.

[McKay gives Sheppard a nervous look, then climbs down and walks over to one of Ford's men, who drops his vest on purpose.]

FORD
He seems a little nervous.
SHEPPARD
You know McKay. He'll be fine.
FORD
You know how to dial the DHD on this thing?
SHEPPARD
Got the addresses memorized.
FORD
Good. Good. Listen, I should probably tell you…there's been a slight change of plans.
SHEPPARD
(unconcerned)
Oh, yeah?
FORD
Kanayo!

[Kanayo and the others point their weapons at McKay. Ronon pulls out his weapon, but Ford's men aim at him as well.]

KANAYO
Don't even think about it!
SHEPPARD
What the hell are you doing?
FORD
McKay's going to stay here while we do the op. Take him!

[Some of Ford's men force McKay to leave.]

MCKAY
What? No! No, no, no, no, no!
SHEPPARD
That wasn't part of the plan.
FORD
Neither was you heading back to Atlantis. You didn't really buy the whole "I want to go home" speech, did you? Huh?

[Sheppard appears disappointed he fell for it. Kanayo takes Ronon's blade weapon from him.]

FORD
You're always underestimating me, Sheppard. You need to stop doing that. Complete the mission, and McKay lives.
SHEPPARD
We can't complete the mission, Ford. It's a bad plan!
FORD
You said it was a great plan.
SHEPPARD
You didn't buy the whole "it's a great plan" speech, did you?
FORD
Get this Dart in the air and come scoop us up. Oh… and the Dart DHD only dials to the planet we're going to and back here, just in case McKay's life isn't enough to get you to do the right thing.

[Ford steps away from the Dart.]

TEYLA
Aiden…what are you doing?
FORD
Just making sure you guys hold up your end of the bargain.
(to his men)
It's okay. Give them back their weapons.
KANAYO
Are you sure?
FORD
Yeah. If we're going to do this, we've all got to work together.

[He steps over and hands Sheppard his pistol.]

FORD
Good luck to us all.

[Jace holds his weapon on Sheppard while he powers up the Dart.]

SHEPPARD
Stand by for pickup.

[The canopy becomes opaque, and the Dart lifts off. It circles back to beam up the remaining group, including Ronon and Teyla.]

INT—WEIR'S OFFICE

[Weir paces; Lorne is seated.]

WEIR
If they were okay they would have radioed in by now.
LORNE
I understand that, ma'am.
WEIR
I want everyone who's cleared for off-world activity to help with the search. The Daedalus is on its way from Earth and they'll be able to help, but until they get here, it's up to us.
LORNE
And I know that, but I need you to understand that it is going to take a very long time to search all the planets on our list. It…it could take months.
WEIR
We can't afford months, Major.
LORNE
And I know that too.
WEIR
Good.

[He leaves.]

EXT—SPACE ABOVE PLANET

[The Dart leaves a planet and approaches the Wraith ship.]

INT—DART

[Sheppard sees readouts on his heads-up display, but can't see anything outside.]

SHEPPARD
This is going to take some getting used to.

[Data streams by over his head, then stops.]

SHEPPARD
Come on. We were getting along so well.

[The display shows the autopilot engaging.]

SHEPPARD
Oh, autopilot? No, no, no, no. No! R2, I need you to turn the autopilot off. Now… Worth a try.

EXT—SPACE

[The Dart flies into a hangar entrance on the side of the Wraith ship.]

INT—DART

[The display shows the Dart flying toward a red target point.]

INT—WRAITH SHIP

[The Dart is enters the large hangar bay and flies upward.]

INT—DART

[The display reads: "Rematerialize cargo? And buttons for Yes and No. Sheppard chooses Yes.]

INT—HANGAR ON WRAITH SHIP

[The Dart flies over a narrow bridgeway. The passengers re-materialize, but some are too close to the edge and fall over, screaming as they fall. Jace is one of them. Kanayo nearly falls, but Ronon grabs him.]

FORD
Jace!
KANAYO
We have to go after them!
TEYLA
No one would have survived that fall.
RONON
We have to get out of the open.

[They start to move off.]

INT—DART

SHEPPARD
(into radio)
You guys okay?
FORD
(over radio)
No, we're not okay. You beamed half of us off the edge of the platform.
SHEPPARD
(extremely concerned)
Teyla and Ronon?

INT—CORRIDOR ON WRAITH SHIP

[The conversation switches between the Dart and the corridor.]

FORD
Is that all you care about? You murdered my men.
SHEPPARD
I've lost all control of the Dart. I had to release you blind.
FORD
Maintain radio silence until we lay the charge.
SHEPPARD
Negative. We need to stay in contact. They’re—

[Ford turns off his radio.]

TEYLA
You should leave that radio on, Aiden.
FORD
Don't tell me what to do.

INT—DART

SHEPPARD
(into radio)
Ford? Ford? Ford! Damn it.

[The display says 'Exit Craft for Inspection'.]

SHEPPARD
I'd prefer not to.

[The display says, 'Opening Canopy'.]

INT—HANGAR IN WRAITH SHIP

[The canopy disappears. Two Wraith soldiers approach Sheppard. He pulls out a machine gun and kills both of them.]

SHEPPARD
Ooh, boy. You mind checking the oil?

[From further away, a high-pitched siren starts up.]

SHEPPARD
This is not good.

[He gets out of the Dart and rushes away.]

INT—CORRIDOR ON WRAITH SHIP

[Ford and the others hear the sound.]

FORD
What is that?
RONON
It's an alarm.
FORD
That shouldn't happen.
RONON
No, it shouldn't.

[Ford turns on his radio.]

FORD
(into radio)
Sheppard, you there? Sheppard, you there?

INT—CORRIDOR ON WRAITH SHIP

[Sheppard is in a different part of the ship.]

SHEPPARD
(into radio)
I think I might have set the alarm off. You guys be careful, because there's probably some guards on the way.

INT—CORRIDOR ON WRAITH SHIP

RONON
Too late. If you want to live, stay behind me.

[Ronon pulls out his blade with determination and moves forward.]

INT—CAVE ON PLANET

[McKay sees the bouncer inject himself in the arm with enzyme.]

MCKAY
Oh, do—
BOUNCER
Want some?
MCKAY
No, I'm good, thanks.
GUARD #2
You sure?
MCKAY
(nervous and frantic)
Yeah, pretty sure. Look, don't you think that they should be back by now? I mean, how long does it take to fly there and fly back, you know? I mean, I say it's a minute from here to the gate, 10 tops from their gate to the hive ship, 20 to get in and lay a charge, and, what, like 10 more to get back out and home, right? So…
BOUNCER
Worrying really isn't going to do anything.
MCKAY
Yeah, well, I subscribe to a different school of thought.
BOUNCER
They're fine.
MCKAY
You can't possibly know that.
BOUNCER
I got a gut feeling.
MCKAY
Yeah, well, I have a different gut…

[The guards approach him menacingly.]

MCKAY
…feeling.

INT—CORRIDOR ON WRAITH SHIP

[Sheppard reloads his machine gun.]

SHEPPARD
(into radio)
Ford what's your position? Ford!

INT—CORRIDOR ON WRAITH SHIP

[Ford and the others are engaged in direct combat with the Wraith. Some are firing while Ronon fights hand-to-hand.]

INT—CORRIDOR ON WRAITH SHIP

[Sheppard hears growling nearby.]

SHEPPARD
Ah, damn it.

[He pulls out a hand grenade and lobs it at a Wraith soldier. It blows up, blinding him. Sheppard moves in and fires on it.]

INT—CORRIDOR ON WRAITH SHIP

[Ford's team continue to fire. Kanayo and Teyla are hit with stun weapons and fall.]

FORD
Keep fighting!

INT—CORRIDOR ON WRAITH SHIP

[Sheppard is stunned from behind. He falls unconscious. A Wraith drags him away.]

INT—CORRIDOR ON WRAITH SHIP

[A Wraith drags Teyla away. Ronon continues fighting, back-to-back with Ford.]

FORD
We can't hold them!

[Ford is hit; Ronon keeps firing with both hands.]

EXT—ATLANTIS, DAY

INT—WEIR'S OFFICE

[Weir speaks into a voice recorder.]

WEIR
And thanks to several modifications the specifics of which are encrypted in this data burst, we believe we can streamline our power consumption a further thirty percent… Also, at the time of this transmission, Colonel Sheppard's team is still missing, and is… is presumed… Colonel Sheppard's team is still missing. End transmission.

[She drops the recorder on the desk, clearly upset.]

INT—CELL ON WRAITH SHIP

[Sheppard is lying on a platform off the floor, his head on Teyla's leg. He wakes up. The rest of Ford's team is there too, awake and pacing.]

TEYLA
Are you all right?
SHEPPARD
We're in a holding cell?
TEYLA
Yes.
SHEPPARD
On a hive ship.
TEYLA
Uh-huh.
SHEPPARD
(sitting up)
Well, then, no, I'm not doing so good.
FORD
I can't believe you screwed up.
SHEPPARD
(standing)
I did what?
FORD
You set off the alarm. You ruined the mission.
SHEPPARD
The Dart went on autopilot the second I got close to the hive. You're lucky I was able to get you out!
FORD
Well, why would it do that? Why would it go on autopilot?
SHEPPARD
That's what Jumpers do when they gate to Atlantis.
FORD
Well, why didn't you say something?
SHEPPARD
(incredulous)
Why didn't I say something?
FORD
You want me to fail. You want me to look bad.
SHEPPARD
(angry)
That's right, you snot-nosed brat. I'd put my team's life on the line just to prove you wrong.

[Ronon hears something.]

RONON
We got company.

[A Wraith arrives with soldiers. Ronon stands at the netting holding them in, blocking the Wraith's view of the others.]

WRAITH
Move away.
RONON
What do you want?
WRAITH
(smiling)
The one who flew the ship.
RONON
Why don't you start with me?

[The netting slides open. A guard stuns Ronon, causing him to fall in pain.]

SHEPPARD
(stepping forward)
Wait, wait, wait. You want me.

[The Wraith sniffs at Sheppard. He and the guards lead Sheppard away. Teyla helps Ronon up.]

FORD
It's all right. There's always a way out.
RONON
If he dies…you die.

INT—QUEEN'S ROOM ON WRAITH SHIP

[The guards lead Sheppard to the a platform. He turns in a circle, looking around. A Wraith Queen appears right in front of him and hisses at him. She raises her hand toward his face and begins to exert mental control over him.]

WRAITH HIVE QUEEN
Kneel.

[Despite trying to resist, Sheppard falls to his knees.]

FADE OUT

TO BE CONTINUED

END CREDITS
