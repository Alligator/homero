SGA 4.06 "Tabula Rasa" Episode Guide

Précis

With almost everyone on Atlantis infected by an amnesia-causing bacteria, McKay finds himself zip-strapped to his desk with cryptic letters on his arm and no memories of who he is or what has happened.

Guide | Transcript

Transcript

TEASER

INT—MCKAY'S LAB

[A disoriented McKay sits on a stool in front of a table with a laptop computer. He panics when he realizes his left hand has been tied to the table.]

MCKAY
Hey! Hey is anyone there?! Look, I need some help in here!

[He struggles with the binding, then notices a post-it note on the tablet computer. It points to a button on the machine with the words "Press Here". He presses the button and a video appears showing a close up of McKay's face.]

MCKAY
(recorded video)
All right, look, I know you're probably panicking right now, but there is no time for that. You need to focus! I'm sure you're wondering…who tied you to the desk. Well, the truth is…it was you. I mean…me. I did it because I needed you to get this message. It would take too long to give you the full explanation, and you wouldn't remember it anyway, so you're just gonna have to just trust me on this. Now, you'll find a knife taped to the underside of your chair. Find it and cut yourself loose.

[McKay finds the knife and cuts off the plastic restraint.]

MCKAY
(recorded video)
Now…I need you to listen. It is vitally important that you find this woman.

[The video display moves to show a monitor displaying a file photograph of Teyla. Over the picture is written: "THIS IS TEYLA. FIND HER". Underneath is displayed "Teyla Emmagan—Athosian". The camera moves again to show McKay. McKay watches the recording of himself while rubbing his face, looking confused and afraid.]

MCKAY
(recorded video)
I don't know where she is, but you have to find her. She can help. Uh, check the mess hall, or maybe they took her to the brig. I don't know. You just have to keep looking. Don't worry about anything else. And for god's sake, don't trust anyone. Just find her, and do it fast, because if you don't, hundreds of people are going to die, including you.

[The recording shuts off and the monitor now displays the picture of Teyla.]

Just relax. I know you're scared. But you're just going to have to trust me. It's for your own good.
(low, to Ronon)
So much for coming quietly.

[Sheppard continues to lead the group down the hall. Ronon looks confused and he and a marine start to veer to a corridor to the right.]

RONON
Hey…! Mess hall's this way.

[Sheppard heads over to Ronon.]

SHEPPARD
Right! Right. Don't look at me that way. It's easy to get turned around in this place.

[The doors open and they enter the mess hall.]

INT—ATLANTIS MESS HALL

[The mess hall is crowding with people. Sheppard and Ronon report to Carter, who's already there.]

SHEPPARD
Major Lorne's picking up some more stragglers. We're starting to get a little resistance.
CARTER
Yeah, they're starting to lose their memories. They probably can't understand why there's a bunch of "soldiers" after them.
SHEPPARD
That's why I told him to take his men to the armory and equip them with stunners.
CARTER
(wincing)
I'm not sure I like the idea of us shooting at our own people.
SHEPPARD
Well, we may not have a choice.
CARTER
Yeah, I suppose you're right. Pretty soon, there's gonna be some panicked, confused people out there. Okay, carry on.

[Carter leaves. Sheppard looks lost in thought.]

RONON
What?
SHEPPARD
Come with me.

[They leave the mess hall.]

INT—ATLANTIS ARMORY

[Lorne's men arm themselves while Lorne pops down some more stimulants. Sheppard and Ronon enter.]

SHEPPARD
You might want to go easy on that stuff, Major.
LORNE
Sir, we need to delay this thing as much as possible. If we all go, there's no coming back.
SHEPPARD
Yeah, I know. That's what I want to talk to you about. I've got an idea.

PRESENT DAY

INT—ATLANTIS CORRIDOR

[Carter and McKay walk down a corridor.]

MCKAY
Didn't we come this way before?
CARTER
No.
MCKAY
Are you sure?

[Carter looks around.]

CARTER
No.
MCKAY
I have an idea.

[He takes a marker out of his pocket and marks up a corner of the wall.]

CARTER
What are you doing?
MCKAY
If we come back here, we'll know we've been this way before.
CARTER
Presuming of course, that we remember that we're the ones who put it there in the first place.

[They hear someone coming down the hall, so they hide against the wall.]

ZELENKA
I know you're out there! Drop your weapons and come out slowly.

[Zelenka spins around randomly pointing a stunner weapon. He's got McKay's tablet computer clutched to his chest. McKay and Carter peer around the corner and see him.]

MCKAY
(to himself)
Little guy with glasses…Huh.

[He frantically looks at the notes on his arms. He starts to move out from hiding, but Carter grabs his arm.]

CARTER
What are you doing?!
MCKAY
Hey, I know him.
(to Zelenka)
Don't shoot. We don't have any weapons.

[He raises his hands in surrender. Carter follows.]

ZELENKA
Who are you?
CARTER
We were captured by the soldiers. We escaped.
ZELENKA
Really?

[McKay spots the computer.]

MCKAY
Wait a second. What is that?

[Zelenka looks at the computer, then shakes his head, raising his weapon.]

ZELENKA
I found it.
MCKAY
Let me see.
CARTER
What is it?
MCKAY
I don't know, but something tells me it's important.
ZELENKA
Why?
MCKAY
I don't know.

[Zelenka hands it over. McKay presses the button on the side which turns on the screen. The picture of Teyla appears along with the message to find her.]

ZELENKA
Hey, how did you know to do that?
MCKAY
I don't know. I just did.
CARTER
Well, that's her! Teyla!
ZELENKA
You know her?
CARTER
Well, not exactly. We're looking for her. We think she can help us.
MCKAY
Huh…
ZELENKA
It says, "find her." How are we going to do that?

EARLIER

INT—CARTER'S OFFICE

[Keller briefs Carter, Teyla, Sheppard, McKay, and Ronon.]

KELLER
This is what I wanted to show you.
TEYLA
This is the bacteria that is causing our disease?
KELLER
Not exactly, but it's very similar.
CARTER
And you found this in the database?
KELLER
I stopped looking in the database. I knew there was a reason why Teyla and Ronon are the only people who have tested negative so far. And we don't have a lot of records on the Satedans, so I started looking through Doctor Beckett's records on the Athosians. This is a blood sample taken from a ten year old boy. He was suffering from something called um…uh…I wrote it down.

[She looks around blankly.]

TEYLA
(hesitantly)
Kirsan fever?
KELLER
Yeah, that's it.
TEYLA
It is a common childhood ailment throughout the galaxy. I had it when I was…eight.
RONON
I had it when I was ten.
TEYLA
But this cannot be the same disease. Kirsan fever does not affect adults, nor does it cause memory loss.
KELLER
Well, that may be true, but the structure of the bacteria is too similar to ignore. They have to be related somehow.

[McKay starts to snap his fingers.]

MCKAY
Wait a minute. Maybe they are the same. That would explain why the sensors didn't identify it as a threat.
TEYLA
How is that possible?
MCKAY
Well, think about it. I mean, this planet didn't have a Stargate until we came here. Maybe when the Ancients were-were first, uh, scouting this place, they accidentally brought this, uh…uh, Kirsan Fever with them. It got trapped here, and over the course of ten thousand years, it mutated to cause the symptoms we're now experiencing.
KELLER
That would also explain why you and Ronon aren't effected. You have antibodies built up from when you were young.
CARTER
Well, if they have the antibodies, then—
KELLER
It would take months to reverse-engineer a cure. I mean, I'm not even sure it's possible.
TEYLA
The sap of the enchuri plant. My people have used it for generations to combat Kirsan Fever. It is highly effective, and not much is needed.
SHEPPARD
Let's go get some.
CARTER
Take a jumper.
RONON
Wait a minute. You disabled the 'Gate, remember?

[Carter looks at them blankly.]

TEYLA
You ordered Doctor Zelenka to take the control crystal. Where did he put it?
SHEPPARD
(into radio)
Zelenka, this is Sheppard, come in.

INT—ATLANTIS CORRIDOR

[Lorne leads his team.]

LORNE
Check out the rooms down there, and there. I want this section cleared out in ten minutes.
SHEPPARD
(over comm)
Lorne, come in.
LORNE
(into comm)
Yes, sir. Go ahead.
SHEPPARD
(over comm)
Have you seen Zelenka?
LORNE
(into comm)
As a matter of fact, I have.

[The conversation switches between Carter's office and the corridor with Lorne.]

SHEPPARD
Did you bring him to the mess hall?
LORNE
Uh, sorry, sir. But he gave us the slip. Must've heard us coming. Cracked one of my people over the head with a metal rod, and disappeared down a service hatch before I could get a shot off. There's no telling where he is now.

[Everyone in Carter's office looks at each other in shock and dismay.]

INT—ATLANTIS CORRIDOR

[Sheppard's team searches the corridor for Zelenka.]

SHEPPARD
(into radio)
Zelenka, respond. Zelenka, come in.
TEYLA
Radek, we are not trying to hurt you. We need your help!
MCKAY
Okay, this is pointless. Look, we don't even know if he still has a radio. And even if we find him, chances are, he's not gonna remember what he did with the control crystal. Let's face it, we're screwed.
SHEPPARD
There's gotta be some way out of this. What about the jumpers? They've got DHDs.
MCKAY
That doesn't matter. Without the control crystal, the 'Gate can't establish a stable wormhole. We're not going anywhere.
SHEPPARD
I'm missing something. I-I can't concentrate.
RONON
What about the mainland?
TEYLA
Ronon's right. The enchuri plant is abundant on many worlds. It may be here as well.
SHEPPARD
All right. Assuming we can find the stuff, how are are we gonna distribute it.
MCKAY
(sighing)
I'll figure something out.

[He heads to his lab.]

TEYLA
(to Ronon)
I'll stay with Rodney. Do you know what to look for?
RONON
It grows like a weed on Sateda.

[Teyla nods and follows McKay.]

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

[Sheppard enters the jumper and looks around blankly. Ronon, carrying some empty sacks, closes the door and motions Sheppard to the pilot's chair.]

RONON
It's, uh, right there.

[Sheppard and Ronon sit.]

RONON
You can fly this thing. It's in your blood…literally.

[Sheppard looks confused, but complies.]

EXT—ATLANTIS, DAY

[The jumper flies out of the bay.]

INT—MCKAY'S LAB

[McKay steps away from his laptop to move to another computer. En route, he starts blinking heavily and looking unsteady.]

MCKAY
Oh…wait a second. What am I doing?
TEYLA
You said the quickest way to spread the cure was to aerosolize it and distribute it through the ventilation system. You are currently attempting to override base protocol for temperature, humidity, and air pressure.
MCKAY
Right. Right. Okay, that's easy. It's just a simple matter of accessing the root directory and…
(he presses a few buttons, and the computer beeps an alarm)
Aw, come on!
TEYLA
Rodney—
MCKAY
No, no, no, no. I should be able to do this in my sleep. Oh god…it's happening to me too, isn't it?
TEYLA
You have to remain calm.
MCKAY
Oh, no, no, no. You don't understand. My memory was lousy to start with. I mean, people's names, birthdays…I once forgot mother's day five years in a row.
TEYLA
What is the ratio of a circle's circumference to its diameter?
MCKAY
Well, that-that's pi. 3.14159265, etc., etc. But that doesn't count, that's easy.
TEYLA
You are a scientist, Rodney. That is what you care about. That is what you will hang onto the longest.
MCKAY
Right. Right, so… Wait a minute, doesn't that make me a really bad person?
TEYLA
It makes you the type of person who is going to save all our lives.
MCKAY
Oh, okay.

[Teyla gets him to turn back to his work.]

PRESENT

INT—ATLANTIS INFIRMARY

[Carter, Zelenka, and McKay enter the infirmary. It is empty, and supplies are scattered everywhere.]

ZELENKA
What is this place?
MCKAY
I don't know. Looks like they left in a hurry.
CARTER
She's not here, let's…
MARINE
(from down the hallway)
This way! Let's go!
CARTER
(whispering)
Quiet! Go-go!

[She shoos at them to hide. They scatter and take refuge behind equipment. Lorne and his team enter.]

LORNE
All right. Everybody spread out. They've got to be in here somewhere.

[The team looks around, passing very near the hiding places. One person spots something in the cabinet Carter hides behind.]

MARINE
(to Lorne)
Sir! Found them!

[He holds up bottles of stimulants.]

LORNE
Good. Let's go. Let's move out. Come on.

[Lorne's team leaves. Carter steps out of hiding.]

ZELENKA
Can we get out of here, please?

[Carter looks at him with a thoughtful expression.]

MCKAY
What is it?
CARTER
I have an idea. Come on.

EARLIER'

EXT—NEW LANTIA MAINLAND, DAY

[The jumper has landed. Ronon steps out. Sheppard hesitantly follows.]

RONON
Let's go.
SHEPPARD
Wait a minute. What is this place?
RONON
The mainland.
SHEPPARD
What are we doing here?
RONON
(impatiently)
I told you already. We need to get that plant.
SHEPPARD
Something seems fishy.

[Ronon primes his pistol and stuns Sheppard, who collapses unconscious onto the ramp.]

RONON
Never gets old.

[Ronon hogties the unconscious Sheppard on the bench inside the jumper and heads out.]

RONON
Sorry, buddy. You're just going to slow me down.

[He closes the jumper door with the remote and runs off.]

INT—MCKAY'S LAB

TEYLA
How's it coming, Rodney?
MCKAY
I'm just about finished…I-I think.

[They hear a clatter outside.]

MCKAY
What was that?
TEYLA
I will go see. Keep working.

[Teyla steps into the corridor outside. McKay looks nervous, then turns back to his computer.]

INT—ATLANTIS CORRIDOR

[Teyla cautiously walks around. Lorne's team comes around a corner, raising their weapons instantly upon spotting her.]

LORNE
Don't move!
TEYLA
(smiling)
Major Lorne. It's me, Teyla.
LORNE
You shouldn't be out here.
TEYLA
Major, it's me.
LORNE
Everyone's been ordered back to the mess hall.
TEYLA
But Doctor McKay and I—
LORNE
Put your hands on your head…slowly.

[Two of the men step forward with restraints ready. When they get close enough, Teyla fights them off with hand to hand. Her movements allow her to dodge Lorne's blast. She gets a stunner off one guard then uses him as a shield. She backs away as Lorne hits his man. They give chase.]

INT—MCKAY'S LAB

MCKAY
(whispering)
Teyla?

[He approaches the door, but sees no one.]

MCKAY
Oh god.

INT—ATLANTIS CORRIDOR

[Lorne and his lone man chase Teyla. They shoot their stunners at each other. One man finally hits Teyla with a stunner blast. They approach cautiously, but she is out cold.]

LORNE
All right, let's get her up.

INT—MCKAY'S LAB

[McKay fumbles with a camcorder.]

MCKAY
Come on…All right, look, I know you're probably panicking right now, but there is no time for that. You need to focus! I'm sure you're wondering…who tied you to the desk.

PRESENT

INT—BRIG

[Teyla paces inside. Lorne enters. He's sweating and looking ill.]

LORNE
You're awake.
TEYLA
Major Lorne, you must set me free—
LORNE
Save it. You're not going anywhere 'till I get some answers. I need to know what you've done to my people.
TEYLA
What I've done? You believe I'm behind this?
LORNE
Is-is it a drug? Some kind of bio-weapon?
TEYLA
Major, they're sick. You as well. The entire base is infected with a disease.
LORNE
Everyone except you.

[He fumbles in a pocket for his bottle of stimulants.]

TEYLA
Yes. I'm immune to its effects.
LORNE
(popping more pills)
Isn't that convenient?
TEYLA
(cautiously)
How many of those have you taken?
LORNE
Why don't you just let me worry about that, okay?
TEYLA
It's the pills. They're what's making you act like this. They're confusing you. They're making you unstable!
LORNE
Making me unstable?! I'm not the one who unleashed a disease on my people, okay? Quit stalling. I want to know what you were doing in the hall—

[He is shot from behind by a stunner blast. Carter wields the stunner. She, McKay, and Zelenka enter.]

TEYLA
Rodney!
MCKAY
Teyla!
TEYLA
(relieved)
Yes. It's good to see you. Colonel Carter, Doctor Zelenka, you as well. How did you find me?
CARTER
Uh, well, we were having trouble finding you by ourselves, so we followed him.
TEYLA
Eh, did you finish your work?
MCKAY
Wh-what do you mean?

[Teyla sighs and moves over to the control panel.]

TEYLA
Can you disarm the force shield?

[McKay studies the panel.]

MCKAY
I-I don't know. Wait…

[He looks on his arm and reads some information, then presses keys on the control panel. The shield deactivates, and the door opens. Teyla steps out.]

TEYLA
Quickly.

[She leads them out.]

EXT—NEW LANTIA MAINLAND, DUSK

[Ronon runs back to the jumper with two full bags of plants. He finds the ramp down, with no Sheppard in sight. He groans in frustration and checks inside the jumper, dropping his bags and priming his gun. As he steps out, Sheppard comes up from behind where he's been hiding next to the vehicle. He raises his pistol.]

SHEPPARD
Don't move! Drop it.

[Angry, Ronon complies, raising his hands in surrender.]

SHEPPARD
Now back away.

[Ronon takes a few steps and turns. Sheppard reaches down and grabs Ronon's gun, keeping his pistol trained on Ronon the whole time.]

SHEPPARD
Now. Tell me. Who the hell are you?
RONON
I know you don't remember everything. You have no idea what's going on. You're confused. Maybe even a little scared. I know I would be. But you have to trust me.

[Sheppard cocks his pistol.]

RONON
We're friends! The things we've been through together…I don't care what anyone says, no disease can wipe that away, not completely. Okay? Deep down, you know I'm telling the truth. Now give me the gun.

[While Ronon's been speaking, Sheppard's been giving careful thought to what he says. His pistol lowers slightly. But at the last sentence, Sheppard raises it again, alert.]

SHEPPARD
Oh, nice try. For all I know, you're the one who tied me up.
RONON
Fine. Then shoot me. Then you'll be all alone, in the middle of a dark forest with no idea who you are, where you're going, or what you're gonna do next. How could I possibly make it any worse?
SHEPPARD
That's a good point.

[Ronon smiles and motions to lower the gun. Sheppard uncocks his weapon.]

INT—MCKAY'S LAB

[Teyla leads the others inside.]

TEYLA
Here. Sit.
MCKAY
Are you sure you wouldn't rather do it?
TEYLA
I do not know how.
MCKAY
Oh, and I do?
TEYLA
Yes! You created the override program. You said you were almost finished.
MCKAY
(desperate, sarcastic)
Oh, then it should be easy.
TEYLA
(encouraging)
Rodney, listen to me. You must do this, it is our only chance. The knowledge is inside you, you just have to find it.

[McKay looks at the computer, then presses Enter. The program runs, then a screen says "Program Complete."</blockquote>

MCKAY
Oh. Wow.
ZELENKA
He did it.
MCKAY
When I said I was almost finished, I-I guess I wasn't kidding.
CARTER
Now what?
TEYLA
Well, now, we just have to hope that Colonel Sheppard and Ronon completed their side of the mission.
MCKAY
Right…right.

[Carter, Zelenka, and McKay all turn to Teyla.]

CARTER, MCKAY, AND ZELENKA Who?</blockquote>

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

KEMP
What is it?
MARINE THREE
A ship. Headed for the bay doors on the roof.
KEMP
Have they identified themselves?
MARINE THREE
No, sir. It could be help.
KEMP
It could also be some kind of an attack. Get in touch with the others. Tell them to meet me at the jumper bay. Find Major Lorne!

[He heads out.]

INT—JUMPER BAY

[Sheppard and Ronon's jumper lands on the floor of the bay.]

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

RONON
Nice flying.
SHEPPARD
Thanks.

[He grabs the sacks as the doors open.]

RONON
Come on, help me with the stuff. We've got to get it to Teyla straight away.
SHEPPARD
Who?
RONON
Come on.

INT—JUMPER BAY

[They exit and are instantly stopped by the Kemp and his men, weapons raised.]

KEMP
Hands in the air. Now.
RONON
Listen, Lieutenant, this is medicine. We need to deliver it.
KEMP
I said, hands in the air.

[Sheppard is pulled aside by a soldier. A frustrated Ronon throws down his sacks and raises his hands.]

KEMP
Let's take them to the brig.
RONON
You're making a mistake. There are sick people who need that.
SHEPPARD
(low to Ronon)
You sure you know what you're doing?

[Lorne arrives.]

LORNE
What the hell is going on here?
KEMP
Sir. We caught these men trying to infiltrate the base.
RONON
Lorne, you have got to listen to me. Everybody on this base is going to die if you don't.
LORNE
And who are you?
RONON
Look in your pocket.
LORNE
What?
RONON
Just look in your vest pocket!

[Lorne looks in the pocket that Ronon indicates. He pulls out a picture. It's a Polaroid of Sheppard. Underneath is written: "Lt. Col John Sheppard. He is your commanding officer. Trust him."]

RONON
You took a picture of him, just in case it came to this. I was there.

[Lorne compares the picture to Sheppard.]

LORNE
He's telling the truth. This is our commanding officer.

[Sheppard looks nonplussed, but rolls with the change in fortune.]

SHEPPARD
That's right. I am your commanding officer. So…you should do what I say.
LORNE
Yes, sir. What are your orders?
SHEPPARD
(after a pause, indicating Ronon)
Do what he says.
RONON
Gun.

[The guard hands back Ronon's weapon. Ronon picks up the sacks of plants.]

RONON
Follow me.

EXT—ATLANTIS, DAY

INT—ATLANTIS INFIRMARY

[Sheppard wakes up slowly in an infirmary bed. Teyla and Ronon are by his side.]

TEYLA
Colonel, you're awake.
SHEPPARD
(groggily)
Mmm. What happened?
RONON
Teyla distributed the plant through the ventilation system. Knocked you out for a bit.
SHEPPARD
How long have I been asleep?
RONON
'Bout a day.
SHEPPARD
A day?!
RONON
Hit some people harder than others.
TEYLA
How you feeling, John? How's your memory?
SHEPPARD
Oh, pretty good, I think. I mean…things are a little fuzzy. If I forget your birthday next year…
TEYLA
(smiling)
I will forgive you. When you are feeling well enough, Major Lorne wishes to speak with you. He feels badly about what happened.
SHEPPARD
It wasn't his fault. Just glad he still had the photo I gave him.
RONON
That was good thinking, by the way.
TEYLA
Yes. If the distribution of the cure had been delayed any longer, many more people would have died.
SHEPPARD
(worried)
What do you mean?
(looking around)
Where's McKay?

[In another section of the infirmary. McKay has his head down on Katie's bed while he holds her hand. She is unconscious. Keller comes over.]

KELLER
You should get some sleep.

[He raises his head.]

MCKAY
It's okay. I'm…fine.
KELLER
I can let you know when she wakes up.
MCKAY
I'll stay.
KELLER
Okay. She's not giving up, you know. She's a fighter.

[She rubs his arm, then walks away. McKay notices her hand shifting on his.]

MCKAY
Katie?

[Katie stirs slightly.]

MCKAY
Doctor?!

[Keller rushes back.]

MCKAY
She's moving her fingers.
KELLER
Katie? Katie! Hey.

[Katie awakens.]

KATIE
Wha—where am I?
KELLER
You're in the infirmary. You're safe. Do you remember anything?
KATIE
Um…
(she spots McKay)
Rodney? It's good to see you.
MCKAY
(relieved)
Yeah. Yeah, it's good to see you too.

[He takes her hand.]

FADE OUT
