SGA 4.14 "Harmony" Episode Guide

Précis

Always keen on helping their allies, Sheppard and McKay accept a proposal made by Harmony’s sisters to accompany her on a trek into the forest to seek guidance to become queen in her late mother’s stead. Will Harmony become the planet’s new queen, or will an enemy assassinate her — and her escorts?

No, Rodney!
MCKAY
Well, will you give it to me later when you decide not to eat it?

You allow him to question you so incessantly?

I guess I'm just used to it by now.
HARMONY
Would it not be easier to beat him?

We don't beat people where we come from.

Why not?
SHEPPARD
Well, we find it's not very effective in the long run…unfortunately.
HARMONY
Really? You are a truly gentle leader, John. I admire that.
SHEPPARD
Thank you, Harmony.

SHEPPARD
Isn't there supposed to be a rope bridge here?
MCKAY
Well, maybe you're reading the map wrong.

No, I think it's pretty straightforward. How old is this map?
HARMONY
It has been passed down from too many generations to know for sure.

Okay, I guess that bridge doesn't exist.

SHEPPARD
You two stay put, I'm going to try to find a safe way across the river.
MCKAY
Well, no, wait, wha—
SHEPPARD
Don't worry. I'll be right back.

[Sheppard leaves. McKay and Harmony exchange glances, then McKay walks away to find someplace to sit. Harmony starts rummaging through Sheppard's pack. With a sigh, McKay sits on a mossy tree trunk. Harmony soon joins him; she munches on a PowerBar.]

MCKAY
Where'd you find that?
HARMONY
John's bag.
MCKAY
It-it's not yours.
HARMONY
I'm hungry.
MCKAY
Well, you can't just take it like that!
HARMONY
When I'm hungry, I eat.
MCKAY
What's going to happen when Sheppard finds out?
HARMONY
Well, he's not going to now, is he?

Well, yes he is, 'cause I'm going to tell him.
HARMONY
(in dangerous tone)
I wouldn't do that if I were you.
MCKAY
Yeah, or what?

I'm the queen.
MCKAY
Not yet, you're not, little sister.
HARMONY
Trust me, you don't want to be on my bad side.
MCKAY
Right? And what are you going to do? You gonna have me beaten?

[He looks nonplussed at her cold glare. Rallying, he stands.]

MCKAY
All right, princess, just…hand it over!

[She gobbles the rest of the bar and hands him the wrapper.]

MCKAY
Oh…see, now you're in trouble. You just wait 'till Sheppard gets back here, 'cause he's gonna—

[Sheppard returns from behind McKay.]

SHEPPARD
All right, I think I found a place for us to, uh…

[He spots McKay holding the empty wrapper.]

SHEPPARD
Is that my PowerBar?
MCKAY
(defensively)
She stole it!
HARMONY
(affronted)
I did not!
MCKAY
Oh, what?! You so did! Tell him!
HARMONY
I would never do such a thing!
MCKAY
The second you were gone, she went into your bag!
HARMONY
You are a liar! You are a lying liar!
MCKAY
Oh, please, who are you going to believe?
SHEPPARD
Well, if she stole it, Rodney…how come you've got the wrapper?
MCKAY
(stammering)
Well, bu—I…I was trying to take it back from her, and then she wolfs it down and hands me the wrapper!
SHEPPARD
(skeptical)
Really?
MCKAY
(fiercely to Harmony)
Okay, you'd better come clean, brat!

[Harmony bursts into sobs and holds out her arms to Sheppard, who grudgingly steps forward to receive her embrace.]

SHEPPARD
It's okay, sweetie. Sometimes, he makes me cry, too.

[Her face hidden from Sheppard in this position, Harmony turns, dry-eyed, to McKay and makes a throat-slitting gesture. McKay blinks. Harmony continues her sobbing act.]

SHEPPARD
All right, we need to get going here. All right? I found a path.

[He extricates himself from Harmony and leads the way.]

HARMONY
(whining)
Can I navigate from the map?

[McKay rolls his eyes.]

SHEPPARD
Sure, as soon as we get back on the path.

[Harmony schools her expression back to calmness, and shoots McKay a bright, smug smile before passing him. McKay watches, flabbergasted, before setting his jaw and following.]

[Later, Harmony leads the way, studying the map. They reach a fork in the trail.]

HARMONY
(uncertain)
Now…we go…

Right.
HARMONY
(confidently)
Correct.

What was that?

That's probably the beast.

Sorry, the what now?

The beast—the protector of the Ruins of Laros. Did Flora not tell you of it?

(fuming)
I guess she forgot to mention it.

HARMONY
Don't worry.

When you say "beast", I'm assuming that's a figure of speech, right?
HARMONY
Oh, no. It's a beast, all right. Though no one is quite certain what it looks like. Those who have cast their eyes upon it have quickly perished.
MCKAY
(sarcastic)
But your little knife will protect us.
HARMONY
I'm the queen. It will not harm me.
SHEPPARD
Well, we may not get the chance to tell the beast that.
HARMONY
It will know.

[She closes her knife blade and starts down the trail. McKay hurries to catch up.]

MCKAY
Listen, why don't we just tell your sisters we made it as far as the ruins? We don't actually have to make it there. I mean, they'd never have to know the difference.
HARMONY
I would know.
MCKAY
Yeah, but you're going to be queen, so who cares? Right?

(upset)
I care! My people care!
MCKAY
Listen…
HARMONY
Perhaps it would be best if you stopped speaking. Your voice hurts my ears!
MCKAY
Yeah, well, that beast could hurt my whole body, okay? So I'm sorry, but this is not what we signed up for! Sheppard, back me up here.

[He turns to Sheppard for support, only to realize Sheppard is not with them. He looks around in alarm. Harmony takes out her knife again.]

MCKAY
Sheppard? John!
HARMONY
According to legend, the beast attacks silently.
MCKAY
I thought you said the beast would know you were the queen.
HARMONY
Well, it didn't attack me.
MCKAY
(into radio)
John? John, are you there?
HARMONY
It is strange, though…the beast is supposed to only attack enemies of the throne. If it was going to kill anyone, it should have killed you.
MCKAY
I'm not an enemy of the throne! I'm not! I'm here to protect you!
GENII SOLDIER 1
Don't move!

[Guns can be heard cocking behind them as men in Genii uniforms appear out of the foliage. McKay raises his hands in surrender.]

HARMONY
So far, I'm not impressed.
GENII SOLDIER 1
Drop your weapon.

[McKay unhooks the P-90 from his vest and lays it on the ground.]

GENII SOLDIER 1
(into radio)
We have her. Map Grid Eleven in the small clearing.
TERAN THE GENII FIELD CAPTAIN
(over radio)
We're on our way.
GENII SOLDIER 1
(to McKay)
Hand her over.
MCKAY
You're Genii!
GENII SOLDIER 1
Step aside, and hand her over.

[McKay puts himself between Harmony and the soldiers.]

MCKAY
(holding his hands up defensively)
This is obviously some sort of huge misunderstanding, all right? I am-I'm Doctor Rodney McKay. I'm-I'm a close, personal friend of, uh, Ladon Radim. You know, your leader?

[They don't lower their weapons. McKay continues to stand between them and Harmony, who hides behind McKay.]

MCKAY
I-I'm-I'm your greatest ally! I—you don't know who I am? Look, I'm the genius scientist! The friend of John Sheppard? I gave you the bomb? No? Nothing? What do they teach you in Genii school?
GENII SOLDIER 1
Hand her over, right now.
MCKAY
Okay, let's just talk about this.

[The soldier takes a couple steps forward.]

GENII SOLDIER 1
I'm growing impatient. Our quarrel is not with you. Once you hand her over, you're free to go.
MCKAY
(perking)
Really?
GENII SOLDIER 1
Yes.
MCKAY
Hmm.

[He appears to consider this option. Harmony looks scandalized.]

HARMONY
You wouldn't! You took a sacred oath to protect me.
MCKAY
Nah, I don't remember doing that.
GENII SOLDIER 1
I'm going to count to three. One…

MCKAY
Where have you been?
SHEPPARD
Well, my Spidey senses felt an ambush comin' on.
MCKAY
What, and you couldn't mention it?!
SHEPPARD
Well, not without tipping them off, no!

[Harmony has stepped around McKay and now stares at the Genii soldier's corpse in shock.]

MCKAY
Well, what took you so long? We were just about to get—
SHEPPARD
I had to take up a position, so I could shoot all three at once.

SHEPPARD
You okay?

Oh, I understand strategy. In fact, I'm forming one right now! The second we return to the castle, I will instruct my guards to—

But, John—

Thumb screws, you like them?
HARMONY
(content)
Yes. Thumb screws are good.
SHEPPARD
(lower, to McKay)
We better find some cover. That backup-he called is going to be here any minute.

[McKay gestures Harmony to get in front of him as he watches the rear.]

[Some time later, Sheppard finds a sheltered area of fallen trees within the larger forest.]

SHEPPARD
All right, this will work.
MCKAY
Those were Genii!
SHEPPARD
Yeah, I picked up on that, Rodney. Thanks.

[They crouch amongst the fallen trunks.]

MCKAY
So what are the Genii doing here? And why do they want to kill her? I mean, aside from the obvious reasons.
SHEPPARD
Harmony?
HARMONY
I don't know. I don't like the Genii.
MCKAY
But you know who they are. Are you trading partners?
HARMONY
Not anymore.
MCKAY
(glowering)
But you were.
HARMONY
Yes. But they cheated my mother on many trades. She banished them from our land.
MCKAY
What about you, huh? When you're queen, are you going to start trade back up?
HARMONY
No! Absolutely not!
SHEPPARD
So maybe somebody in the royal court's a little more willing to play ball, you know, once the path is cleared.
HARMONY
If I were to die, my sister would succeed me. But if the Genii think that she would be more willing to trade than me, they are sadly mistaken.
MCKAY
(to Sheppard)
You really believe that Ladon would give the marching orders to kill a little girl? Even this one?
SHEPPARD
I don't know how these people work. Maybe he told someone to "fix the problem" and this is what they came up with.
MCKAY
Well, it's creative.
SHEPPARD
Knowing why they're here really doesn't help us out. All right, I need to get a take on how many are out there. You stay with her, okay?
MCKAY
(with resigned sigh)
Sure.
SHEPPARD
(to Harmony)
All right, stay here, and stay quiet.
HARMONY

Of course, John. Thank you for taking care of this. I'm not sure what I would do if you weren't here.

HARMONY
Tell me something, "Doctor." The place where you sleep at night…is it guarded?
MCKAY
I know what you're trying to do, and it's not going to work, okay? I'm not scared of you. You're just a kid—a bratty little kid. I couldn't care less that you're going to be queen. In fact, nothing could impress me less, so why don't you just cool it with the "bad seed" act and just go back to ignoring me.
HARMONY
Surely they can't guard you all the time?

[McKay looks back at her with a disturbed expression. She shoots him an innocent smile.]

INT—FOREST, DAY

[Genii patrol the trail.]

TERAN
(over radio)
Squad Three, take up position on the ridge. Squad Four, move to Map Grid Nine.
GENII SOLDIER
(into radio)
Acknowledged.

[Sheppard remains hidden, but has heard the radio conversation.]

SHEPPARD
(sotto voce)
Wonderful.

INT—FALLEN TRUNKS, FOREST, DAY

[Harmony continuously glares at McKay.]

MCKAY
Stop it. What are you doing?
HARMONY
I'm burning your face into my memory.
MCKAY
You're—
HARMONY
This way I can give the bounty hunters an extremely accurate description of you.
MCKAY
All right, your highness, I have had just about enough out of you with this whole—

[Sheppard returns.]

SHEPPARD
(whispering fiercely)
I thought I said keep talking to the minimum!
MCKAY
I was…she was…

HARMONY
How many Genii are out there, John?
SHEPPARD
If I had to guess, I'd say all of them.
MCKAY
(worried)
What, so, a lot of them?
SHEPPARD
(impatient)
Yes, Rodney. A lot.
HARMONY
This will make the pilgrimage most difficult.
SHEPPARD
That's funny. I was thinking the same thing.
HARMONY
I find we have much in common, John.
SHEPPARD
(noncommittally)
Mmm. Yeah. Anyway, I figure we wait for nightfall, and then head back to the settlement.
HARMONY
Back to the settlement?
SHEPPARD
Yep. Road trip's over.
HARMONY
But that would make this journey a failure!
SHEPPARD
You win some, you lose some. If you want to be queen, you've got to learn how to cut your losses.
HARMONY
But the lunar cycle is nearing its end. If I do not make it to the ruins by tomorrow's sundown, I will forfeit the throne!
SHEPPARD
I'm sure they'll make an exception.
HARMONY
No, they cannot. It is our way.
SHEPPARD
(with frustrated sigh)
It's too dangerous to move forward.
HARMONY
For you, perhaps.

[Sheppard makes a face and turns to face her.]

SHEPPARD
(emphatically)
If it's too dangerous for me, it's definitely too dangerous for you. We're gonna wait for nightfall, and then we're gonna head back to the village. End of discussion!
MCKAY
(drily)
Fat chance of that.
HARMONY
It's not your decision to make. I'm the queen.
SHEPPARD
(fiercely)
No, not yet! And until you are, you're just a regular kid. I call the shots!
HARMONY
(standing)
Many would consider disobeying the queen a treasonous act.
SHEPPARD
Well, I'm sure they wouldn't look too fondly on letting the queen get killed. We're heading back.

EXT—CASTLE, NIGHT

INT—MARDOLA'S BEDCHAMBER

[There is a soft knock at the door. Mardola goes to answer it. Teran is there, wearing a hooded cloak. She lets him in.]

MARDOLA
You should not be here.
TERAN
I was careful. No one saw me, don't worry. We use this from now on to stay in contact.

[He lays a Genii radio on the table.]

MARDOLA
Does that mean it's not over yet?
TERAN
No. Her two guardians have complicated things greatly. They killed three of my men.
MARDOLA
Yes, well, they were an unforeseen complication. My apologies.
TERAN
Your apologies do me little good.
MARDOLA
We will increase your pay. Don't worry. Threefold.
TERAN
(intrigued)
Threefold?
MARDOLA
Triple the work, triple the pay.
TERAN
You want us to kill them all?
MARDOLA
If they survive, they'll come back and tell my sister that the Genii were involved. Now we can't have that, can we?

[Teran hisses, then bows and takes his leave.]

EXT—FALLEN TRUNKS, FOREST, DAY

[Harmony sleeps under a blanket at their makeshift shelter. McKay steps around her to join Sheppard out at the "entrance".]

MCKAY
(low)
Listen…I've been doing a little thinking.
SHEPPARD
Oh, you have, have you?
MCKAY
I've got a Wraith stunner in my backpack. She can't weigh more than ninety pounds. I say we stun her and just carry her back.
SHEPPARD
(considering)
Hmm…Nah, we can't stun a thirteen year old girl.
MCKAY
It's kind of dangerous out there. I mean, the forest is crawling with Genii. Who knows when she could throw one of her fits.
SHEPPARD
She'll be fine. She understands how dangerous this situation is. I talked to her.
MCKAY
I'm sure you did, but she is thirteen, she's unreasonable, among many other awful attributes.
SHEPPARD
You know, they say you dislike the things in others that you, in fact, dislike about yourself.
MCKAY
So, what?
SHEPPARD
I'm just saying…
MCKAY
We are nothing alike!
SHEPPARD
Okay.
MCKAY
She is a stuck-up, know-it-all brat…

[He stares at Sheppard who pointedly flinches but says nothing.]

MCKAY
Oh, ha ha ha.
SHEPPARD
All right, it's about as dark as it's going to get. Let's wake her up and get moving.

[They turn around to find Harmony is gone.]

SHEPPARD
Oh, no.
MCKAY
Oh, that sneaky little…I told you we should've stunned her!

EXT—FOREST, NIGHT

[Sheppard and McKay move cautiously through the woods, searching for Harmony. They keep their voices low.]

MCKAY
I'm going to kill her.
SHEPPARD
You really need to work on your child skills.
MCKAY
We have been searching for her for over an hour. You know, we have had not one, but-but two close encounters with the Genii. All so some…useless, spoiled brat can make her meditation appointment?
SHEPPARD
More searching, less complaining.
MCKAY
Sorry, I'm hungry. And when I'm hungry, I get cranky.
SHEPPARD
And when you get cranky, you get hit.
MCKAY
Okay, okay.
SHEPPARD
I wish Ronon was here.
MCKAY
You know…you said that out loud, right? I mean, I-I'm right here.
SHEPPARD
Well, it's nothing personal. It's just he's a better tracker than both of us combined.
MCKAY
Hmm, that's true.

[They continue through the forest.]

MCKAY
Okay, I'm starting to hallucinate.
SHEPPARD
Why? What do you see?
MCKAY
Not see, smell. I smell food. Delicious, fresh-cooked food. Roast beef, hamburgers, hot dogs, pork on a spit.

[Sheppard pauses.]

SHEPPARD
Chicken.
MCKAY
Yeah, sure, the more, the merrier.
SHEPPARD
No, I smell…chicken.

[McKay taps his nose and starts moving forward.]

MCKAY
The nose that knows. Follow me.

EXT—CAVE, NIGHT

[McKay and Sheppard arrive at a cave entrance.]

MCKAY
Ah. Found it. It's coming from down there. Told you. You don't need Ronon while I'm here.
SHEPPARD

[She stalks off, McKay and Sheppard roll their eyes and follow.]

EXT—CASTLE, DAY

INT—MARDOLA'S QUARTERS

[Mardola reads at her table. The door slams open and guards, followed by Harmony and Flora enter. Mardola stands, smiling.]

MARDOLA
(to Harmony)
You've returned!

[The door shuts, and the guards start to search the room. Mardola looks at them worriedly.]

MARDOLA
(to guards)
What are you doing? These are my private quarters!
FLORA
It's all right, Mardola. They've already searched my room. You should allow it.
MARDOLA
(angry)
Why?!
HARMONY
The Genii attacked us.
MARDOLA
Oh…well, it was a good thing Colonel Sheppard was there to protect you.
FLORA
They knew the location of the ruins, Mardola. How?
MARDOLA
You ask as if I would know.

[A guard places the Genii radio he found in Mardola's dresser on the table in front of them. Mardola's eyes widen in fear, and she looks back at Harmony, who's staring at her, hurt and betrayed.]

MARDOLA
You're too young to be queen, Harmony!
HARMONY
(coldly)
Perhaps…but when you're released from prison, you'll be too old to remember.

INT—THRONE ROOM

[The court is full of people. McKay and Sheppard stand at a red carpet in front of Harmony's throne. Harmony is dressed in a fancy gown with a crown on her head. Flora stands on the lowest tier, to the right of the throne.]

HARMONY
Doctor Rodney McKay and Colonel John Sheppard. Your contribution to my people may never be repaid.
SHEPPARD
Well, we didn't do it for compensation, Your Highness-ness.
MCKAY
(under his breath)
Kiss-ass.
HARMONY
I personally owe you a debt of gratitude for your help defeating the regicidal Genii. I've had one of the court's finest artisans feature you in a rendering of our glorious victory.

[Sheppard and McKay exchange pleased looks. Harmony steps towards a curtain behind the throne and nods. Servants pull off the curtain. The court applaud as a large painting is revealed. It pictures McKay in the foreground firing his weapon; Harmony stands in the center, her hand on the temple pedestal, a halo from the beast glowing around her; Sheppard cowers in fright behind Harmony.]

MCKAY
Yeah, pretty much how I remember it.

[McKay starts to clap as well as Sheppard looks stunned. Harmony grins.]

FADE OUT

END CREDITS
