SGA 4.02 "Lifeline Part 3" Episode Guide

Précis

While the team risks going into the Replicators' city to obtain a ZPM to power the city-ship Atlantis, Col. Samantha Carter joins the crew of the Apollo to search for the missing base.

(into radio)
All right. It's plugged in again.

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

MCKAY
(into radio)
Still nothing. Look, what did you do?!

INT—CORE ROOM

SHEPPARD
(into radio)
I didn't do anything!

INT—ASURAS CORRIDOR

[Two by two, Replicators run up the corridor into the field. They continue to disintegrate.]

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

WEIR
(into radio)
They keep attacking the field. They're deliberately sacrificing themselves.

INT—CORE ROOM

RONON
(into radio)
Why would they do that?

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

WEIR
(realizing)
The more they attack the field, the quicker they can figure out how to override it.

INT—CORE ROOM

RONON
(into radio)
Then we need to get the hell out of here.
SHEPPARD
(into radio)
Rodney, why is this not working?
MCKAY
(over radio)
It's not working because the core is not recognizing the tablet.
SHEPPARD
(into radio)
Well, why not?

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

MCKAY
(into radio)
Look, I don't know!

INT—ASURAS CORRIDOR

[Another pair of Replicators disintegrate, but later this time.]

INT—ASURAS CONTROL ROOM

ASURAN TECHNICIAN
We're almost through the field.
OBEROTH
Good. I'm on my way.

[He leaves.]

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

MCKAY
(into radio)
Okay, you're going to need to implement adjustments in the negotiation protocol to exploit parallelism at the device interface.

INT—CONTROL ROOM

SHEPPARD AND RONON
(into radio)
What?!
MCKAY
(over radio)
Just do what I say. Start by unplugging the cable. Now access the tablet's root directory.

[Sheppard moves to comply.]

SHEPPARD
(frustrated)
This is not simple!

INT—ASURAS CORRIDOR

[A Replicator slowly pushes through the shield. Pairs of Replicators follow, passing more easily each time.]

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

WEIR
You have to get them out of there now!
MCKAY
We're not done!
WEIR
There's no more time! The Replicators have breached the field!

EXT—ASURAS, DAY

[The puddle jumper still sits on the rooftop with the anti-replicator shield.]

INT—CORE ROOM

SHEPPARD
What do you mean: "they've breached the field"?
MCKAY
They've figured out how to override it. Look, we need to move much faster. You need to make changes to the negotiation protocol to allow for direct data placement. We need to allow for re-synchronization of the packet boundaries within the data stream.

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

WEIR
Oberoth is heading to the core room! He knows it's us!
MCKAY
Well, how far is he? Can they make a run for it?
WEIR
(desperately)
No. Replicators are approaching from every direction. They're trapped!
MCKAY
Which means they're as good as dead.

INT—CORE ROOM

SHEPPARD
We can hear you, you know.

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

MCKAY
Wha-what? Uh, no. Wh-wh-when I say: "as good as dead", I mean it as more of a-of a…

[While McKay has been speaking, Weir hits the release button on the door and leaves the jumper.]

MCKAY
Elizabeth, what are you doing?
WEIR
Stay here!

[She runs out.]

MCKAY
Elizabeth, you can't go out there!

INT—CORE ROOM

MCKAY
(over radio)
Elizabeth!
SHEPPARD
What's going on, Rodney?

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

MCKAY
Elizabeth just left the jumper.

INT—CORE ROOM

SHEPPARD
Why?

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

MCKAY
I-I don't know. Sh-she just-she just ran out.

INT—CORE ROOM

SHEPPARD
(into radio)
Elizabeth, this is Sheppard. Do you copy? Elizabeth, respond.
RONON
If they capture her…
SHEPPARD
I know, I know.
MCKAY
(over radio)
So what do we do now?
RONON
She said to hit the kill switch on the nanites if she did anything suspicious.

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

MCKAY
I know, but…

[McKay collapses into a chair and looks at his computer.]

INT—CORE ROOM

RONON
Sheppard…make the call.

[Sheppard looks torn, but after a pause…]

SHEPPARD
Do it.

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

[McKay looks equally upset. Eventually he starts typing, but the computer won't accept his command and instead shows an error message and makes a warning beep.]

MCKAY
It's not working.

INT—CORE ROOM

SHEPPARD
What?
RONON
Why not?
MCKAY
(over radio)
Ju-just give me a second.

[Sheppard and Ronon wait impatiently, Ronon continuing to guard the door to the room.]

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

MCKAY
(panicking)
I-I'm not getting confirmation. And don't ask why, because I don't know, okay?! I mean, the last time she had active nanites inside her, she was able to exert a certain amount control. Maybe…maybe she's doing it here now? Or maybe there's interference that's affecting the frequency? I don't know. I need to run a diagnostic.

INT—CORE ROOM

RONON
Better yet, why don't you figure a way how to get us out of here?
SHEPPARD
If we're screwed, we might as well complete the mission.

[He plugs the tablet computer back into the core panel.]

INT—ASURAS CORRIDOR

[Oberoth walks determinedly down a corridor. As he turns a corner, Weir is standing there waiting for him. In a reversal of roles, she sticks her hand into his head.]

INT—OBEROTH'S MIND

[Oberoth and Weir face each other.]

OBEROTH
Doctor Weir?
WEIR
Hello, Oberoth. You're surprised.
OBEROTH
Indeed I am.
WEIR
Niam infected me with nanites.
OBEROTH
Yes, I can detect them now.
WEIR
We were able to neutralize them, and then reprogram and manipulate them to suit our needs.
OBEROTH
Industrious of you.
WEIR
Which is why, now that I've been able to establish a physical link through you, I have direct access to the Replicator collective.

[Oberoth considers this news, looking as if he's trying to defy her. He appears frightened.]

WEIR
I'm in control now.

INT—CORE ROOM

[Sheppard finishes his connections.]

SHEPPARD
Okay, I've got it plugged in. Anything?

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

[McKay studies his computer display. Suddenly lines of Replicator code appear.]

MCKAY
Yes! Yes, it's working!

INT—CORE ROOM

[The door opens. Ronon hides behind the pillar, unnoticed as several Replicators enter and face the core device itself. Sheppard takes cover behind it. The Replicators suddenly freeze. As Sheppard rises with his weapon and Ronon comes out from behind the pillar, they see the Replicators' situation.]

SHEPPARD
What the hell just happened?

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

[McKay pauses in his work.]

MCKAY
The Replicators have all frozen.

INT—CORE ROOM

SHEPPARD
Well, I can see that, but who did it?

[Ronon disarms the frozen Replicators.]

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

MCKAY
Well, it wasn't me. It might have been Elizabeth.
RONON
(over radio)
Can she do that?
MCKAY
I don't know. I mean, she'd have to link directly with the Replicator collective, which means, the chances they'll be able to take over her nanites has just increased exponentially.

INT—CORE ROOM

SHEPPARD
Well, how long before they come back to life?

[Ronon finishes disarming the Replicators.]

MCKAY
(over radio)
Your guess is as good as mine, but I would err on the side of less time rather than more.
RONON
Can we go?
SHEPPARD
Yeah, first things first. How we doing with the upload?

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

[McKay continues typing.]

MCKAY
Almost there. Just a few more seconds.
SHEPPARD
(over radio)
How many seconds?
MCKAY
Don't start with that again!

INT—OBEROTH'S MIND

[Oberoth appears to be struggling.]

OBEROTH
I'm impressed with your strength…Doctor Weir. But I must remind you that it's only a matter of time…before I…make the necessary corrections to overcome the hold you have on me.
WEIR
I am prepared to fight for as long as I need.
OBEROTH
Good for you. Your desire to protect your people is admirable, but you will, ultimately, fail.
WEIR
Keep thinking that. Keep allowing your arrogance to undermine you.
OBEROTH
You know it's true, Doctor Weir. I can feel you weakening already.

[Now it is Weir who looks as if she is starting to struggle.]

OBEROTH
Despite your desire, this is a fight for which you are…clearly, ill-equipped.

INT—CORE ROOM

MCKAY
(over radio)
That's it, you're done! Unplug and go!

[Sheppard pulls out the computer and they both run out of the room.]

INT—ASURAS CORRIDOR

[Sheppard and Ronon rush down the corridors, passing several frozen Replicators.]

INT—OBEROTH'S MIND

[Both Oberoth and Weir appear to struggle. Oberoth finally reaches out and grabs Weir's arm. She inhales deeply.]

OBEROTH
Once again, you have underestimated me.

[Weir continues to struggle against him.]

INT—ASURAS CORRIDOR

[As Sheppard and Ronon move down a corridor, one of the Replicators turns as they pass. Ronon knocks him back, but others give chase.]

SHEPPARD
Whoa! They're moving again!

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

[Two Replicators enter the rear bay with weapons drawn. McKay raises his hands in surrender.]

MCKAY
Yep. I know.

INT—OBEROTH'S MIND

[Oberoth is more confident now. Weir lowers her head and closes her eyes as if shutting down completely.]

INT—ASURAS STAIRWELL

[Sheppard and Ronon are closely pursued. As they reach a landing, armed Replicators are waiting. The pair are at a standoff, with nowhere to go.]

INT—ASURAS CELL

[Sheppard, McKay, and Ronon stand in the cell. Oberoth enters the room.]

SHEPPARD
(insolently)
Well, this is familiar.
OBEROTH
I'm trying to decide if this incursion of yours reflects considerable bravery or stupidity.
MCKAY
Where's Doctor Weir?
OBEROTH
Her attempts to seize control of the collective proved too taxing for her, rendering her mind and body virtually moribund. She has been taken to be…repaired.
SHEPPARD
You're going to take control of her.
OBEROTH
That has already occurred. Unfortunately, I was not able to glean all the information I need from her. So, I will have to extract it from you.
MCKAY
Information?
OBEROTH
About the precise location of Atlantis. You left your planet before we were able to destroy it.
RONON
(deadpan)
Well, sorry to spoil your plans.
OBEROTH
Nothing is spoiled. Merely…prolonged. And now with your help, we will complete the destruction.
SHEPPARD
Seeing how none of us will volunteer the information…
OBEROTH
You know quite well that we have a simple method for extracting what we need.

[Oberoth signals with his head, and the cell shield lowers and the door opens. Oberoth and others enter the cell. Two guards force Sheppard to his knees. Another holds a weapon on Ronon to prevent his interference. Sheppard winces as Oberoth enters his head. Oberoth winces, redoubling his concentration. Sheppard looks unconcerned.]

SHEPPARD
Problems?

[Oberoth looks around. McKay and Ronon smirk at him. Oberoth pulls out his hand. Sheppard morphs into Weir.]

INT—OBEROTH'S MIND

OBEROTH
(shocked)
What?!
WEIR
I figured, you messed with our minds, so I'd mess with yours. You didn't overpower me, Oberoth. The Replicator guards didn't capture my team. It was all a fictional scenario played out in your mind. In reality, your guards are still frozen, and my team is escaping as we speak. You were wrong, Oberoth. I have never underestimated you, but you have clearly underestimated me.
OBEROTH
(furious)
You will not leave this city alive!
WEIR
Maybe not. But my team will.

INT—ASURAS CORRIDOR

[Sheppard and Ronon run down the corridor where in Oberoth's fiction, the Replicators came to life. This time they have no problems.]

SHEPPARD
Still nothing from Elizabeth?
MCKAY
(over radio)
No. I can't communicate with her. I can't track her. It's like she just-just disappeared.
SHEPPARD
If she's the one who froze the Replicators, that means she's probably still alive.
MCKAY
(over radio)
For the moment, maybe.
SHEPPARD
Well, she can't be that far.
RONON
We'll find her.

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

MCKAY
You'd better make it fast. These Replicators could unfreeze at any moment.
SHEPPARD
(over radio, sarcastic)
Thanks, Rodney.

[McKay rolls his eyes.]

INT—OBEROTH'S MIND

OBEROTH
I admire your tenacity, but I won't be fooled again.

[Weir gulps, looking strained. Oberoth studies her.]

OBEROTH
You're struggling, Doctor Weir.

[They both redouble their efforts. Oberoth reaches out.]

INT—ASURAS CORRIDOR

[Oberoth grabs Weir's arm and starts to force her hand out of his head. Sheppard and Ronon arrive, aiming their weapons at Oberoth, but unable to get a clear shot because of Weir's position.]

SHEPPARD
Elizabeth…
WEIR
Get to the jumper.
SHEPPARD
You're coming with us.

[Ronon fires at Oberoth, but he stays in position.]

WEIR
I can't keep them frozen much longer!
SHEPPARD
We're not leaving you behind!
WEIR
If you don't leave right now, none of us will get out of here, so go! That's an order.

[Weir half turns her head to look at him. Oberoth seizes the opportunity to regain control. The Replicators unfreeze and start firing at Sheppard and Ronon, forcing them to fall back.]

RONON
Come on!
SHEPPARD
Elizabeth?!
WEIR
GO!

[Replicators surround Weir as Sheppard and Ronon retreat.]

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

[Sheppard and Ronon race in, startling McKay who's working with the ZPM.]

MCKAY
Oh, thank god!

[Ronon closes the door behind them.]

MCKAY
Elizabeth?

[Ronon shakes his head. McKay stands, stunned.]

SHEPPARD
Can we cloak? Rodney, can we cloak?!

[McKay snaps out of his fugue state.]

MCKAY
No. Not yet. No, we-we drained too much power trying to convert the cloak into an AR field. Look, I-I'll try to interface the ZPM, but we're gonna need some time.

[Sheppard powers up the jumper as McKay gets to work.]

SHEPPARD
All right, we'll just have to make a run for it, then.

EXT—ASURAS, DAY

[The puddle jumper lifts off and flies away at high speed.]

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

SHEPPARD
(tightly)
How's hyperspace looking?
MCKAY
Not good. Look, harnessing power from the ZPM is tricky. If I'm off even just the slightest amount, then we could all—
SHEPPARD
(interrupting)
Two ships headed our way.

[The jumper is rocked by a blast.]

SHEPPARD
Rodney?!
MCKAY
Look, I need more time!

[More explosions rock the ship.]

SHEPPARD
We just lost weapons!
MCKAY
Dammit.
RONON
Come on, McKay!
MCKAY
This is not simple, you know! If I don't properly calibrate the power output, we could incinerate the moment the hyperspace window opens!
SHEPPARD
I'm picking up something dead ahead.

[A Replicator jumper uncloaks directly in front of them blocking their path.]

SHEPPARD
Oh, crap. They're powering up weapons!

[The Aurora-style battle cruiser is attacked by weapons on its port side. Sheppard and the others look, but can't see the source from their vantage point.]

SHEPPARD
What the hell was that?

EXT—SPACE

[The battle cruiser exchanges fire with the Apollo.]

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

ELLIS
(over comm)
Colonel Sheppard, this is the Apollo, do you read?
SHEPPARD
(surprised, into comm)
Colonel Ellis, is that you?

INT—APOLLO BRIDGE

ELLIS
(into comm)
Affirmative. The 302 bay is open, and you have permission to come aboard. I suggest you hurry.
SHEPPARD
(over comm)
Copy that, Apollo. We're on our way.

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

MCKAY
(into comm)
Can I just add that your timing is pretty damn perfect?
CARTER
(over comm)
We would've gotten here earlier, but we had a little trouble finding Atlantis.
MCKAY
(into comm)
Sam? Wha-is that you?

INT—APOLLO BRIDGE

CARTER
(into comm)
It's me, Rodney. I'll explain when you get on board.

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

SHEPPARD
(into comm)
Colonel…we lost Doctor Weir on the Replicator planet. Can you, uh, lock on her locator beacon and beam her up?

INT—APOLLO BRIDGE

[Carter checks her screen and exchanges concerned looks with Lee and Ellis.]

CARTER
(into comm, sympathetically)
I'm not picking up any signal.

INT—PUDDLE JUMPER

[The team appear crestfallen.]

SHEPPARD
(into comm)
Copy that.

EXT—SPACE

[During the firefight, Sheppard's puddle jumper flies into the hangar on Apollo. Apollo instantly opens a hyperspace window and escapes through it.]

EXT—SPACE

[Atlantis flies through space. Its engines are powered, and the shield encompasses the entire city-ship now.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[Lee, Carter, McKay, Sheppard, Ronon, and Teyla stand in front of a display.]

MCKAY
Here's a good one. M35-117.
CARTER
Does Doctor Weir know about it?
MCKAY
Well, it's not on our primary or our backup lists. I purposely searched for planets we'd never considered before. The, uh, Replicators can probe her mind all they want, they won't find it.
SHEPPARD
Good…for us.
MCKAY
(subdued)
Well, look, it's a stable atmosphere, a big ocean, besides the, uh, large, particularly venomous snake-like creature that inhabits the mainland, it looks to be a welcoming environment.

[Carter and Lee exchange looks. Sheppard shrugs his approval.]

MCKAY
All right.
(into radio)
Radek, how we doin?
ZELENKA
(over radio)
All set here. The ZPM is online.
MCKAY
Okay. Here we go.

[He works the controls.]

EXT—SPACE

[A hyperspace window opens and Atlantis enters it.]

EXT—SPACE ABOVE M35-117

[Atlantis exits hyperspace above the planet.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[Everyone heads to the still-shattered window to look at the ocean-filled world below them.]

SHEPPARD
We did it.
MCKAY
Well, there's still the small matter of landing this thing.
SHEPPARD
Yeah. I'll just jump in the chair and ease us down.
CARTER
Well, that could be tricky. We drained a lot of power from the ZPM just getting here.
SHEPPARD
Have we got enough energy to keep the shield up for reentry?
MCKAY
There better be. What, a city this size will create considerable friction. We come in too fast or too steep, and the shield could attenuate under the stress and the whole place could be torn to shreds, or-or burn up, or both?

[Carter looks askance as she glances at Sheppard.]

SHEPPARD
It never ends with you, you know?
MCKAY
What?
SHEPPARD
Tell everyone to buckle up, we're going in.

[Sheppard leaves.]

EXT—SPACE, ORBIT ABOVE M35-117

[Atlantis enters the atmosphere.]

INT—ATLANTIS CHAIR ROOM

[Sheppard concentrates while sitting in the chair.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[Carter, Lee and McKay sit at some controls. Teyla and Ronon stand nearby.]

CARTER
Shields holding. So far so good.

[McKay sighs.]

TEYLA
What's wrong?
MCKAY
The angle's too steep. We keep going like this, we're gonna put too much strain on the shield, which means more power draining from the ZPM.
(into radio)
Sheppard, we're coming in too steep. You need to ease up on the angle of reentry.
LEE
In fact, you mean entry. You see, we never actually left this…
CARTER
(warning)
Bill!
LEE
Oh, right, right.

[Lee goes back to watching his station.]

EXT—M35-117, DAY

[Atlantis's shield creates friction as it flies through the layers of atmosphere.]

INT—CHAIR ROOM

MCKAY
(over comm)
Ease up on the angle.
SHEPPARD
(stressed)
I heard you the first time, Rodney.

INT—CONTROL ROOM

MCKAY
(into comm)
And yet, we're still coming in too steep!

[There is a heavy vibration.]

MCKAY
Hang on!

EXT—M35-117, DAY

[Atlantis enters the lower atmosphere over the ocean.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

LEE
We're over the ocean!
MCKAY
Sheppard, you need to slow us down. We're coming in over the water.

INT—CHAIR ROOM

SHEPPARD
Copy that.

EXT—M35-117, DAY

[Atlantis's descent slows.]

INT—CHAIR ROOM

MCKAY
(over comm)
Still too fast. Slow down!

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

MCKAY
You want to touch down gently, like a…leaf kissing the surface of a pond.

[The ship continues to shake. A sharp impact knocks everyone around.]

EXT—M35-117, DAY

[Atlantis lands in the ocean, splashing hard and pushing a wave of water outward.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[Everyone regains their equilibrium.]

MCKAY
(sarcastic, into radio)
Nice kissing!

INT—CHAIR ROOM

[Sheppard sits up and shuts down the chair.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

CARTER
(still breathless)
How we doing?

[McKay checks his display.]

MCKAY
Systems are looking good…so far. And plenty of power in the ZPM.
CARTER
Drop the shield.

[McKay does so.]

INT—CHAIR ROOM

CARTER
(over comm)
Colonel Sheppard, we made it. The city's floating safely on the ocean. Excellent landing.
SHEPPARD
(relieved)
Thank you.

EXT—ATLANTIS, NIGHT

INT—WEIR'S OFFICE

[Teyla picks up a photograph of Weir's dog Sedgewick on the desk and studies it. Ronon enters. Teyla packs the photo in a box, following it with Weir's laptop. Ronon enters quietly and slowly.]

RONON
Who do you think's gonna replace her?

[Teyla sighs heavily.]

TEYLA
I don't know.

[She picks up Weir's father's pocket watch.]

RONON
Whoever it is…that's tough shoes to fill.

[Teyla wraps the fob and chain around the watch and gently places it in the box.]

TEYLA
(sighing)
Yes.

[Ronon places his hand on Teyla's shoulder. She reaches up and grabs it, trying not to break down.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[Carter and Lee hover over McKay, who's seated at a station.]

CARTER
Okay, let's give it a go.
MCKAY
Right.

[He dials the Stargate, which activates. Zelenka glances over upon hearing the kawhoosh and breaks out into a smile.]

CARTER
(into comm)
This is Atlantis calling Stargate Command. Do you read?

[There is no answer at first, and they exchange nervous looks.]

SGC TECHNICIAN
We read you, Atlantis. It's good to hear from you.

[McKay sighs in relief. Carter breaks into a grin.]

CARTER
Great to be heard! Tell General Landry we're back on the grid.
ZELENKA
(nervous)
Rodney…?
MCKAY
What?
ZELENKA
Long range scanners have detected a massive fleet of ships being launched from the Replicator planet…

[McKay gets up to join Zelenka.]

ZELENKA
But they're not heading for us.

[McKay studies the display.]

MCKAY
That's a Wraith planet. The attack command must have been activated. Whaddya know, it worked.

[Zelenka laughs in triumph.]

EXT—ATLANTIS BALCONY, NIGHT

[Sheppard stands looking at the two moons floating above him. Carter joins him.]

CARTER
There you are.
SHEPPARD
Two moons.
CARTER
Actually, there are five. It's just these are the only two visible to the naked eye.
SHEPPARD
Really?
CARTER
Yeah.
SHEPPARD
Huh. I heard you and Rodney got us reconnected to the intergalactic bridge.
CARTER
Yep. General Landry was understandably relieved to hear from us.
SHEPPARD
You know, in all the excitement, I never got a chance to congratulate you.

[Carter looks puzzled.]

SHEPPARD
Your promotion. You're a full bird colonel now.

[He playfully salutes her. She nods, looking embarrassed.]

CARTER
Ah. Thank you.
SHEPPARD
So, you sticking around?
CARTER
No. General Landry wants Doctor Lee and I to 'gate back, give him a full briefing on the situation here. The Apollo 's going to remain in orbit for the time being in case you need any assistance.

[Sheppard nods thoughtfully.]

SHEPPARD
Thanks for saving our asses back there from the bad guys. That was a risky move.
CARTER
It was nothing compared to the risk that you and your team took. Frankly, I'm amazed you were able to pull it off.
SHEPPARD
(shrugging, uncomfortably)
We had a lot of help.
CARTER
(sighing)
Yeah. General Landry sends his condolences. He was deeply saddened to hear about Doctor Weir…we all were.
SHEPPARD
I haven't given up hope. If there's a chance she's still alive, I'll find her.
CARTER
I know.

[They stand together staring out at the night sky.]

FADE OUT

END CREDITS
