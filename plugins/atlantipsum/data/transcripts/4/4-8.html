SGA 4.08 "The Seer" Episode Guide

Précis

Atlantis encounters the Vedeenans and tries to save their ill leader, Davos. Davos appears to see the future, and predicts the destruction of Atlantis. Carter must decide how to react to his prophesy when the Wraith Todd proposes a collaboration but then brings the enemy to Atlantis.

INT—CORRIDOR OUTSIDE BALCONY

[Teyla gasps at the vision. Davos heaves in some breaths and falls unconscious.]

INT—BRIEFING ROOM

[Woolsey paces around the room. Sheppard's team and Carter are seated around it.]

WOOLSEY
Teyla's vision only confirms what I've been saying all along.
CARTER
If we destroy the hive ship, we lose our best chance of stopping the Replicators.

[He stops and stands in front of her.]

WOOLSEY
We may not have a choice. One way or another, events are going to result in the destruction of that ship.
SHEPPARD
Well, if you believe that, then you believe Atlantis is going to be destroyed. We might as well pack up our bags and go home. How about this? We play along. We wait for them to transmit the program to Rodney's computer. Then, we blow 'em out of the sky.
CARTER
Betray them before they have a chance to betray us?
RONON
They're Wraith. Is anyone going to lose any sleep over this?
CARTER
Rodney, can you guarantee that you could finish the job without the Wraith's help?
MCKAY
(smugly)
If I have the original virus? Not a problem.
CARTER
(insistently)
Can you guarantee it?
MCKAY
(laughing nervously)
Well, I mean…no.
TEYLA
That is not the only problem. If we do not destroy the hive quickly enough, it may have time to send a message.

[Woolsey sits and starts to write.]

CARTER
All right, I think I need to meet this Wraith myself. Mister Woolsey…?

[Carter stands to leave, but waits for Woolsey.]

WOOLSEY
You-you want me to come with you?
CARTER
Well, I'm sure you want your report to the IOA to be as thorough as possible.
WOOLSEY
(reluctantly)
Of course.

[He follows her.]

INT—ATLANTIS CORRIDOR

WOOLSEY
I've never met a Wraith face-to-face before.
CARTER
Oh, well you're in for a treat.

[The door to the brig opens.]

WOOLSEY
Ladies first.

[Carter looks at him and chuckles then heads inside.]

INT—ATLANTIS BRIG

CARTER
Open it.

[The guards turn off the shield and open the door.]

WOOLSEY
Colonel!
CARTER
It's all right.

[She enters the cell, flanked by two guards. Woolsey reluctantly with her. The Wraith stands.]

CARTER
I'm Colonel Samantha Carter.
TODD
A pleasure to make your acquaintance.

[He looks expectantly at Woolsey.]

WOOLSEY
(nervously)
Richard Woolsey.
CARTER
I understand you're proposing that we work together on a joint venture.
TODD
That is correct.
CARTER
Last time we went down this road, things didn't exactly work out the way we'd hoped.
TODD
You were betrayed. Believe me, I understand your reluctance.
CARTER
If we do this, we do it our way. Your ship remains in orbit. They make the slightest false move, and they'll be destroyed.
TODD
Fair enough.
CARTER
The program will be downloaded into a non-networked computer in one of our labs. All work will be conducted on this base. You will remain under constant guard. And when you're done, we keep the virus.
TODD
It won't be easy to upload it into the Replicator network. Are you sure you're up to the task?
CARTER
We did it before, we'll do it again.

[Todd studies both her and Woolsey.]

TODD
I agree to your terms. You see, I want this as much as you do.
CARTER
Now, let's say that this works. What happens then?
TODD
In my time as a prisoner among the Genii, I lost my position among Wraith. I still have a few loyal followers but nothing like the standing I once had.
WOOLSEY
So you're going to use this to leverage your way back in?
TODD
In a manner of speaking.
CARTER
Sooner or later, we're going to end up on opposite sides.
TODD
Yes. But not today. Do we have an agreement?
CARTER
(after a pause)
We do.
TODD
I believe among your people, it is customary to shake hands.

[He extends his feeding hand. The guards raise their weapons, and Carter reacts slightly. The Wraith laughs.]

TODD
Just a little Wraith humor.
WOOLSEY
(whispering angrily to Carter)
I hope you know what you're doing!

[He leaves. Carter follows.]

EXT—SPACE

[A Wraith ship jumps out of hyperspace and heads to the planet.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[Chuck is at his station. Carter, Sheppard, and Woolsey stand nearby.]

CHUCK
A Wraith ship just jumped out of hyperspace.
CARTER
Are they powering weapons?

[Chuck checks his controls.]

CHUCK
(shaking his head)
Negative. They're sending a transmission.
CARTER
(into comm)
Rodney, you're on.

INT—MCKAY'S LAB

[McKay and Ronon are there. Several lines of code appear on the screen.]

RONON
That's it? That's their superweapon?
MCKAY
Well, what were you expecting?
RONON
A big gun? Something that goes boom?
MCKAY
(into comm)
Sam, that's it. We got it.

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

SHEPPARD
I can go down to the Chair Room. Finish this off right now.
CARTER
Can't risk it, John.
(into comm)
Security, have the Wraith escorted to Doctor McKay's lab.

[Woolsey takes a nervous breath.]

EXT—ATLANTIS, DAY

INT—MCKAY'S LAB

[The Wraith, shackled with restraints that attach to his waist, and a security escort have joined McKay there. The Wraith studies the code.]

TODD
(shaking his head)
No. This is all wrong.
MCKAY
What do you mean?
TODD
You must iterate all values of the expression before you can aggregate the results.
MCKAY
Are you kidding? We're better off using known data points and extrapolating.
TODD
You risk compounding errors.
MCKAY
What?!

[Todd leans close, indicating his restraints. McKay stiffens and the guards grow alert.]

TODD
(low, to McKay)
If my hands were free, I could help you.
MCKAY
No, it-it's fine. I'll do it myself, thanks.
TODD
As you wish.

[Todd turns back to the computer. McKay nervously goes back to his work.]

INT—CARTER'S OFFICE

[Carter and Woolsey are already there when McKay storms in.]

MCKAY
He's holding out on us! Part of the virus is missing. I mean, sure they're minor subroutines, nothing that would be greatly affected by the changes I made to the Replicator base code, but still…necessary to make it work! Look, I probably wouldn't have even noticed it, except…well, you know, I'm really good. Look, he's definitely up to something!
WOOLSEY
We know.
MCKAY
You know. What do you mean, you know?
CARTER
A few minutes ago, we started tracking another hive ship on course for Atlantis.

INT—MCKAY'S LAB

[Ronon holds his gun at the Wraith. McKay and Sheppard are also there.]

TODD
I swear I know nothing of this.
SHEPPARD
You gave our position away to another hive ship.
TODD
Why would I betray you now? Just as we were beginning to trust each other?
MCKAY
We're not really, are we? You held back some of the virus.

[The Wraith rears back and smiles slightly.]

TODD
Just a small portion. Nothing that prevents us from completing our work, and once that is done, I will gladly transmit the remainder.
RONON
I say we kill him right now.
SHEPPARD
I agree.
TODD
I had no way of knowing you would not simply…take the virus and then turn against me.
SHEPPARD
I'm offended you could even think that.

[The Wraith chuckles ruefully.]

RONON
The point is, you lied. How do we know you aren't lying about the ship?
TODD
Wraith are divided into many camps. There is much suspicion and mistrust. The last thing I need is for another hive to appear before I can complete this work!
MCKAY
(snapping his fingers)
That's it. That's why they're coming. They're tracking his ship. Think about it. A single hive, off by itself out in the middle of nowhere orbiting a supposedly uninhabited planet. That's bound to raise a few eyebrows. I mean…if you had eyebrows.
TODD
There is only one solution. You must cloak the city.

INT—CARTER'S OFFICE

[Sheppard, Woolsey, and Carter are there.]

WOOLSEY
We cloak the city, we lose shields. We'd be completely vulnerable to attack!
CARTER
And what if he's telling the truth? If we don't cloak, we'll be giving up our position.
WOOLSEY
He's been playing us from the start. This is what he wanted all along.
SHEPPARD
All right, wait a minute. A little while ago, you thought the Replicators were going to destroy the city. Now you think the Wraith are going to do it. Which one is it?
CARTER
(to Sheppard)
How long until the second hive gets here?
SHEPPARD
Two hours.

[Carter sets her jaw and nods.]

INT—ATLANTIS INFIRMARY

[Carter arrives at Davos's bedside. Linara is with him.]

CARTER
Hello again.
DAVOS
Colonel! It's nice to see you, but I'm afraid I can't offer you any more insight into future events. These medicines dull my mind.
CARTER
That's all right. That's not why I came. Although I did want to ask you a few questions about your visions, if you feel up to it.
DAVOS
Of course.
CARTER
Have you ever been wrong?
DAVOS
No.
CARTER
Knowing how events play out, you haven't been able to change the future or-or avoid it?
DAVOS
I'm afraid not.
CARTER
What about the culling on your planet?
DAVOS
I did not see a culling, merely the Wraith ships arriving, and that is exactly what happened. Doctor McKay's vision happened as he saw it, but his interpretation was incorrect.
CARTER
(with rueful smile)
Pretty hard to misinterpret what I saw.
DAVOS
I make no judgments, but I do know it will come to pass.
CARTER
It's hard for me to accept that. You're telling me that the future is predetermined, but I have always believed that the future is what you make it.
DAVOS
Perhaps both are true. Perhaps the future is predetermined by the character of those who shape it. One thing has been clear to me since the moment I set foot in this city. The galaxy is at a crossroads. Never before have I sensed that the future of so many worlds can turn on the actions of so few.

[Carter sighs nervously.]

EXT—ATLANTIS, NIGHT

EXT—BALCONY, NIGHT

[Sheppard joins Carter on the balcony.]

SHEPPARD
The other hive ship will be here soon.
CARTER
I know. You should get down to the Chair room.
SHEPPARD
So we've decided to take 'em out?
CARTER
Right now, I'm having a hard time deciding anything.
SHEPPARD
You know what the problem is? Too much information.
CARTER
(skeptically)
Really?
SHEPPARD
There's only so much information you need to make a decision. Anything after that complicates it. Like these visions, are they making things better or worse?
CARTER
Well, they helped you avoid being captured by the Wraith.
SHEPPARD
No, you and I both know we would never walk into a trap unprepared, even if McKay didn't have that vision.
CARTER
"The future is predetermined by the character of those who shape it."
SHEPPARD
What's that?
CARTER
Nothing.
SHEPPARD
Look, technically, Woolsey is correct. The safety of this city is our first priority, and based on that, there is only one decision. Take out both the hive ships as soon as possible.
CARTER
But…
SHEPPARD
But…what the hell's the point of being out here if we're not going to help people? We're going to be safe and sound while the rest of the galaxy gets slaughtered?
CARTER
So play it safe, or take a chance with the Wraith and maybe save millions of lives. Th-this kind of decision used to be easy, but then I was just making it for myself. Now, I'm asking a lot of people to take a chance with me.
SHEPPARD
Everybody on this base chose to be here, and they did so because they believe in the same things you do.
CARTER
If anything goes wrong…
SHEPPARD
I'll be ready.

[Carter nods. Sheppard leaves. Carter remains on the balcony, looking out at the city.]

INT—ATLANTIS CONTROL ROOM

[McKay and other personnel are at various stations. Woolsey stands observing. Carter enters.]

CARTER
Rodney, prepare to cloak the city.
MCKAY
All right, people, you heard her. Let's do this.

[Woolsey stands flabbergasted as the staff do their jobs. He moves over to Carter.]

WOOLSEY
My understanding is it takes some doing to switch back from the cloak to the shields. It's not something that can be done right away.
CARTER
That's right.
WOOLSEY
So if we commit to this course of action, there's no going back.
CARTER
I'm afraid not. There's still some time before the second hive gets here. You can return to Midway if you like.
WOOLSEY
But then my report wouldn't be as thorough as possible, would it?

INT—CHAIR ROOM

[Sheppard sits in the Ancient chair, activating it.]

SHEPPARD
(into comm)
I'm in position, Colonel.

INT—CONTROL ROOM

CARTER
(into comm)
Understood.
(to McKay)
Rodney, how we doin?
MCKAY
Engaging cloak…now.

EXT—ATLANTIS, NIGHT

[The city disappears.]

INT—INFIRMARY

[Davos's breathing becomes more labored. Linara notices.]

LINARA
Father? Father?! Doctor! Doctor, help him!

[Keller rushes over.]

KELLER
Davos? Davos!
LINARA
(crying)
Father?

[The life signs monitor flatlines.]

LINARA
No.
KELLER
I'm sorry.

[Linara takes his hand and sobs.]

EXT—SPACE ABOVE NEW LANTIA

[The other hive ship appears near the first one above the planet.]

INT—CONTROL ROOM

MCKAY
The second hive just jumped out of hyperspace. This is it…First hive is sending a transmission. They're probably trying to explain what they're doing here.

INT—CHAIR ROOM

SHEPPARD
C'mon guys, what's happening?
MCKAY
(over comm)
Stand by.

INT—CONTROL ROOM

CARTER
Rodney…?
MCKAY
They're still just talking. Whoa, whoa, whoa, whoa. Wait a minute. We got problem.
SHEPPARD
(over comm)
What is it? Wha-what's going on?
MCKAY
Both ships just powered weapons.
WOOLSEY
Oh my god.

INT—CHAIR ROOM

SHEPPARD
That's it. Firing drones.

[He reaches for the controls.]

CARTER
(over comm)
John, wait.

[Sheppard's hand hovers over the armrest.]

INT—CONTROL ROOM

CARTER
Just…give it a second.
WOOLSEY
Are you insane? They're going to wipe us out!
CARTER
Shut up, Woolsey.
WOOLSEY
(into comm)
Colonel Sheppard, as a ranking representative of the IOA, I'm assuming command of this base. I'm ordering you to launch the drones now!

INT—CHAIR ROOM

[Sheppard flexes his hands above the controls, uncertain.]

CARTER
(over comm)
Belay that order!

INT—CONTROL ROOM

CARTER
(to Woolsey)
One more word out of you, and I will have you removed!
MCKAY
Hold on, something's happening. The hives are firing on each other.
CARTER
Are you sure?
MCKAY
Definitely. They're not pulling any punches, either.

EXT—SPACE

[The two hive ships do battle. One ship is destroyed. The blast wave takes out the other ship.]

INT—CONTROL ROOM

MCKAY
(gasping)
They just destroyed each other. I guess the other Wraith didn't buy their story.
CARTER
Was either ship able to get off a subspace message?
MCKAY
Oh, right.

[He checks his computer.]

MCKAY
(relieved)
Negative. We-we're good!

[Carter smiles. Woolsey looks away, still unnerved.]

CARTER
(into comm)
John, you can stand down.

INT—CHAIR ROOM

[Sheppard deactivates the chair and sits up.]

SHEPPARD
Gladly.

EXT—ATLANTIS, DAY

INT—INFIRMARY

[Linara watches as they remove her father's corpse from the room. Teyla is nearby.]

LINARA
I'm taking him back to Vadeena. To be buried according to our custom.
TEYLA
It is good that he is going home.
LINARA
I don't know what my people will do without him. He's always been there, warning us of danger, protecting us from harm. Now for the first time in my life, I fear the future.

[She tears up. Teyla grasps her arm comfortingly.]

TEYLA
Just because something is unknown does not necessarily mean it needs to be feared. Trust yourself, and the rest will unfold as it is meant to.

[Linara nods and leaves. Keller comes over.]

KELLER
He was an interesting man.
TEYLA
Yes. He knew that I was pregnant.
KELLER
Really? You know, pretty soon, other people are going to find out, too. You're gonna have to tell them.
TEYLA
I know.

[She sighs.]

INT—BRIG

[Todd sits on a bench. Several guards surround him as Sheppard comes near. The Wraith starts to exit the room.]

TODD
So all it took was the destruction of my ship for you to finally trust me.
SHEPPARD
Oh, not true. I still don't trust you.
(to guards)
Take him to the lab. He's got work to do.

[The guards have restraints ready. The Wraith sighs as he allows himself to be led away.]

INT—ATLANTIS GATE ROOM

[Carter waits by the active wormhole. Woolsey walks up, rolling his case.]

WOOLSEY
Well…this is awkward.
CARTER
(nodding)
Yes.
WOOLSEY
For the record, I thought I was making the right call.
CARTER
It was a difficult situation…for everyone.
WOOLSEY
I just have one last question. How did you know the two Wraith ships would fire on each other?
CARTER
I didn't.

[Woolsey looks confused.]

CARTER
You going to put that in your report?
WOOLSEY
It probably would be better if I didn't. There may be such a thing as being…too thorough.
CARTER
Right.

[Woolsey leaves. The wormhole deactivates. Carter smiles, then begins to walk away. Sheppard arrives and meets her. They walk down the corridor together.]

SHEPPARD
Where's Woolsey?
CARTER
Oh, you just missed him.
SHEPPARD
That hurts my feelings. I didn't even say goodbye yet.
CARTER
How's McKay doing?
SHEPPARD
He and the Wraith are back at it, but, uh, I guess they're having a problem getting this virus to work.
CARTER
I'd hate to think this was all for nothing.
SHEPPARD
Well, we got out alive, and we kept our location secret. That still counts for something.
CARTER
Hmm.
SHEPPARD
Still thinking about those visions, huh?
CARTER
(sighing)
I can't get them out of my head. Everything Davos saw came true!
SHEPPARD
For all we know, then it won't come true for another thousand years!
CARTER
Or it could come true tomorrow!
SHEPPARD
Right. So, what else is new?

FADE OUT

END CREDITS
